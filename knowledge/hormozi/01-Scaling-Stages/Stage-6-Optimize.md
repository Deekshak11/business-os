# Stage 6 — Optimize

## Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Theme | **Optimize** |
| Headcount | **20–49** |
| Your role | **Leader** (not only manager of managers) |
| Leadership | **Two full layers** (e.g. director + manager) |
| Context | ~2.1M companies in this band |

## Primary constraint

**Everything is inefficient.** You have resources (two products, team, spend) but split attention and rising CAC. Graduate by doing things **better**, not only adding more volume.

## Function constraints & graduation

### Product
- **Constraint:** Improving two products slows both (split attention).
- **Graduate:** Install **incremental product improvement processes** for both products — product pipeline per product with **separate owners**.

### Marketing
- **Constraint:** Ads convert worse on **colder** traffic; CAC rises as you expand the market.
- **Graduate:** Implement an **ad assembly process** to increase creative volume/quality (see `Playbook-Goated-Ads.md` — Hook × Meat × CTA factory).

### Sales
- **Constraint:** Closing efficiency on core product **drops** and becomes inconsistent. Refunds may fall while closes also fall (safer but not growing).
- **Graduate:** Install **sales training systems**, individual coaching, and a **team cadence** for consistent closing efficiency.

### Customer service
- **Constraint:** **Churn rises** from volume + second product.
- **Graduate:** **Customer segmentation by cohort** and **activation points** — reduce churn per segment; engage/activate existing customers intentionally.

### IT / security
- **Constraint:** People join and leave with IP, data, and access risk.
- **Graduate:** Cybersecurity basics — malware protection, password practices, proper **onboarding/offboarding** access procedures.

### Recruiting
- **Constraint:** Roles exceed your network; can’t fill higher-level seats via friends.
- **Graduate:** Learn to **headhunt** higher roles; multi-step interview filters for different skills (e.g. head of finance, director of marketing).

### HR
- **Constraint:** Higher-level talent expects full packages (benefits, bonus, retirement).
- **Graduate:** Benefits, retirement, **advanced compensation plan** to attract the talent needed.

### Finance
- **Constraint:** Spending to grow but not seeing return; money “missing.”
- **Graduate:** Scale **AR/AP** discipline; more granular financial data / balance-sheet visibility; **pay taxes quarterly** and stay compliant.

## Bottom line

Grow by **optimization of what you have** — processes, creative volume, sales systems, segmented retention — before the next organizational leap.
