# Stage 8 — Specialize

## Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Theme | **Specialize** |
| Headcount | **100–249** |
| Your role | **President** |
| Leadership | **Four layers** including a full executive team (execs → directors → ≥2 management layers) |
| Context | ~900k companies (under 1M reach this band) |

## Primary constraint

**No one is good enough to know everything.** Create dedicated people/teams for specific tasks. Specialize product, brand, sales routing, CS journeys, tech, recruiting standards, HR, and finance roles.

## Function constraints & graduation

### Product
- **Constraint:** Too many features overwhelm customers — Frankenstein product accumulated over years.
- **Graduate:** Survey what customers **really value** → **prune** (improve through **deletion**) → pruned pieces can become **cross-sells / add-ons**.

### Marketing
- **Constraint:** Broader creative → lower quality leads; **brand diluted**; CAC rises again.
- **Graduate:** Grow brand with **standardized messaging**, **big free value**, and strong **positive brand associations**. Repeat core messaging that got forgotten; pour into the audience (give), not only extract.

### Sales
- **Constraint:** Closing efficiency on **premium** product drops.
- **Graduate:** Route **best leads** for premium to **best closers**; automated metrics by closer and by product to keep routing honest.

### Customer service
- **Constraint:** Multiple customer journeys **conflict** or are poorly timed.
- **Graduate:** **Proactive cross-sell recommendations** + a **blended customer journey** across products so interactions make sense together.

### IT
- **Constraint:** Department outgrows general software; too many internal inquiries; no inquiry process; no departmental policies.
- **Graduate:** **Specialized tech stacks per department**; internal ticketing/inquiry process; staff as needed; departmental processes and **SLAs** (how long recurring IT tasks take).

### Recruiting
- **Constraint:** Inconsistent recruiter standards → culture suffers; wrong candidates go too far.
- **Graduate:** **Standardize selection process**; **founder final fit check** for culture protection.

### HR
- **Constraint:** Volume of new/existing employees; performance suffers; compliance/complaints rise.
- **Graduate:** **Group onboarding** (often software-enabled); documented **performance management**; employee liability insurance; **reinforce cultural values** at every layer (same “potent messaging” problem as brand).

### Finance
- **Constraint:** Still paying retail; overcharged; cash unprotected; no returns on cash.
- **Graduate:** Renegotiate rates (you’re rare at this scale); specialized finance roles (e.g. tax); consistent **financial audits** (often external yearly) — risk is often **internal**.

## Bottom line

Specialize labor, product surface area, brand message, and systems. Dedicated ownership beats generalists-at-scale.
