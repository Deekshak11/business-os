# Stage 7 — Categorize

## Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Theme | **Categorize** |
| Headcount | **50–99** |
| Your role | **Executive** |
| Leadership | **Three layers** under you (directors, etc.) |
| Context | ~1.2M companies (~4% or less of the company set) |

## Primary constraint

**Every system is overwhelmed and the business is disorganized.**  
Graduate by **triage**: put things into buckets (leads, applicants, expenses, journeys) and run organized processes.

## Function constraints & graduation

### Product
- **Constraint:** Product gets outdated; improvements take too long.
- **Graduate:** Fix **one problem at a time** with **focused feature-specific teams** (prioritize with more people in that structure).

### Marketing
- **Constraint:** Lead flow **volatile** — typically only **one** acquisition channel.
- **Graduate:** Fund a **second acquisition channel** padded by:
  1. Much stronger **referral** process  
  2. **Segmented lead nurture by lead score** (score existed earlier; now market differently by segment via email)  
  Extra cash from referral + nurture funds the second channel. Pick the **most similar** platform first; use a deliberate channel-selection process.

### Sales
- **Constraint:** Leads get **wasted**; not consistently worked.
- **Graduate:** Better **schedule coverage** (throughput); **lead nurture checklist**; **kudos culture** for working leads (celebrate show-rate discipline as much as close rate).

### Customer service
- **Constraint:** Only **reactive** firefighting / chaos.
- **Graduate:** **Proactive customer journey** + communications matched to **milestones**; focus on **renewals and ascensions**.

### IT
- **Constraint:** Data, IP, and access **scattered** and disorganized.
- **Graduate:** Network/cloud solutions; data host; centralize hardware (on-site storage or remote cloud norms).

### Recruiting
- **Constraint:** Too slow to hire; candidates lost to other employers; too many open roles to track.
- **Graduate:** Install an **ATS** (applicant tracking = CRM for candidates); add **specialized recruiting firms** for hard skill sets.

### HR
- **Constraint:** Employee data in too many places; no single source of truth.
- **Graduate:** **HRIS** (human resource information system) for compliance and employee lifecycle data.

### Finance
- **Constraint:** Team spends money poorly now that founder isn’t the only spender.
- **Graduate:** Quarterly expense management; **budgets by department**; cards with limits; expense approval process; insurance coverage; better projections.

## Bottom line

**Organize and categorize** so the machine can scale past single-channel, single-bucket chaos.
