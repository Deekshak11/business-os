# Stage 2 — Advertise

## Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Theme | **Advertise** |
| Headcount | **1** (freelancers OK; freelancers ≠ FT employees) |
| Your role | **Doer** — in the dirt; not “above” any task |
| Leadership | Freelancers helping you; no real leadership layer |
| Still counts as | Within the ~30M company set (no FT employee threshold yet) |

## Primary constraint

**Inconsistent advertising → inconsistent lead flow → inconsistent revenue.**  
You must advertise **daily** at volume and stabilize set/close basics.

## Function constraints & graduation

### Product
- **Constraint:** Paying customers have higher expectations; product still unreliable.
- **Graduate:** Fix until good enough **in an unscalable way** (jump over the moon for customers if needed).

### Marketing
- **Constraint:** Lead flow inconsistent because *you* are inconsistent.
- **Graduate:** Live by the **Rule of 100** and prioritize advertising daily:
  - 100 calls / dials / day, **or**
  - 100 minutes on social / posting / day, **or**
  - 100 of *anything* that is real outreach/advertising activity daily  
  Goal: **consistent** advertise → consistent leads.

### Sales
- **Constraint:** Conversions inconsistent; you’re still learning to sell.
- **Graduate:** Nail a **script for setting and closing**. Use **CLOSER** framework (see `CLOSER-Sales-Framework.md` + `Playbook-Closing.md`).

### Customer success
- **Constraint:** Paid customers have higher standards; issues surface.
- **Graduate:** Fix fulfillment problems that create refunds/churn before scaling ads harder.

## Founder behaviors that win

- Volume of advertising over perfect creative (then improve).
- One channel executed hard beats five channels dabble.
- Personal selling + simple script.
- Unscalable product excellence while learning.

## Founder behaviors that fail

- “I’ll hire a marketer” before you can describe what works.
- Zero daily outreach/content/ads discipline.
- Scaling paid spend on a broken product/close rate.
