# Playbook — Branding

## When to use

- Competing only on features/price  
- Inconsistent look/message across channels  
- Need status association for premium offers  
- Scaling content/ads with no coherent identity  

## Core idea (Hormozi-aligned)

Brand is not logos for vanity. Brand is **what people expect and say about you** when you’re not in the room — status, trust, category ownership. It multiplies ad efficiency and price power when consistent.

## Operator principles

1. **Own a category sentence** — who you help + outcome.  
2. **Consistency** — same promise, proof style, visual/verbal cues.  
3. **Status alignment** — Value Equation dream outcome often = status; brand signals that status.  
4. **Proof-backed brand** — empty aesthetics without results reverse brand.  
5. **Stage fit** — early stages: brand via results + volume; later: systems and assets.

## Checklist

- [ ] One-sentence market position written  
- [ ] Visual + verbal guidelines simple enough to follow  
- [ ] Proof integrated into brand surfaces  
- [ ] Offers named (Offer Enhancers) consistently  
- [ ] Content/hooks reinforce the same identity
