# Rule of 100

## When to use

- Lead flow is spiky or near zero  
- Founder “posts when inspired”  
- Stage 2 graduation requirement  
- Need a non-negotiable daily standard  

## Rule

**Do 100 of a real advertising action every day.**

Examples from the Scaling Roadmap:

- 100 calls / dials per day  
- 100 minutes on social media outreach/engagement per day  
- 100 minutes of posting/content work per day  
- 100 of *any* consistent advertising unit that matches your Core Four choice  

## Why it works

- Removes “I advertised this week… sometimes” ambiguity  
- Forces skill reps (hooks, scripts, offers) through volume  
- Creates enough samples to see what works  
- Aligns with Stage 2 theme: **Advertise** consistently  

## Implementation

1. Pick **one** primary Core Four method for the 100.  
2. Define the unit clearly (dial, message, minute, post attempt).  
3. Track daily completion (binary: hit 100 or not).  
4. Review weekly: engaged leads, conversations, cash.  
5. Improve offer/hook/script **without** dropping volume.

## Anti-patterns

- Counting busywork that isn’t advertising  
- Switching methods every day to avoid discomfort  
- Doing 100 once then vanishing for a week
