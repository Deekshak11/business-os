# Advertising Core Four

## When to use

- “I need more leads” without a channel plan  
- Stage 2 daily advertising system  
- Deciding where to apply Rule of 100  
- Diagnosing why lead gen is stuck  

## Goal of advertising

Produce **engaged leads** (interest in what you sell), not vanity reach.

## The Core Four

There are only **four** ways to get leads:

| # | Method | Meaning | Cost profile |
|---|--------|---------|--------------|
| 1 | **Warm outreach** | Contact people who already know you | Time, low $ |
| 2 | **Cold outreach** | Contact strangers one-to-one | Time / list / tools |
| 3 | **Free content** | Broadcast value; audience comes to you | Time, low $ |
| 4 | **Paid ads** | Pay to put message in front of strangers | Cash |

You can later get **other people** (employees, agencies, affiliates, customers) to run these for you — still the same four.

## Operator guidance

1. **Start where you are richest**  
   - No money → warm + content + cold  
   - Money → can add paid  
   - Skills/time constraints change mix, not the list of four  

2. **One-page advertising plan**  
   - Which of the four this week  
   - Daily volume (Rule of 100)  
   - Lead magnet or core offer CTA  
   - Metric: engaged leads → sales conversations → cash  

3. **Don’t invent a fifth fantasy channel**  
   Podcasts, SEO, events, etc. still collapse into content, outreach, or paid.

4. **Lead magnet often multiplies Core Four**  
   Especially for higher ticket (`Lead-Magnet.md`).

## Channel selection by stage (heuristic)

| Stage | Bias |
|-------|------|
| 0–1 | Warm + free content + free trials |
| 2 | All four possible; volume daily on 1–2 methods |
| 3+ | Add nurture; still Core Four underneath |
| 5+ | Paid + content scale when LTV supports CAC |
