# Summary — Scaling Roadmap Stages 0–9

Full detail lives in `01-Scaling-Stages/`. This is the compact map.

| Stage | Theme | Headcount | Role | Primary move |
|-------|--------|-----------|------|--------------|
| 0 | Improvise | 0–1 | Researcher | Free thing people try |
| 1 | Monetize | 1 | Starter | First dollar; V1 sellable |
| 2 | Advertise | 1 + freelancers | Doer | Rule of 100; set/close script |
| 3 | Stabilize | 1–4 FT | Trainer | #1 complaint; nurture; qualify; onboard |
| 4 | Prioritize | 5–9 | Manager | Niche V2; qualification friction; KPIs/CRM |
| 5 | Productize | 10–19 | Director | Second product; attribution/CRO; sales aids; CS loop |
| 6 | Optimize | 20–49 | Leader | Better not more: pipelines, ad assembly, sales systems, cohorts |
| 7 | Categorize | 50–99 | Executive | Organize: 2nd channel, proactive CS, ATS/HRIS, budgets |
| 8 | Specialize | 100–249 | President | Prune product; brand; route leads; dedicated stacks/roles |
| 9 | Capitalize | 250–500 | Chairman | Big bet M&A/R&D; brand-first omni; capital readiness |

**Rule:** Organize by **headcount**, attack **one constraint** per stage. Stage 9 is the **last** stage in the roadmap.
