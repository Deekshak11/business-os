## **SECTION 6.1 — CHOOSING A PLATFORM (A Comparison of When to Use Each)**

---

**Email marketing platforms:** Multiple options (each with different strengths/weaknesses).

**The choice depends on:** Budget, list size, complexity of needs, and required integrations.

---

**Ladeira's recommendation for Brazil:**

"ActiveCampaign is the preference (70% of VTSD clients use it — robust automation, a Portuguese interface, Brazilian support, native integrations with Hotmart/Eduzz/Monetizze, acceptable pricing at R$150-900/month depending on contacts).

Klaviyo: If it's Shopify e-commerce (perfect integration, excellent revenue tracking).

Drip: If a beginner wants something visual (drag-and-drop workflows, a gentle learning curve).

**Avoid what's frequent:** Switching platforms (migration is painful, lists get corrupted, automations need to be rebuilt, sequences are lost — choose carefully at the start, commit for a minimum of 2-3 years)."
