## **SECTION 5.4 — LAUNCH SEQUENCES (WALKER'S PLF — 20-EMAIL, 4-PHASE SCRIPTS)**

---

**(A complete 12-14 day framework, 17-22 emails total, a template for each phase)**

---

**PHASE 1 — PRE-PRE-LAUNCH (Days 1-3, 3 Emails):**

---

**PPL Email #1:**

```
Subject: "I discovered something (about the scaling market — sharing it with you)"

Body (280w — already developed in the previous Section 2.5, keeping it):

[Opportunity reveal — 89% are stuck, why (an isolated system), the gap (missing integration), the next few days (education is coming), transparency (the pitch is only on day 10, upfront and honest), permission (reply YES to opt-in or ignore, okay)]

Open: 40-55%, Engagement: 10-20% (replies, clicks)
```

---

**PPL Emails #2-3:**

**(A similar structure — video content in each, a self-check diagnosis, educational value, community comments, building anticipation, still zero pitch)**

---

**PHASE 2 — PRE-LAUNCH (Days 4-9, 6-8 Emails):**

**(Each email: a deep-dive video on 1 pillar, extensive actionable education, case studies, proof, engagement with comments, still zero pitch, R$2-3k in equivalent value delivered for FREE, peak reciprocity)**

---

**PHASE 3 — LAUNCH (Days 10-12, 6-8 Emails):**

---

**Launch Email #1 (already developed previously — cart open, complete offer stack, 72h window, 27 spots, Sunday deadline, a decision framework, an informed choice)**

**Launch Emails #2-6:**

**(Condensed templates — each email in the 72h launch window has a different angle: #2 an additional case study of Pedro, #3 3 embedded video testimonials, #4 a 10-question FAQ is answered, #5 24h remaining urgency with quantified loss, #6 a final 6h FOMO with 8 spots remaining)**

**Cumulative launch conversion:** 25-40% (sequence recipients → purchase, staggered over days 10-12, some immediately, some think for 24-48h, some at the last minute).

---

**PHASE 4 — POST-LAUNCH (Days 13-14, 2 Emails):**

**(Email #1: extreme urgency 12h before the close, Email #2: sold out confirmation with info on the next cohort — enforcing real scarcity)**

---

**TOTAL PLF:** 17-22 emails, 12-14 days, 20-40% conversion (the highest method because of extreme reciprocity over 10-14 days + real scarcity + being informed).
