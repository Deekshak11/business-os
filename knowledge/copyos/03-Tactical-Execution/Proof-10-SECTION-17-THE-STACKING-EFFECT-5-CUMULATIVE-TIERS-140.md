## **SECTION 1.7 — THE STACKING EFFECT (5 CUMULATIVE TIERS = +140%)**

---

**Masterson/Agora** (decades of testing financial newsletters, info products):

> *"Each tier of proof = a boost in credibility.*
>
> *BUT: Stacked tiers = MULTIPLICATIVE (not additive).*
>
> *1 tier alone: Baseline conversion.*
>
> *5 stacked tiers: +140% conversion (vs. 1 tier — the cumulative effect is exponential)."*

---

### **TEST VALIDATION (Agora's Internal Data):**

**Promo A — Tier 1 ONLY (3 Testimonials):**

Conversion: 100% baseline (assumed).

---

**Promo B — Tiers 1+2 (Testimonials + Data):**

Conversion: 142% (+42% vs. A).

**It's not additive** (Tier 2 alone is typically +30%, combined it's +42% — an extra +12% synergy).

---

**Promo C — Tiers 1+2+3 (Testimonials + Data + Case Study):**

Conversion: 189% (+89% vs. A).

**The synergy continues** (Tier 3 alone is +25%, combined with 1+2 it's an incremental +47% — it's multiplicative).

---

**Promo D — Tiers 1+2+3+4 (+ Authority Credentials):**

Conversion: 221% (+121% vs. A).

---

**Promo E — ALL 5 TIERS (Testimonials + Data + Case Study + Authority + Extreme Guarantee):**

Conversion: **240%** (+140% vs. A).

---

**SAME offer, same copy, ONLY the proof was variable** (the count of tiers was different).

**1 tier → 5 tiers = 2.4x conversion** (a 140% improvement — from stacking proof alone).

---

**Why it's multiplicative (not additive):**

**Each tier:** Addresses a different type of skepticism.

- **Tier 1 (Social):** "Did it work for others?" (testimonials answer this)
- **Tier 2 (Statistical):** "Is it a pattern or just outliers?" (data shows the pattern)
- **Tier 3 (Demonstrative):** "Is this real or exaggerated?" (visuals verify)
- **Tier 4 (Authority):** "Who are YOU?" (credentials validate your qualifications)
- **Tier 5 (Guarantee):** "What if it doesn't work for ME?" (the guarantee protects them)

**Different objections** (each tier removes one) = **a cumulative removal** (5 tiers = 5 objections dissolved, resistance is comprehensively overcome).

---

**Schwartz:**

> *"Proof is NOT a single element (a testimonial alone, or data alone).*
>
> *Proof is a SYSTEM (multiple types, stacked, reinforcing).*
>
> *Each type: Addresses a different angle of skepticism.*
>
> *All types together: Skepticism is surrounded (there's no escape, the credibility is overwhelming)."*
