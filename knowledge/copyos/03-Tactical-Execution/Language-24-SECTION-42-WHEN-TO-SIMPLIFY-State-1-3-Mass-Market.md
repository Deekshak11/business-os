## **SECTION 4.2 — WHEN TO SIMPLIFY (State 1-3, Mass Market)**

---

**Simplify when:**

✅ **State 1-2** (they are already aware, want a quick transaction, not a lengthy education).

✅ **Mass market** (wide age range of 18-65, varied education, grade 8 is accessible to 80%+).

✅ **Simple products** (don't require complex explanation — a weight loss pill, a pack of templates, a simple service).

✅ **Short-form** (200w emails, 50w ads — limited space, every word must be simple and clear).

✅ **Mobile-dominant** (60%+ of traffic is mobile, fast scrolling, complexity = bounces).

---

**Simplicity techniques:**

**A. Short words:** Prefer 1-2 syllables (3+ occasionally, 4+ rarely).

"Use" not "utilize," "buy" not "purchase," "help" not "facilitate."

---

**B. Short sentences:** 8-15w on average (not 25w+).

Break up long ones: Periods replace commas (split ideas, create digestible chunks).

---

**C. Active voice:** "You earn $10k" (active, direct) not "$10k is earned" (passive, indirect).

---

**D. Conversational:** Write how you speak (contractions are OK — "you'll," "it's," an informal tone is appropriate for the mass market).

---

**Hemingway Grade 6-8:** Aim for this (paste your copy, the editor highlights grade 9+ sentences — simplify until the majority are 6-8).
