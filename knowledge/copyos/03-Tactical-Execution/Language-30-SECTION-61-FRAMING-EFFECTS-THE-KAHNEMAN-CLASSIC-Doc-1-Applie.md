## **SECTION 6.1 — FRAMING EFFECTS (THE KAHNEMAN CLASSIC — Doc 1 Applied)**

---

**Kahneman** (Thinking, Fast and Slow, Chapter on Framing):

The classic experiment (surgery):

**Frame A (Survival — Positive):**

"90% of patients survive this surgery."

**Chose the surgery:** 82%

---

**Frame B (Mortality — Negative):**

"10% of patients die from this surgery."

**Chose the surgery:** 54%

---

**THE SAME information** (90% survive = 10% die, statistically identical).

**A different frame** (positive survival vs. negative mortality).

**The decision:** 82% vs. 54% = **a 28 percentage point difference** (from the frame alone — the information was unchanged).

---

**Implication for copy:**

**The same offer, framed differently = dramatically different conversion.**

---

### **POSITIVE FRAMING (Benefits, Gains, Achievements):**

**When to use:**

✅ **Benefits section:** Listing what they GAIN (positive bullets — "achieve X," "gain Y," "become Z").

✅ **Transformation:** Before→After (focus on the positive AFTER — "revenue at $100k," "family time," "health restored").

✅ **Guarantees:** "Guaranteed success" (a positive framing of the outcome, not "you won't fail," which is negative).

✅ **A positive brand tone:** Some brands are optimistic (Brunson is enthusiastic, motivational — positivity is pervasive).

---

**Positive language:**

Gain, Conquer, Achieve, Obtain, Get, Receive, Benefit, Thrive, Grow, Develop, Improve, Advance, Rise, Elevate, Reach, Fulfill, Transform, Liberate

---

**Examples:**

1. "Gain $20k/month with copywriting (autonomy, geographic freedom, family time — a positively transformed life)."

2. "Achieve financial independence in 10 years (not 40 — decades saved, freedom early)."

3. "Conquer expertise (admired by peers, respected by clients, an authority — status ascending)."

---

### **NEGATIVE FRAMING (Losses, Mistakes, Warnings):**

**When to use:**

✅ **Urgency:** "Lose $1,000 after the deadline" (a loss frame = 2.5x stronger urgency).

✅ **Mistakes section:** "Avoid the mistakes that cost $50k" (protective, loss avoidance).

✅ **Agitation:** Problem escalation (the pain worsens, the consequences are severe — fear motivation).

✅ **Scarcity:** "Only 12 left" (the loss of opportunity is implicit — you'll miss out).

---

**Negative language:**

Lose, Waste, Cost, Fail, Err, Miss, Avoid, Prevent, Block, Destroy, Ruin, Sacrifice, Give up, Neglect, Ignore, Postpone, Hesitate, Procrastinate

---

**Examples:**

1. "Lose $50,000/year with this pricing error (undercharging $500 when it's worth $5k — leaving money on the table, irreversibly)."

2. "Every month of delay = $32k of opportunity GONE (the system generates that on average, you postpone, it's lost forever)."

3. "Avoid total burnout (your current trajectory: 65h today, 75h in a year, 85h in year 2 — a collapse is inevitable if you don't intervene)."

---

### **THE 60/40 BALANCE (Positive Majority, Strategic Negative):**

**Optimal copy analysis:**

A 1,000-word section:

**Positive framing:** 600 words (60% — benefits, gains, transformations, achievements, an optimistic tone).

**Negative framing:** 400 words (40% — losses to avoid, mistakes, warnings, urgency, consequences).

---

**Why 60/40 (not 50/50 or 80/20):**

**All positive (100%):** Weak (no urgency, no fear, insufficient motivation — "sounds nice, I'll buy later" = never).

**All negative (100%):** Depressing (doom, hopelessness, avoidance — the reader exits, they don't engage).

**50/50:** Neutral (balanced BUT not optimal — loss is 2.5x stronger, it needs a smaller % to create the same motivation, so 50% loss is overwhelming).

**60/40:** Tested as optimal (Ariely + Kahneman research — positive attracts, negative motivates, the balance = engagement + action).

---

**Example of a 60/40 balanced paragraph:**

```
"The 3-Pillar system transforms your business in 9-12 months (positive — transformation). [GAIN]

You'll scale from $30k → $100k+ (revenue growth, a positive outcome), you'll work 35h not 65h (time freedom, positive), you'll be present with your family (relationships restored, positive). [GAIN 60%]

BUT: Every month you postpone = $32,000 of potential LOST (opportunity cost, negative urgency), your competitors advance while you stand still (the gap widens, market share is lost, a negative consequence). [LOSS 40%]

Act now (positive command) — to avoid losing a wasted year (negative urgency). [BALANCE]"
```

**~100 words:** 60w are positive (transforms, scale to $100k, 35h, present with family — gains), 40w are negative (lose $32k, competitors advance, a wasted year — losses) = a 60/40 balance.

**Tone:** Hopeful (positive majority) + Urgent (negative minority motivates action).
