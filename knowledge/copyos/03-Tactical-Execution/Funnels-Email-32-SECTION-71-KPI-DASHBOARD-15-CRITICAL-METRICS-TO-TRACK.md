## **SECTION 7.1 — KPI DASHBOARD (15 CRITICAL METRICS TO TRACK)**

---

**Metrics = data for decisions** (don't guess what's working — measure, and optimize based on the numbers).

---

### **THE 15 ESSENTIAL KPIs:**

---

**KPI #1 — OPEN RATE (Primary Engagement):**

**Formula:** Opens ÷ Delivered × 100

**Benchmarks:**

- Indoctrination emails 1-3: 60-80% (peak interest)
- Nurture/Broadcast: 25-40% (ongoing)
- Promotional: 20-35% (some resistance to pitches)
- Re-engagement: 10-25% (cold list)

**Industry average:** 21% (Mailchimp 2024 — all industries).

**Target:** 30%+ (above average, strong engagement).

**Optimization:** Subject lines (Doc 6 formulas), send time (9-11 am in Brazil, per Ladeira), personalization (name, segmentation).

---

**KPI #2 — CLICK-THROUGH RATE (CTR — Action Engagement):**

**Formula:** Clicks ÷ Opens × 100 (OR Clicks ÷ Delivered — two methods, choose one and be consistent).

**Benchmarks:**

- Educational emails: 15-25% (high curiosity links)
- Promotional: 5-12% (CTA clicks for an offer)
- Broadcast: 8-15% (varied content)

**Industry:** 2.6% average (Mailchimp — clicks/delivered).

**Target:** 10%+ (clicks/delivered), OR 20%+ (clicks/opens).

**Optimization:** Clear CTAs (Doc 5 — a specific command, a green button, multiple CTAs in a long email), relevant links (not spamming links everywhere — 2-4 strategic links in an email).

---

**KPI #3 — CONVERSION RATE (Email → Sale):**

**Formula:** Purchases ÷ Email Recipients × 100

**Benchmarks:**

- Soap Opera: 8-18% (story selling)
- Nurture 90/10 pitch: 12-25% (earned trust)
- Launch sequence: 20-40% (extreme reciprocity)
- Single promotional: 1-5% (baseline)

**Target:** 8%+ for sequences (above the single email baseline).

**Optimization:** The offer (Doc 4 — 3:1+ ROI, Level 4-5 guarantee), proof (Doc 8 — 5 tiers), urgency (Doc 4 — real deadlines).

---

**KPI #4 — REVENUE PER EMAIL (RPE — Economic Efficiency):**

**Formula:** Total Revenue Generated ÷ Emails Sent

**Benchmarks:**

- Daily broadcast: R$0.50-2.00/email (1,000 subscribers, a pitch 1/10, 15% conversion, ticket R$300-2,000)
- Launch sequence: R$15-50/email (high because of the 20-40% conversion, tickets R$1-5k)
- Nurture: R$2-8/email (pitch emails 1/10, 12-18% conversion)

**Target:** R$1+ per email is sustainable (daily), R$10+ for high-value sequences.

**Optimization:** Increase LTV (upsells, ascension — each customer = more revenue), optimal frequency (a daily vs. 3x balance of revenue/tolerance).

---

**KPI #5 — LIST GROWTH RATE (Sustainability):**

**Formula:** (New Subscribers - Unsubscribes) ÷ Total List × 100 (monthly)

**Benchmarks:**

- Healthy: +5-10%/month (growth offsets unsubs, net positive)
- Stagnant: 0-2%/month (growth = unsubs, neither growing nor shrinking)
- Declining: Negative (unsubs > new — a problem, needs fixing)

**Target:** A minimum of +5%/month (growth compounds, the list doubles in 14-20 months).

**Optimization:** Lead magnets (compelling, multiply traffic sources), referrals (incentives for subscribers to refer), reduce unsubs (increase value, optimize frequency).

---

**KPI #6 — UNSUBSCRIBE RATE (List Health):**

**Formula:** Unsubscribes ÷ Delivered × 100 (per email), OR Monthly total ÷ List size (per month).

**Benchmarks:**

- Excellent: <0.5%/month (<1% per email)
- Good: 0.5-1%/month
- Acceptable: 1-2%/month
- A problem: >2%/month (insufficient value, excessive frequency, irrelevant content)

**Industry:** 0.1-0.5% per email (Mailchimp average).

**Target:** <1%/month (good list health, a balance of value/tolerance).

**Ladeira:** "An unsub rate of <1%/month in Brazil is excellent (audiences are tolerant if the value is high, and the 90/10 ratio is maintained)."

---

**KPI #7 — ENGAGEMENT SCORE (A Composite Metric):**

**Formula:** (Opens × 1) + (Clicks × 3) + (Replies × 5) + (Purchases × 10) = a score per subscriber

**Example:**

Subscriber Maria (last 30 days):
- Opens: 8 emails (8 × 1 = 8 pts)
- Clicks: 3 links (3 × 3 = 9 pts)
- Replies: 1 email (1 × 5 = 5 pts)
- Purchases: 0 (0 × 10 = 0 pts)

**Engagement Score: 22** (actively engaged, not yet a customer).

---

**Segmentation by score:**

- **Hot (30+):** Highly engaged (pitch emails work, upsells convert)
- **Warm (15-29):** Moderately engaged (education + soft pitches)
- **Cold (<15):** Low engagement (re-engage or remove)
