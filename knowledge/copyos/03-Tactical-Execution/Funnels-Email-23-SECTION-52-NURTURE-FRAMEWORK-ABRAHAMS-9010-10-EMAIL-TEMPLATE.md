### **NURTURE EMAILS 1-9 (90% — Pure Education, Zero Pitch):**

---

**EMAIL #1 (Education — Single Concept, Actionable):**

```
Subject: "The 4 U's headline formula (A Score of 30+ Guaranteed)"

Body (350w):

Hi [Name],

Today: Headlines (80% of copy's persuasion — Ogilvy's "80 cents of your dollar," critical to master).

THE PROBLEM: Most headlines are weak (a score of <20/40 points, 1-2% conversion, wasted effort on body copy that never gets read).

THE SOLUTION: The 4 U's Formula (Bob Bly — an objective scoring from 0-40 points, a threshold of 30+ = tested winners).

THE 4 U's:

1. USEFUL (A clear benefit — 0-10pts)
   Specific and quantified > vague
   Example: "Earn R$10-30k/month" (9pts) vs. "Earn money" (4pts)

2. URGENT (Timing is NOW — 0-10pts)
   Deadline/scarcity > timeless
   Example: "Expires today at 11:59 PM" (9pts) vs. "Always available" (0pts)

3. UNIQUE (Differentiated — 0-10pts)
   <1,000 Google results = unique (9pts), >100k = saturated (3pts)

4. ULTRA-SPECIFIC (Details — 0-10pts)
   5+ numbers (10pts), 3-4 (8pts), 1-2 (5pts), zero (2pts)

SCORING: Sum each U (0-40 total)

THRESHOLD: 30+ points = strong (test), <30 = refine

ACTIONABLE:

Worksheet (link below — score your headlines, identify the lowest U, fix that specific one, re-score until you get 30+).

[LINK: Free 4 U's Worksheet PDF]

Tomorrow: Curiosity bullets (80%+ engagement — a specific technique from page 47).

— [Name]

P.S. Score this email's headline? (The subject itself — practice: "The 4 U's headline formula (A Score of 30+ Guaranteed)" = U:8, U:6, U:7, U:8 = 29/40, almost at the threshold, refine the urgency → 31, a meta example).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Email: Taught the 4 U's (a complete framework), actionable (a worksheet to apply it yourself), standalone value (learn to score headlines for free), tease for tomorrow (bullets are next), P.S. is meta (scores the email's subject itself — teaching by example), zero pitch.**

---

**[EMAILS #2-9 have a similar structure — each teaches a different concept: #2 5-component Bullets, #3 5 tiers of Proof, #4 7 types of Urgency, #5 4-component Value Equation, #6 5 levels of Guarantees, #7 5 senses of Sensory language, #8 Loss vs. gain framing, #9 Voice consistency — each is 300-400w, an actionable worksheet/template, standalone value, zero pitch, a tease for tomorrow for continuity]**

**Total for emails 1-9:** Purely educational (9 concepts taught, R$2,000+ in equivalent consulting value for FREE, massive reciprocity established with Abraham's 10:1 — gave 10x before asking 1x).

---

**EMAIL #10 (10% — The Pitch, After Value Has Been Delivered):**

```
Subject: "Loved the 9 educational emails? (An optional offer for you)"

Body (450w):

9 days (daily emails at 09:30 AM BRT, concepts taught):
• 4 U's headline scoring
• 5-component bullet structure
• 5 tiers of proof stacking
• 7 types of urgency (real vs. fake)
• The Value Equation to maximize perceived value
• 5 levels of guarantees (the stronger paradoxically lowers refunds)
• Sensory language immersion
• Loss framing is 2.5x stronger than gain
• Voice consistency

Value delivered: R$2,500+ (a 15h consulting equivalent — each email is worth 1.5-2h of consulting, 9 × 1.7h avg = 15.3h × R$150-200/h rate = R$2,295-3,060 in value, I gave it for FREE, Abraham's 90/10 generosity, hoping for gratitude and reciprocity).

TODAY (email #10 of the sequence, as promised transparently on day 1): I'm presenting an offer (it's optional — critical to understand).

THE COMPLETE COPYWRITING SYSTEM:

[OFFER STACK — Doc 4 presentation]:

Core: 12-module course (R$3,500 value)
7 Bonuses: Templates, swipes, scripts, a calculator, a masterclass, a group, updates (R$6,000 total)
VALUE: R$9,500

Investment: R$2,997 (2.2:1 ROI, full payment OR 3×R$1,099)

Guarantee: Your first sale in 60d OR a R$2,997 refund + keep the R$6k bonuses + a R$500 penalty = R$9,497 total for you (zero risk, you gain even if you fail)

Spots: 100 max, 67 filled, REMAINING: 33

Deadline: Sunday 11:59 PM BRT (5 days — after: price is R$4,997, 2 bonuses are removed for R$1,200, wait for the March cohort in 4 months)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

BUT (CRITICAL): IT'S OPTIONAL

Emails 1-9: Yours to keep for FREE (apply it yourself DIY — the concepts taught are actionable, the template/worksheet links were given, it's possible to succeed alone without buying).

Some subscribers: Will DIY (they'll apply the free education and succeed — I genuinely CELEBRATE that, I'm proud the free value worked, no purchase needed, a happy outcome for me).

Others: Want the complete system (vs. DIY piece-meal — ALL the templates, not just the worksheets, copy reviews with specific feedback for you, accountability calls to get unstuck, a 12-week implementation together not alone, the guarantee protects them with zero risk).

BOTH are valid routes: It's your autonomous decision (total respect, zero judgment on which you choose).

Interested in the system? [5-min Application Link]

Not interested? (Perfectly okay — the next emails 11-19 will go back to 90/10 education, new concepts will continue to be taught, the next pitch will only be in email 20, not a barrage of pitches, I promise the ratio will always be maintained, the relationship is valued regardless of a purchase).

Gratitude: For your 9 days of attention (did you open each one? Read? Learn? — your engagement is deeply appreciated, I genuinely wish you success whichever route you choose — DIY for free OR the system, I want you to succeed with both outcomes).

— [Name]

P.S. The system's details are transparent: [Link for pricing, guarantee terms, spots, deadline, 12-question FAQ, 5 case studies, 8 video testimonials — MAXIMUM information, for a data-driven informed decision, not an emotional pressured impulse].

P.P.S. Questions? (Reply to this email — I'll answer in <24h, to clarify doubts, zero sales pressure, genuinely here to help your decision, here to support).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Email #10: Recaps the value of the 9 emails (acknowledges the generosity), a complete offer (stack, pricing, guarantee, urgency), OPTIONAL is emphasized multiple times (DIY is okay and celebrated, the system is an optional convenience, autonomous choice, zero pressure), not interested = the emails continue (reassurance of no pitch barrage), gratitude (the relationship is valued regardless), P.S. for maximum information (transparency links), P.P.S. for support (questions welcomed, genuine help), 12-20% conversion (recipients → purchase, high trust from the 9 emails of value).**
