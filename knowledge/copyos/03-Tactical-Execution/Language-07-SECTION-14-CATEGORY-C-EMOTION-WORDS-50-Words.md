## **SECTION 1.4 — CATEGORY C: EMOTION WORDS (50 Words)**

---

**Function:** Trigger feelings, not just thoughts (emotional System 1, bypasses the rational).

---

### **USAGE EXAMPLES (Power Words Integration Sentences):**

**Before (neutral, weak):**

"You can learn copywriting and make money with this course."

**Power words:** Zero (learn, make, course = neutral, no emotional triggers).

**Impact:** Low (boring, skippable, forgettable).

---

**After (power words integrated):**

"DISCOVER how to MASTER copywriting — GAIN $10-30k/month with a GUARANTEED and PROVEN system for 340 students (89% SUCCESS tracked, total PROTECTION with our guarantee, TRANSFORM your life NOW)."

**Power words:** 9 (discover, master, gain, guaranteed, proven, success, protection, transform, now) in a 29-word sentence = 31% density.

**Emotions triggered:**
- Curiosity (discover)
- Achievement (master)
- Gain (gain $10-30k)
- Safety (guaranteed, protection)
- Social proof (proven 340, success 89%)
- Transformation (transform your life)
- Urgency (now)

**Impact:** HIGH (multiple triggers, System 1 activated, strong emotional response, memorable, action-inducing).

---

### **FREQUENCY GUIDELINES (Don't Overload):**

**Optimal:** 5-15% of the words in your copy should be power words (balance — enough to trigger, not excessively spammy).

**Example:**

100-word paragraph: 5-15 power words (strategic placement).

1,000-word section: 50-150 power words distributed.

---

**Overuse warning:**

"NOW DISCOVER the SECRET INCREDIBLE method that TRANSFORMS EVERYTHING IMMEDIATELY — GUARANTEED FREE and EXCLUSIVE!!!"

**Every word capitalized/power** (overwhelming, spammy, zero credibility — brain: "too much, fake, ignore").

---

**Balanced:**

"Discover the system that transformed 47 companies (tracked for 23 months, with results verifiable by analytics). Guaranteed to double your scale OR you get a refund + $5k. Act now — this expires Friday."

**Power words:** 6 (discover, transformed, verifiable, guaranteed, now, expires) in a 32-word sentence = 19% (effective, not overwhelming).
