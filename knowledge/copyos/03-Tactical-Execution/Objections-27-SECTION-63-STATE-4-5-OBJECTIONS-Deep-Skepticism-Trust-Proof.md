## **SECTION 6.3 — STATE 4-5 OBJECTIONS (Deep Skepticism — Trust, Proof, Credibility)**

---

**State 4-5 (Problem-Aware/Unaware):** Deep skepticism (burned multiple times, cynical, zero trust), their objections are existential (does ANYTHING work? Can I trust ANYONE? Is it worth TRYING?).

---

**State 4-5 objections:**

1.  **"Nothing works"** (resigned, hopeless — the perception that they've tried everything)
2.  **"Everyone promises, nobody delivers"** (total cynicism, universal distrust)
3.  **"Why would you be any different?"** (their differentiation is questioned, a pattern of distrust)
4.  **"Prove it works BEFORE I pay"** (an impossible demand, a chicken-and-egg, a barrier)
5.  **"The risk is too high"** (extreme loss aversion, paralysis)

---

**Overcoming State 4-5 (EXTENSIVE Proof, DEEP Empathy, an EXTREME Guarantee):**

```
Q: "I've tried 5 systems, they ALL failed (R$18k wasted, zero results). Why should I trust YOU? Why would this one be any different when all the others weren't?"

A (400w — State 4-5 needs depth, empathy, and massive proof):

[PART 1 — DEEP ACKNOWLEDGEMENT, 80w]:

"I understand DEEPLY (not a superficial 'I understand' — DEEPLY).

You've tried 5 systems, invested R$18k, and have zero results = that's PAIN (the R$18k financial loss is painful, BUT what's worse is the Emotional toll — your hope was crushed 5x, the self-doubt ('maybe I'M the problem?'), the cynicism ('nothing works, it's all a scam'), the resignation ('I'll give up trying' — an extreme hopelessness).

Your distrust is TOTALLY valid (you've been burned 5x, a pattern of disappointment has been established, your defensive walls are up automatically — that's healthy protection, I completely respect it).

I'm not asking for blind trust (that would be absurd after 5 failures — I'm asking you to examine the different EVIDENCE, and to decide informed by data, not faith)."

[Profound empathy — it acknowledges the financial + emotional pain, it validates the distrust as healthy protection, it's not asking for blind trust — only an examination of the evidence, with deep respect]

[PART 2 — EXTENSIVE ADDRESSING, 250w]:

"Why we're different (it's not a 'trust me' claim — it's objective EVIDENCE):

DIFFERENCE #1 — A VERIFIABLE Track Record:
• 47 companies (not 5, not 10 — 47, a large sample)
• 89% succeeded (42 out of 47 — statistically significant, not a few anecdotes)
• The 11% of failures are SHOWN (transparently — we don't hide them, we're honest that 5 were refunded, and the reasons are analyzed, as you've already seen)
• Verification: 47 case studies (with analytics, invoices, timelines — you can download each PDF and check for yourself, it's not 'trust me' — it's VERIFY for yourself)

DIFFERENCE #2 — An EXTREME Guarantee (That Others Don't Offer):
• It's results-based (not satisfaction-based — a SPECIFIC RESULT of 2x OR your money back)
• A R$5,000 penalty (we PAY you if we fail — massive skin in the game, zero competitors offer a penalty, an extreme difference in confidence)
• A R$9,497 total back (you gain even if you fail — an asymmetric upside, the downside is a R$6.5k gain, we only offer it because we're confident the 89% track record covers it)

We checked 20 competitors (your 5 previous ones + 15 others in the market):
• A basic guarantee: 14 out of 20 (70% — a generic 30-60d money-back, zero skin in the game for them)
• A better-than-money-back guarantee: 4 out of 20 (20% — you keep the bonuses, moderate skin in the game)
• A results-based guarantee: 2 out of 20 (10% — a specific result, zero penalty)
• A penalty guarantee: 0 out of 20 (ZERO — no one offers to pay YOU if they fail, only us)

We are unique (literally — 0 out of 20 competitors) = an absolute differentiation.

DIFFERENCE #3 — Radical Transparency (Failures Are Shown, Limitations Are Admitted):
• The 11% of failures (are shown honestly, not hidden — others claim a suspicious 100%)
• The refunds (we've honored a total of R$37k paid to the 5 — proof the guarantee is real and enforced, not empty marketing)
• Our limitations (only digital businesses, not manufacturing — honest boundaries, we don't claim to be experts in everything)

Our competitors: They hide their failures (100% success claims — impossible, creates distrust), their guarantees are vague (unclear terms, questionable enforcement), they claim universal expertise (a red flag — nobody is an expert in everything).

Independent PROOF (not just my claims):
• Google Reviews: 4.8/5 stars (89 reviews from verified purchases — a third-party platform, we can't manipulate it, real customers)
• Video testimonials: 12 (on-camera, real faces/voices, authenticity verified — it's hard to fake 12 different people)
• Case studies: Public (you can download and verify the analytics/invoices yourself — total transparency)

[Objective evidence — a large, verified track record, a unique extreme guarantee, radical transparency vs. competitors who hide things, independent third-party proof, we're NOT asking for trust — we're asking you to VERIFY the evidence yourself, and decide based on the data]"

[PART 3 — REDIRECT, 70w]:

"Have you examined the evidence? (An 89% verified track record, a unique guarantee with a R$5k penalty, transparency about the failures, independent proof that you can check).

The difference is objectively validated (it's not a subjective claim — the DATA shows it, you can verify it, a comparison to competitors shows the uniqueness).

Still skeptical? (That's healthy — I offer a 30-min call, to examine the evidence deeper together, ALL your questions will be answered, with zero pressure on your decision, for maximum information, so you can verify each claim — examine it autonomously, and decide informed).

Are you ready? (You're protected by a total guarantee, the objective evidence has been examined, the difference has been proven with data — try it with zero-risk OR choose to remain skeptical, I respect your choice)."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

TOTAL: 400w (a deep 80w empathy that profoundly acknowledges the pain of 5 failures, an extensive 250w address with multiple layers of objective evidence from the track record/guarantee/transparency/proof, and a 70w redirect with an invitation to examine deeper OR to try it, protected — State 4-5 needs DEPTH to break through extreme cynicism, a brief answer is insufficient, comprehensive is what builds trust back slowly when it's been damaged).
```
