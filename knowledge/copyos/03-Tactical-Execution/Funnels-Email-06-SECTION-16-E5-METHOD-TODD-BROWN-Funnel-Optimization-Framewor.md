## **SECTION 1.6 — E5 METHOD (TODD BROWN — Funnel Optimization Framework)**

---

**Todd Brown** (E5 Method — systematic funnel optimization, 5 elements):

> *"Funnel converting low? (2-5% when it should be 15-25%)*
>
> *Don't rebuild randomly (guessing the problem).*
>
> *Use the E5 diagnostic (identify WHICH of the 5 elements is weak, and fix that specific one).*
>
> *All 5 elements are necessary (missing one = the funnel breaks)."*

---

### **THE 5 E's (Todd Brown's Framework):**

---

**E #1 — ENHANCE DESIRE (Amplify Want):**

**What:** Desire for the outcome (not the product — the outcome transformation, Doc 3 Drill levels 4-5).

**How to measure:** Content engagement (are emails being opened? Is the VSL being watched? — if low, desire is weak).

**Fix:** Amplify the dream outcome (paint a vivid sensory picture, stack benefits, emotional LF8, Doc 9 techniques).

---

**E #2 — ELEVATE AUTHORITY (Build Your Credibility):**

**What:** Trust in you (Doc 8 — credentials, proof, testimonials, guarantee).

**How to measure:** Is the "who are you?" objection frequent? (insufficient authority).

**Fix:** Use the 7-component authority stack (Doc 8 Part V), 5 tiers of proof (Doc 8 Part I).

---

**E #3 — ELIMINATE OBJECTIONS (Remove Barriers):**

**What:** Specific objections (price, time, will it work?, is it for me?, etc — Doc 11 develops this completely).

**How to measure:** Are FAQ questions repeating? (the same objections from multiple prospects = a pattern, not addressed in the funnel).

**Fix:** FAQ section (Doc 5 blueprints), guarantee (Doc 4 risk reversal), proof (Doc 8 to overcome skepticism).

---

**E #4 — ESTABLISH VALUE (Justify Investment):**

**What:** Perceived value > price (Doc 4 Value Equation, 3:1+ ROI).

**How to measure:** Is the "it's expensive" objection common? (value not communicated sufficiently).

**Fix:** Stack presentation (Brunson, Doc 4 Part VII), comparisons (your R$2,997 vs. an alternative R$10k coaching), ROI calculation (R$3k investment, R$100k+/year return = 33x).

---

**E #5 — ENCOURAGE ACTION (Create Urgency Now):**

**What:** Timing is NOW (Doc 4 Part VI — 7 types of urgency).

**How to measure:** Is "I'll think about it" frequent? (urgency is absent, leading to procrastination).

**Fix:** Real deadline (specific date/time), scarcity (limited spots), loss frame (you lose R$X by delaying), opportunity cost (every day without it = R$Y lost).

---

**E5 DIAGNOSTIC (Funnel Troubleshooting):**

```
Funnel only converts at 3% (goal is 15%+):

E5 CHECKLIST (Identify the Weak Element):

☐ E1 — Desire: Is engagement high? (opens 30%+, watch time 60%+)
  If NO: Enhance desire (amplify outcome, use vivid sensory details, stack benefits)

☐ E2 — Authority: Is trust established? ("who are you?" objection is rare)
  If NO: Elevate authority (add proof, stack credentials, more testimonials)

☐ E3 — Objections: Are they addressed? (FAQ covers top 5-10, strong guarantee)
  If NO: Eliminate objections (identify via surveys, address each in FAQ/guarantee)

☐ E4 — Value: Is the ROI clear? (3:1+ is shown, comparisons provide context)
  If NO: Establish value (use a stack presentation, add comparisons, calculate ROI transparently)

☐ E5 — Urgency: Is it present? (deadline, scarcity, loss frame)
  If NO: Encourage action (add 3-4 types of real urgency from Doc 4)

IDENTIFY the weakest E (lowest check): Fix that specific one (targeted optimization, don't rebuild everything randomly).

RE-TEST: Has conversion improved? (iterate with E5 until 15%+ is achieved).
```

**Todd Brown:**

"E5 is a diagnostic tool (it identifies WHERE the funnel breaks — no guessing, it's systematic).

Fixing 1 weak E (targeted): Conversion +20-80% (a single fix for a specific problem).

vs. Random rebuild: Conversion +5-15% (a shotgun approach, not targeted)."
