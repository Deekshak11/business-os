## **SECTION 7.2 — VOICE CONSISTENCY (Throughout Touchpoints)**

---

**Your voice MUST be consistent** (email, sales letter, ad, social media, webinar — ALL in the same voice).

**Inconsistency = confusing** (a casual email, a formal letter, a profane ad — personality shifts = distrust, "who IS this person?").

---

**Touchpoints consistency checklist:**

```
VOICE AUDIT (For Each Touchpoint):

☐ Email sequences: [Voice X — casual/formal? Emotional/rational?]
☐ Sales letters: [Same Voice X?]
☐ Ad creatives: [Same Voice X?]
☐ Social media: [Same Voice X?]
☐ Webinars: [Same Voice X?]
☐ Customer support: [Same Voice X?]

IF all are the same: ✅ Consistent (a unified brand personality, trust)

IF they vary: ❌ Inconsistent (feels like a personality disorder, confusing, fix it by aligning them all)
```

---

**Common mistakes:**

❌ **Casual email, formal letter:** A disconnect (is it the same person? Confusing).

❌ **Profane ad, polished website:** Jarring (an aggressive tone shift).

❌ **Vulnerable on social media, polished in sales:** Inauthentic (the perception of being fake — raw and real on social, polished and corporate in sales = which one is the real you?).

---

**The fix:** Choose your voice (Doc 3), apply it EVERYWHERE (all touchpoints with the same tone, vocabulary, rhythm — a unified personality).
