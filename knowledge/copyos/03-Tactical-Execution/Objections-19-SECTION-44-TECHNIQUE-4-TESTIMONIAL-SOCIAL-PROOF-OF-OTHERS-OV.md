## **SECTION 4.4 — TECHNIQUE #4: TESTIMONIAL (SOCIAL PROOF OF OTHERS OVERCOMING IT)**

---

**The objection:** "My situation is different (I have unique circumstances, it won't work for me specifically)."

---

**Overcoming it with a Matched Testimonial:**

```
"'My situation is different' — that's understandable (every business has unique aspects, the hesitation is valid).

OTHERS thought the same (their situations were also 'different'):

MARIA, 51 years old, a SINGLE MOM (she thought: 'I'm a single mom, 2 kids, zero time, it's impossible to manage a 12-week program' — her situation was indeed different):

The reality of how she managed: Her lunch breaks (30 min daily, she sacrificed the 60 min she had before → 30 min was enough, she found 2.5h/week). On weekends (the kids were with their dad on Saturdays, 2h free — she attended the calls). She completed it (10 out of 12 weeks — she missed 2 because she was sick, but it was enough for results).

The result: R$12k/month as a freelancer (part-time copywriting, the flexibility a single mom needs — her 'different' situation didn't stop her, she adapted and it worked).

Her quote: 'I thought it was impossible (as a busy single mom) — BUT my priorities shifted (my kids' future, my financial stability = their security), I found the time when I was committed. A different situation, the SAME transformation is possible.'

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

CARLOS, 29, a DESIGNER (he thought: 'I'm not a writer (I'm a visual designer, words aren't my strength), copywriting won't work for me' — a different situation skill-wise):

The reality: The templates (fill-in-the-blanks, not a blank page — his design skills were applied to the layouts, the copy templates provided the words, a powerful combination).

The result: R$25k/month (design + copy bundled — clients value the combo, premium pricing, differentiation).

His quote: 'I'm not a natural writer (I'm a designer) — BUT the templates worked (the fill-in-the-blanks removed the 'blank page' terror, design + copy = a unique offer that clients love). A different background, the SAME results (the skill combo was actually an advantage, not an impediment).'

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

JOÃO, 63 years old, a LATE CAREER SHIFT (he thought: 'I'm 63, I don't master technology, a digital business is impossible for someone old' — a different situation age-wise):

How he managed: The tech support (the team helped with the setups, the tools weren't a barrier, tech hand-holding was available).

The result: R$18k/month (online consulting, copywriting services — age was NOT a barrier, his 40 years of career experience = credibility, he learned the digital delivery).

His quote: 'I thought I was too old (tech is intimidating at 63) — BUT the support helped (every tech issue was resolved by the team), my age = wisdom (40 years of experience that clients value, not an impediment but an advantage). A different age, a transformation is possible.'

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE PATTERN (from 3 'different' situation testimonials):
• A busy single mom (she managed)
• A designer, not a writer (the templates worked)
• A tech-challenged 63-year-old (the support helped)

Each one thought: 'My situation is different, it won't work' (your objection might be similar).

Each one discovered: A different situation BUT the SAME transformation is possible (adaptations exist, support is available, the diversity of the 47 companies proves its universality — e-commerce, info products, agencies, SaaS, consulting, ages 29-65, solo/teams, tech-savvy/challenged — ALL succeeded with the pattern).

Is your situation different? (Probably — BUT there were 47 other different situations, and each one worked, yours is likely included; the diversity proves the system's adaptability)."
```

**500 words** — 3 testimonials (each with a "different situation" — a single mom, a designer, a 63-year-old, the diversity demonstrates), each one managed/succeeded (the adaptation is shown, it wasn't an impediment), the pattern (different situations, the same transformation — universality), the conclusion (yours is also different, BUT likely included, the diversity proves it).

---

**Effectiveness:** 9/10 (situation-specific social proof is powerful for identification, "if a person in a situation like mine succeeded, I can too").
