## **SECTION 6.2 — MULTIPLE PLACEMENTS OF THE GUARANTEE (7 Locations)**

---

**LOCATION #1 — IN THE HEADLINE (If It's a Unique Differentiator):**

If the guarantee is UNIQUE (your competitors don't offer a similar one), mention it in the headline.

**Example:**

"Your First Sale in 60 Days is GUARANTEED or You Get a Refund + $500 — The Copywriting System with 340 Students and an 89% Success Rate (The Only Course to Offer a Penalty Guarantee)"

**A guarantee in the headline:** It differentiates you (you're the only one offering it), it's proof (an 89% success rate = confidence), and it creates curiosity (why would they offer a penalty? They must be confident).

---

**LOCATION #2 — IN THE BULLETS (As a Proof Bullet):**

**Example of a bullet:**

"• An extreme guarantee: Your first sale in 60d OR we pay YOU a total of $2,497 (a $1,997 refund + a $500 penalty — confidence from our 89% track record, we'd only offer it if it works, our skin in the game proves it)"

**The bullet:** It's a proof element (not just an offer — the WHY of the offer is explained as a confidence signal).

---

**LOCATION #3 — IN THE PROOF SECTION (As Tier 5, After the Testimonials):**

```
[3-5 TESTIMONIALS]

[The DATA Track Record]

[A link to the CASE STUDY]

[THE GUARANTEE — as Tier 5 Proof]:

"THE ULTIMATE PROOF (Of Our Extreme Confidence):

The Guarantee: Achieve [a specific result] OR we pay YOU [a penalty].

The Terms: [The detailed conditional]

Why we offer it: 89% achieve it (tracked with 340 people, the data justifies our confidence — you only offer a penalty guarantee if your track record proves it works for the majority, and the math covers the 11% of failures + the penalty, with positive margins).

The inference: The strength of the guarantee = the strength of the product (it's a proxy — you'd only offer it if it REALLY works, otherwise you'd go bankrupt from refunds + penalties to everyone).

For you: You're totally protected (in the worst case you GAIN [an amount] if it fails, in the best case you transform with [an outcome], so your risk is zero, and there's an upside in both scenarios).

[The guarantee isn't just an offer, it's the ultimate PROOF of confidence]"
```

**150 words of guarantee proof** — it explains WHY it's offered (89% confidence), the inference the reader makes (its strength = it works), and the total protection (zero risk, you gain even if it fails).

---

**LOCATION #4 — IN THE OFFER SECTION (The Primary, Detailed Location):**

Doc 4, Part VII (the stack presentation) — the guarantee is after the pricing and before the urgency.

**The full terms:** The conditional, the specific result, the refund amount, the penalty, keeping the bonuses — it's comprehensive.

---

**LOCATION #5 — IN THE FAQ (For the "What If It Doesn't Work?" Objection):**

```
Q: "And what if it doesn't work for ME? (89% succeed, BUT 11% didn't — I could be in the 11%)"

A: "A valid concern (11% didn't achieve 2x).

The protection: The guarantee (you GAIN $7,997 even if it fails — a $2,997 refund + you keep the $5,800 in bonuses + a $5,000 penalty = your risk is zero, there's an upside even in the worst case).

The track record: 89% succeed (42 of 47 — IF you fully apply the conditional, under normal circumstances, there's a 98% likelihood based on the pattern).

[The guarantee = is the answer to the objection, it's proof + reassurance combined]"
```

---

**LOCATION #6 — IN THE CTA (For Pre-Action Reassurance):**

```
"Ready to start?

Your risk is zero (we guarantee to pay YOU $7,997 if you don't 2x — you're totally protected, with an upside in both scenarios).

Click the button below..."
```

**30 words** — a mention of the guarantee pre-CTA (for final reassurance, it removes the "what if I lose my money?" barrier — zero risk is stated).

---

**LOCATION #7 — IN THE P.S. (80% Read It, a Final Mention of the Proof):**

```
"P.S. The guarantee is serious (we pay YOU $7,997 if it fails — our 89% track record justifies our confidence in offering this risk, we'd only offer it if it works, and you're totally protected). Click now. [Link]"
```

**A 40-word P.S.** — it emphasizes the guarantee (it's serious, the track record, the protection), and it's a final nudge (the proof of credibility is reinforced).

---

**Total mentions of the guarantee:** 7 locations (in the headline IF it's a differentiator, in a bullet, in the proof section as Tier 5, detailed in the offer, in the FAQ, in the CTA, and in the P.S. — it's omnipresent, not just concentrated in the offer section).

**Impact:** Proof reinforcement (each mention = the confidence signal is repeated, credibility is accumulated, and skepticism is progressively dissolved).
