## **SECTION 3.2 — NURTURE SEQUENCE (ABRAHAM'S 90/10 RULE — AGORA'S PRACTICE)**

---

**Jay Abraham** (Getting Everything You Can — the 90/10 Rule):

> *"Give 90% (education, value, free help — extreme generosity).*
>
> *Ask 10% (a pitch, a sale, a favor — minimal requests).*
>
> *The common inverted ratio (marketers pitch 90%, educate 10% — it burns lists, massive unsubscribes).*
>
> *90/10 is sustainable (trust builds, the list grows, and WHEN the pitches come = they are accepted because of the 9x value previously received)."*

---

**Agora Financial** (decades of newsletters, billions in revenue, daily emails):

"Daily emails (365/year — extreme frequency).

The ratio: 330 emails of pure education (90%), 35 pitch emails (10% — ~1 pitch every 10 days).

Unsubscribe rate: 0.8%/month (extremely low — the industry typical is 2-5%/month, Agora is 4x better because of the value).

Sustained open rate: 28-35% (for years — not the typical decay, consistent value = maintained engagement).

Pitch conversion: 12-18% (when they pitch, it's high because of the trust established from the previous 9 value emails)."

---

### **NURTURE SEQUENCE STRUCTURE (10-Email Example — 90/10 Applied):**

---

**Emails 1-9 — PURE EDUCATION (90%, Zero Pitch):**

**Each email (250-400w):**

- **Subject:** Curiosity OR value (Doc 6 — "Why X?", "How to Y", "The secret of Z")
- **Body:** Actionable education (teach 1 concept, with examples, to apply on your own for free)
- **CTA:** Soft (an optional "learn more" link, not a pitch, no pressure)
- **Value:** Standalone (the email is useful on its own, it doesn't require a purchase to understand/implement)

---

**Example Nurture Email #3 (Education):**

```
Subject: "Why 'Features' Kill Sales (The Translation Fix)"

Body (300w):

"A mistake made by 67% of beginner copywriters (myself included in 2019 — burned through an R$8k project that flopped because it was feature-heavy):

Listing FEATURES: "The course has 12 modules, 40 hours of video, 50+ templates, email support" (boring, the client thinks: 'So what? What do I get out of it?').

BENEFITS translation (The Drill from Doc 3 — drill down):

Feature: "12 modules"
→ Functional benefit: "You learn copywriting completely"
→ Emotional benefit: "You sell without cold calls (avoiding rejection)"
→ Aspirational benefit: "R$20k/month, 6h/day, working from the beach"
→ Identity benefit: "An admired expert (family is proud)"

EVERY feature: Translate it through the 5 levels (drill down on each — so what? Until you reach an identity transformation).

Example translation:

"A 12-module course for complete copywriting (FEATURE)
→ You master 4 U's headlines, curiosity bullets, email structures (FUNCTIONAL)
→ Your copy gets READ not ignored, clients respond to 40-60% of your outreach, not 8% (EMOTIONAL — validation)
→ You close your first R$2-5k client in 45-60 days, potential for R$10-30k/month revenue in 12 months (ASPIRATIONAL — lifestyle)
→ Identity shift: From a regular employee → an expert copywriter (peers respect you, clients admire you, your family is proud — LF8 social approval, LF6 superiority) (IDENTITY)"

[Translation shown: Boring feature → compelling identity, a clear progression, each level is more desirable]

Actionable: List your product's features (10-20), and drill down on EACH one through the 5 levels (worksheet link below — practice the translation, master benefit communication).

Tomorrow: Bullet writing (80%+ curiosity — a specific technique from page 47, applied to the features you translated today).

— [Name]

P.S. The translation worksheet: [Link to a free PDF — no opt-in, generosity, apply it immediately]."

[Email: Education (feature→benefit translation is taught), actionable (a worksheet to apply it yourself), standalone value (learn even if you don't buy anything), tease for tomorrow (continuity of the sequence), P.S. a free resource (90/10 generosity)]
```

**Open rate:** 30-45% (educational sequence, value in each email, trust-building).

**Pitch:** ZERO (pure education, no selling, the 90% rule).

---

**[Emails 4-9 are similar — each teaches a different concept (bullets, headlines, proof, urgency, structures, etc), always actionable, standalone value, zero pitch, a tease for tomorrow for continuity, links to free resources — extreme generosity for 9 emails]**

---

**EMAIL #10 — THE PITCH (10%, After 9 Value Emails):**

```
Subject: "Loved the last 9 emails? (I have an offer for you — optional)"

Body (400w):

"9 days of education (daily emails, 9 concepts taught — feature→benefit translation, curiosity bullets, 4 U's headlines, tiers of proof, real urgency, structures, power language, rhythm, framing — each was actionable, FREE, yours to keep and apply).

Value delivered: R$2,000+ (in consulting equivalent of 10-15h — I gave generously, Abraham's 90/10 rule, hoping for gratitude and reciprocity).

TODAY (email #10, as promised transparently on day 1): I am presenting an offer.

[Offer stack — Core + bonuses, R$9,500 in value, R$2,997 price, guarantee, urgency, CTA]

BUT (critical): It's OPTIONAL (no pressure — emails 1-9 are yours to keep for free regardless, it's possible to apply them yourself DIY, the offer is for the convenience of a complete system + implementation together + support, not an absolute necessity).

Some: Will apply it for free (what was taught in the emails, and succeed alone — I celebrate that, I'm proud the free value worked).

Others: Want the system (all the templates, copy reviews, accountability calls, faster implementation, the guarantee protects them — the convenience + speed + support are worth R$2,997).

Both are valid: It's your autonomous decision (I totally respect it, zero judgment on the route you choose).

Interested in the offer? [LINK to Details]

Not interested? (That's okay — emails 11+ will go back to 90/10 education, the next pitch will only be in email 20+, no constant pitching, I promise the ratio will be maintained).

Gratitude: For your 9 days of attention (you opened, you read, you learned — your engagement is valued, I wish you success regardless of a purchase).

— [Name]

P.S. Offer details: [Link] (total transparency — pricing, guarantee, spots, deadline, FAQ, so you can decide informed, not pressured)."

[Email #10: Acknowledges the value of the 9 emails (recaps the generosity), the offer is presented (transparently as promised on day 10), OPTIONAL is emphasized (no pressure, autonomous choice), both routes are valid (DIY for free OR buy the system, respect for each), it's okay if they're not interested (the emails will continue 90/10, not a pitch barrage), gratitude (the relationship is valued regardless), P.S. link to details (maximum information, an informed decision)]
```

**Email #10 conversion:** 12-25% (recipients → purchase, HIGH because of the trust from the 9 emails, massive reciprocity, the pitch is rare at 10% = accepted when it comes).

---

**Emails 11-20 — BACK TO 90/10 (Education Resumes, Pitch in Email 20):**

The cycle repeats: 9 education emails (new concepts, continued value) → 1 pitch email (a different offer OR the same offer with a new angle) → repeat.

**Sustainable:** For years (Agora has run for decades, the list grows, engagement is maintained, unsubscribes are low at 0.8%/month, pitch conversion is consistent at 12-18% — a proven long-term methodology).
