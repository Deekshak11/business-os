## **SECTION 2.6 — GUSTATORY LANGUAGE (Savoring, Taste)**

---

**Gustatory = taste** (literal foods, OR metaphorical experiences).

---

**Gustatory Words (12):**

Savor, Taste, Flavor, Sweet, Bitter, Salty, Sour, Umami, Delicious, Tasty, Palate, Degust

---

**Literal usage (food products):**

"Savor: The keto chocolate (rich cocoa, the sweetness of stevia not sugar, a smooth creamy texture, a guilt-free indulgence — taste pleasure WITHOUT the carbs to sabotage your fat loss)."

---

**Metaphorical usage (abstract):**

"Savor the victory: Your first $5k project closed (the sweet taste of success, not the bitter taste of rejection from the previous 10 pitches, the taste of triumph finally — an emotional gustatory metaphor)."

"The bitter taste of failure (before), now the sweet taste of success (after — a taste contrast for the emotional journey)."
