## **SECTION 6.2 — STATE 3 OBJECTIONS (Comparison — Does a Better Alternative Exist?)**

---

**State 3 (Solution-Aware):** They are researching solutions, their objections are comparative (which is the best? Why is yours > others?).

---

**State 3 objections:**

1.  **"How are you different from your competitors?"** (the differentiation is unclear)
2.  **"Why not just DIY?"** (free alternatives, YouTube, books)
3.  **"Will cheaper options work?"** (are budget alternatives sufficient?)
4.  **"What makes yours better?"** (the USP is unclear, a comparison is needed)

---

**Overcoming State 3 (A Comparison Table, Clear Differentiation):**

```
Q: "I can learn on YouTube for free — why pay R$1,997?"

A (250w — a detailed comparison, education is appropriate for State 3):

"YouTube is a valid option (there is abundant free content, some do succeed with DIY — I celebrate that route).

An honest COMPARISON (YouTube vs. Our System):

┌──────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│Aspect│YouTube DIY│Our System│The Difference│
├──────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│Cost│R$0 (free)│R$1,997│+R$1,997 for you│
│Content│Scattered (100 channels, conflicting advice, 200h of watching to curate yourself)│Structured (12 sequential modules, a proven path, 40h total)│-160h saved (time = money, 160h × R$100/h in value = R$16k saved)│
│Implementation│Solo (alone, you Google when you're stuck, trial-and-error)│Together (calls, reviews, real-time help when you're stuck)│Completion: 8% DIY vs. 87% with us (a 10x difference)│
│Results│12-36 months (a steep learning curve, costly mistakes)│6-12 months (a shortcut, mistakes are avoided with guidance)│Time: 2-3x faster (an opportunity cost of R$84k/month × an 18m delay = R$1.5M saved)│
│Guarantee│None (free = zero protection, a risk of wasted time)│First sale in 60d OR R$2,497 back (your time + money are protected)│You absorb the risk vs. We absorb it│
│Support│Zero (unreliable community forums, strangers)│24/7 (email, calls, a group — dedicated)│Stuck = you get unstuck vs. stuck = you quit (the difference in completion)│
└──────────────────────────────────────────────┘

THE MATH:
• YouTube: R$0 upfront + R$16k in time wasted curating + R$1.5M in opportunity cost from the delay + the total risk is yours = a R$1.516M REAL cost (it's invisible BUT real).
• The System: R$1,997 upfront - R$16k in time saved - R$1.5M in delay avoided + the protection of the guarantee = -R$1.514M (a NEGATIVE cost — you SAVE R$1.5M by choosing the system vs. DIY).

YouTube seems 'free', but the System is actually cheaper (in a real cost comparison, when time + opportunity + risk are included).

Some succeed with DIY (8% — they are exceptional, talented, persistent, and lucky — I celebrate them).

The majority (92%) quit/fail with DIY (from the data on completion for courses/free content — being alone is hard, most need structure/support/accountability to succeed).

You choose: DIY (valid, free, 8% odds) OR the System (R$1,997, 87% odds, a guarantee protects you, 2-3x faster, with support) — an informed choice, both options are presented transparently."
```

**250 words** — a comparison table (visual, scannable, a direct side-by-side of the features), a real cost analysis (the hidden R$1.516M of YouTube vs. a net savings of R$1.514M with the system), the success rates (8% vs. 87% — the odds favor the system), the choice is presented (both are valid, informed, autonomous), it's honest (some succeed with DIY, the majority need support — transparency).
