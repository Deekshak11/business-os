## **SECTION 3.3 — BREAKING FALSE BELIEFS (VEHICLE, INTERNAL, EXTERNAL)**

---

**Brunson's 3 False Beliefs** (Doc 2 Part V — categories of obstacles):

---

**FALSE BELIEF #1 — THE VEHICLE (Skepticism about the Category/Product):**

**The belief:** "Copywriting doesn't work in Brazil" OR "The digital business market is too saturated to succeed" OR "Keto diets don't work long-term" (doubt about the category — the vehicle itself is questioned).

---

**An Epiphany Bridge for the Vehicle Example:**

```
BEFORE (You Believed the Falsehood):

"In 2017: I thought copywriting was an English-only skill (that Brazilians don't pay well for Portuguese copy, the market is small, it's impossible to earn R$10k+/month in Brazil — a firm belief, that the 'vehicle' of copywriting was broken in Brazil).

The evidence for the belief: I saw courses from foreigners (in the USA, $5-15k projects are common — in Brazil? R$500-800 was typical, 10-30x less, my perception was of an 'inferior' market).

The result of the belief: I didn't try copywriting seriously (I dabbled, I charged R$500, I got R$500 — a self-fulfilling prophecy, the belief was confirmed: 'See? I told you, Brazil doesn't pay' — a cycle)."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE DISCOVERY/EPIPHANY (The Experience That Changed the Belief):

"In February 2018: A client approached me (a small business in Curitiba, with R$8k/month in revenue from their online store).

The proposal: A sales letter (to rewrite a product page, to increase conversion).

My quote (conservative, expecting a rejection): R$2,500 (which was high for me then, I was nervous to pitch it).

The client's response: 'OK, when do we start?' (I was SHOCKED — they accepted R$2,500 instantly, with zero negotiation, a small R$8k/month business paid 31% of their monthly revenue for copywriting and didn't hesitate).

WHY did they pay?: The letter generated R$43,000 in revenue in 3 months (tracked with analytics — conversion went from 3.8% → 11.2%, with the same traffic, 2.9x the revenue, R$43k vs. R$15k in the previous 3m = +R$28k additional).

The client's ROI: A R$2,500 investment → a R$28,000 return = 11.2x (it was MASSIVELY worth it for him — he happily paid R$2,500, it wasn't charity, it was a smart investment).

THE EPIPHANY moment: 'Businesses pay for RESULTS (not language, not geography — if you deliver an ROI, Brazilian clients will pay WELL, just like in the USA — the copywriting vehicle works ANYWHERE that value is delivered, my belief that 'Brazil doesn't pay' was FALSE, a massive market exists, I was limiting myself, not the market).'

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

AFTER (The New Belief = Success):

"Today (in 2024, 6 years after the epiphany):

My revenue: R$60k/month on average from copywriting in Brazil (8-12 clients/year, R$5-15k for each project — the market is robust, it pays well).

My clients: 340 students taught (89% got their first sale in 60d — in the Brazilian market, the vehicle works replicably, it wasn't just a fluke for me).

The belief shift: From 'copywriting in Brazil is impossible' → 'copywriting in Brazil is HIGHLY viable, IF you use value-based pricing (delivering an ROI), NOT hourly as a commodity' (the vehicle works, the approach matters).

The proof: 340 have succeeded (a pattern that the vehicle is valid in Brazil, my false belief was what was limiting me, breaking the belief = unlocking the potential that was always there)."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE LESSON FOR THE READER (The Bridge is Complete):

"You might have a similar False Belief ('Brazil doesn't...' 'The market is saturated...' 'This category doesn't...' — your skepticism about the vehicle).

The belief is a mental limitation (not the reality of the market — I proved it, 340 proved it, the vehicle works IF you approach it correctly).

To break the belief: Try it (test the vehicle yourself, use value-based pricing not commodity pricing, deliver an ROI — the experience will shift your belief as it shifted mine, success follows the belief shift).

The vehicle works (it's been proven — the question isn't the vehicle, it's your approach; fix your approach = the vehicle will work)."

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE TOTAL EPIPHANY BRIDGE: 600w (before my false belief, the discovery moment that shifted it, the success I achieved after, the lesson for the reader — identification, the path is shown, the belief is broken via a story not an argument)

THE FUNCTION: To break the Vehicle False Belief (that copywriting works in Brazil) via a relatable personal story, not a logical argument (stories bypass the resistance that logic creates).
```

---

**Brunson:**

"Logical arguments: Strengthen resistance (the prospect defends their belief, the backfire effect — 'prove me wrong' = they dig their heels in deeper).

An Epiphany story: Bypasses resistance (you're not arguing, you're sharing an experience — for identification, 'he was like me and believed a falsehood, an experience showed him the truth, and he succeeded' — they want to replicate it, the belief shifts naturally, not by force)."
