## **SECTION 2.1 — LEAD GENERATION FUNNEL (Free Lead Magnet → Email Capture)**

---

**When to use:**

✅ **Build a list** (for cold traffic, not ready to buy immediately — capture the email, nurture later)

✅ **Low awareness** (State 3-5 — they need education before a pitch, a lead magnet = free educational value)

✅ **Long sales cycle** (B2B, high-ticket — slow decision, nurture required for weeks/months)

✅ **Authority building** (position yourself as an expert via free content, not a pushy vendor)

---

### **STRUCTURE (3 Linear Pages):**

---

**PAGE 1 — AD CREATIVE (Traffic Source):**

**Elements:**

- **Headline:** Curiosity OR benefit of the lead magnet (Doc 6 formulas)
- **Visual:** Image/video (relevant to the lead magnet, professional BUT not overproduced)
- **CTA:** "Download Free," "Register," "Get Access" (low barrier, not "buy")
- **Copy:** 50-150 words (tease the benefit, soft urgency like "limited time," CTA)

**Goal:** Click (CTR 1-5% for cold traffic, 5-15% for warm retargeting).

**Example:**

```
[IMAGE: Ebook cover "The Complete Guide to Copywriting"]

HEADLINE: "Download Free: 127-Page Copywriting Guide (50+ Templates, Used by 8,347 Copywriters)"

COPY (100w):
"Learn:
• Headlines with a 4 U's score of 30+ (3-5x conversion)
• Curiosity bullets with 80%+ engagement
• Email structures that close clients

FREE download (127p PDF, instant access).

8,347 have downloaded (★★★★★ 4.8/5, 340 reviews 'best free resource').

[BUTTON: DOWNLOAD FREE NOW]

Zero cost. 1-click unsub anytime."

[Ad copy: Clear benefit bullets, social proof of 8,347, 4.8/5 ratings, green CTA, reassurance of free/easy unsub]
```

---

**PAGE 2 — OPT-IN LANDING PAGE (Email Capture):**

**Elements:**

- **Headline:** Benefit of the lead magnet (15-30w — Doc 6)
- **Subhead:** Expands on the value (20-40w)
- **Bullets:** 8-12 (tease the lead magnet's content — Doc 7)
- **Form:** Email only (minimum friction — Doc 4, each field adds -10-15% conversion)
- **CTA Button:** "Download," "Get Instant Access" (specific action, ease emphasized)
- **Social proof:** Numbers (8,347 downloaded), ratings (4.8/5), micro-testimonials (brief quotes)
- **Privacy:** Reassurance (we never sell your email, zero spam, easy unsub — removes hesitation)

**Goal:** Opt-in (conversion of 20-50% from ad clickers → email subscribers).

---

**Expected conversion:** 30-45% (ad clickers → opt-ins, compelling lead magnet, simple email-only form).

---

**PAGE 3 — THANK YOU PAGE (Delivery + Optional Tripwire Offer):**

**Elements:**

- **Confirmation:** "Email sent! Check your inbox (in 60 seconds, subject '[Lead Magnet] — Download Inside')."
- **Delivery:** Immediate download link (don't wait for the email — instant gratification, some emails are delayed, a backup link helps)
- **Next steps:** "While it's downloading, could you take a quick 60-sec survey?" OR "Special offer (available for the next 10 minutes only)..."
- **Optional tripwire:** An R$47-97 offer (one-time, massive discount, related to the lead magnet — "Loved the free guide? Get the template pack for R$47 (normally R$297, 84% off today only)")

**Goal:** Deliver (satisfaction with the lead magnet) + optional Tripwire sale (partially recover CAC immediately, don't wait for sequences).

---

**Tripwire thank you page:**

```
[HEADLINE]:
"✅ Confirmed! Your Guide Has Been Sent (Check Your Inbox)"

[SUBHEAD]:
"While It Downloads... A Special Offer for the Next 10 Minutes Only"

[OFFER]:
Loved the Free Guide? (127 pages, 50+ templates — comprehensive)

Get the IMPLEMENTATION Pack (the next level):
• Fill-in-the-Blank Templates (100+ headlines, 200+ bullets, 50+ emails — copy/paste and adapt for yours)
• Swipe Files (tested winners, annotated to show why they work — learn the patterns)
• Video Training (3h of advanced content, normally R$497 — yours for just R$47 today)

TOTAL VALUE: R$1,294
TODAY (for 10 minutes): R$47 (96% off, a one-time offer, never again)

[COUNTDOWN: 09:47 remaining]

[BUTTON: YES, ADD FOR R$47 (84% Savings)]

[Small link: "No thanks, I just want the free guide" — allows them to decline, no pressure]

Guarantee: 30-day refund (zero risk, try it for 30 days, keep it even if you refund).

[Tripwire: R$47 offer, R$1,294 value stacked, 10-min countdown for urgency, green CTA, opt-out link (ethical), guarantee removes risk]
```

**Tripwire conversion:** 8-18% (thank you page visitors → R$47 buyers — perfect timing post-opt-in when emotion is high, irresistible offer of R$47 for R$1,294 in value).

**CAC recovery:** 8-18% × R$47 = R$3.76-8.46 per lead (if CAC is R$15/lead from ads, the tripwire recovers 25-56% immediately — a cash flow boost).

---

**COMPLETE LEAD GEN FUNNEL (3 Pages):**

```
Ad (2% CTR cold) 
→ Landing (35% Opt-in) 
→ Thank You (12% Tripwire)

1,000 ad impressions:
• 20 clicks (2% CTR)
• 7 opt-ins (35% on landing page)
• 0.84 tripwire sales (12% on thank you page × 7)

Results:
• Emails: 7 (R$15 CAC each if ad spend is R$105 — acceptable for lead gen)
• Revenue: R$39.48 (0.84 × R$47 — tripwire recovery)
• Net CAC: R$105 - R$39 = R$66 ÷ 7 emails = R$9.43/email actual (tripwire reduced CAC by 37%)

[Funnel economics: The tripwire significantly reduces CAC, making emails cheaper to acquire, and improving the profit margins of nurture sequences]
```
