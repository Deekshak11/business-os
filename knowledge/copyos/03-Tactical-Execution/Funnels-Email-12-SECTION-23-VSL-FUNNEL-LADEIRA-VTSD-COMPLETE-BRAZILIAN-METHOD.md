## **SECTION 2.3 — VSL FUNNEL (LADEIRA VTSD — COMPLETE BRAZILIAN METHODOLOGY)**

---

**Leandro Ladeira** (VTSD — Sell Every Single Day, a specific methodology for Brazil from 2018-2024):

> *"The VSL Funnel is the dominant structure for info products in Brazil from 2020-2024.*
>
> *Why VSL > Sales Letter in Brazil:*
>
> *• Mobile dominance (72% of traffic is mobile in Brazil vs. 58% in the US — video is mobile-friendly, long text is not).*
>
> *• Video consumption (Brazilians prefer to watch vs. read — TikTok, YouTube, Instagram are cultural).*
>
> *• Attention span (8-12 seconds on average in Brazil, video captures it better than a wall of text).*
>
> *• Trust in voice/face (seeing a person = higher credibility in Brazil — it's cultural, personal relationships are valued).*
>
> *The tested VTSD funnel: 2,400+ campaigns (for Empiricus, Hotmart, Eduzz products), average conversion of 4.7% from cold traffic (vs. 1.2% for sales letters with the same offers) — 3.9x better with a VSL."*

---

### **LADEIRA'S VSL FUNNEL STRUCTURE (4-STEP SPECIFIC TO BRAZIL):**

---

**STEP 1 — NATIVE AD (Taboola, Outbrain, Facebook — Cold Traffic):**

**Specific creative:**

- **Headline:** Extreme curiosity (Doc 6 Question, Secret, Discovery types)
- **Image:** Authority figure OR a shocking result (a doctor, an entrepreneur, before/after)
- **Platform:** Taboola/Outbrain (native widgets), Facebook feed (native style, not an obvious ad)

**Ladeira specific:** "Brazilian headlines = less hype than in the US (Brazilians are skeptical of 'clickbait' — they prefer factual curiosity, not the exaggerated 'SHOCKING!!!!' overused in the US)."

---

**Example native ad:**

```
[IMAGE: Doctor in his 60s, doctor's coat, stethoscope — visual authority]

HEADLINE: "67-Year-Old Doctor Never Gets Sick: 5 Morning Habits 89% Ignore (Harvard Validated with 12,000 People)"

[Curiosity — what are the 5 habits? Why do 89% ignore them? Harvard = credibility]

[CLICK → Advertorial]
```

**Expected CTR:** 1.5-3% (native cold traffic in Brazil — Ladeira's VTSD benchmarks).

---

**STEP 2 — ADVERTORIAL (800-1,200 Words — Bridge from Native Ad → VSL):**

**Ladeira:**

> *"The advertorial is critical in Brazil (don't skip straight from ad → VSL).*
>
> *Why: Cold traffic (State 4-5) → a 45-min VSL directly = 80%+ bounce rate (too big of an ask, no trust yet).*
>
> *The advertorial is a bridge (it educates for 3-5 min, builds credibility, warms the cold traffic → lukewarm, THEN the VSL is a reasonable ask).*
>
> *Without an advertorial: VSL completion is 15-25%.*
>
> *With an advertorial: VSL completion is 45-65% (2-3x higher — warming up works)."*

---

**Advertorial Structure** (Doc 5 Section 2.B.2 developed — recap applied here):

```
[DATELINE]: "São Paulo, Nov 19, 2024"
[BYLINE]: "By [Fictional Journalist's Name OR 'Editorial Staff']"

[EDITORIAL HEADLINE]: "Retired Doctor Reveals: 5 Simple Habits Prevent 90% of Illnesses (Harvard Study with 12,000 Participants Confirms)"

[LEAD — 200w]:
The story of Dr. Chen (a fictional composite), 67 years old, 40 years of practice at Johns Hopkins, never sick for 20 years (colleagues ask his secret).

[BODY — 400w]:
Habits 1-2 revealed (500ml of water upon waking, 15 min of sunlight — actionable and free, value delivered), Habits 3-5 are teased (the critical ones, the complete protocol is linked below — creates a gap).

[CTA — 100w]:
"Get the complete protocol for FREE (40p PDF, authorized by Dr. Chen, Harvard study referenced). [LINK → VSL page]. 12,000 have downloaded, transformations are documented."

[Total: 800w advertorial — editorial format (looks like an article from Estadão/UOL, not an obvious ad), real education (2 actionable free habits — reciprocity), a gap for 3 habits (curiosity drives the click to the VSL), CTA is a link to the VSL (a natural transition as a continuation of the education)]
```

**Advertorial → VSL conversion:** 30-50% (readers click the VSL link — they are warmed up, curious, and partially educated).

---

**STEP 3 — VSL PAGE (30-45 Min Video + Minimal Distractions):**

**Ladeira's VSL page specs:**

---

**Layout:**

```
[ABOVE THE FOLD — Visible Without Scrolling]:

[VIDEO PLAYER — 16:9 Ratio, Autoplay ON (Muted), Captions ON]
(Centered, large, 80% of screen width, the dominant visual element)

[HEADLINE Above Video — 20-40w]:
"Watch: Dr. Chen Reveals the Complete 5 Habits + The Exact Protocol (43 Minutes — Worth Your Full Attention)"

[SUBHEAD Below Video — Optional]:
"12,000 Have Applied It, 78% Have Not Been Sick for 18+ Months of Tracking"

[NO Menu, NO Navigation, NO Sidebar — ZERO Distractions]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

[BELOW THE FOLD — Scroll Down]:

[CTA BUTTON — Hidden Until Min 35 of the VSL, THEN Appears Sticky]:
"SECURE ACCESS TO THE COMPLETE PROTOCOL"
(Green, large, appears at min 35-38 of the VSL per Ladeira's timing, becomes sticky on scroll afterward)

[TESTIMONIALS — 3 Brief Text]:
Micro-quotes (30-50w each, reinforcing social proof during/after the VSL)

[FAQ — 5-8 Questions]:
Anticipated objections (price, does it work?, time?, is it for me? — Doc 11)

[GUARANTEE Box]:
Visually highlighted (blue, with a seal, clear terms — Doc 4 Level 4)

[FINAL CTA]:
Repeat button (green, "Secure Access Now," with an urgency reminder "27 spots left, expires Friday")
```

---

**Ladeira's critical timing:**

"The CTA button appears at MIN 35-38 of the VSL (not at min 0 — that's too early, value hasn't been delivered, resistance is high).

Why 35-38? (Tested with 2,400 VSLs):

• Min 0-34: Visible CTA = 2.1% conversion (premature, no value delivered, asking too soon).

• Min 35-38: CTA appears = 4.7% conversion (value delivered after 35 min of free education, reciprocity established, optimal timing for the ask).

• Min 40+: CTA is late = only 40% completion (many exit before 40 min, miss the CTA — too late).

**The sweet spot: Min 35-38** (enough value delivered, completion rate is still 60-70%, conversion is maximized)."

---

**VSL script structure** (Doc 5 Section 2.A.2 — minute-by-minute):

Min 00-02: Hook (pattern interrupt, promise, roadmap)  
Min 02-08: Diagnosis (problem identified, self-check)  
Min 08-25: Education (3 pillars taught, actionable, generous)  
Min 25-35: Proof (video testimonials, data, guarantee)  
Min 35-43: Offer (Brunson stack, price, surprise bonus, urgency)  
Min 43-45: Wrap-up (recap, final urgency, gratitude)

**Ladeira's emphasis:**

"Education is 78% of the VSL (min 0-35, massive free value), Pitch is 18% (min 35-43, after the value), Wrap-up is 4% (a respectful close).

The education:pitch ratio is HIGHER in Brazil (vs. the typical US 60/30/10 — Brazilian audiences want MORE free value before a pitch, it's a cultural expectation of generosity, an extreme application of Abraham's 90/10 rule)."

---

**STEP 4 — CHECKOUT PAGE (Final Conversion, Minimum Friction):**

**Elements:**

- **Headline:** Core benefit (remind them what they are buying)
- **Order form:** Minimum fields (name, pre-filled email, card, zip code — 5 fields max, each additional one adds -10% conversion)
- **Guarantee repeated:** Visual box (final reassurance, zero risk)
- **Urgency:** Countdown (if applicable — bonus for 30 min, deadline on Friday)
- **Trust badges:** SSL, cards accepted, security seals (visual credibility)
- **One-click upsell:** After purchase (as an order bump, not a separate page — zero friction)

---

**Ladeira's checkout optimization for Brazil:**

"Payment methods in Brazil:

• Credit card: 60% of conversions (installments are critical — 12x interest-free = +40% conversion vs. a single payment).

• Boleto: 25% (preferred by some — add the option, conversion increases +25% total by offering both).

• Pix: 15% (instant, growing — add it from 2021+, younger demographics prefer it).

**Offer ALL 3** (card + boleto + Pix — maximum accessibility, optimized conversion for each preference)."

---

**Complete VSL Funnel conversion:**

```
1,000 ad impressions (native on Taboola)
→ 25 clicks (2.5% CTR)
→ 12 from advertorial → VSL (48% warmed up)
→ 8 VSL completions (67% completion — Ladeira's benchmark)
→ 0.38 purchases (4.7% of VSL completers)

MATH:
• Cost: R$125 (Taboola R$5/click × 25 clicks)
• Revenue: R$758 (0.38 × R$1,997 offer)
• Profit: R$633
• ROI: 5.1:1 (R$758 ÷ R$125 spend)
• CAC: R$329/customer (R$125 ÷ 0.38)

LTV R$6,311 (from the previous Doc) ÷ CAC R$329 = 19.2:1 ratio (exceptional — massive scaling possible).
```
