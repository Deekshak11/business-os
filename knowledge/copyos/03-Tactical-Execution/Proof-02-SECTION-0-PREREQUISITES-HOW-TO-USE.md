# **SECTION 0 — PREREQUISITES & HOW TO USE**

---

### **Prerequisites:**

**→ REQUIREMENT: Doc 4 completed** (Offer engineered, guarantee defined — proof VALIDATES specific offer claims)

**Helpful:**
- Doc 2 (Avatar, State/Stage — to select the appropriate proof type for each)
- Doc 3 (Big Idea, Mechanism — proof supports these)
- Doc 1 (Laws — to understand WHY proof works psychologically)

---

### **Time Investment:**

**Reading:** 3-4 hours (proof frameworks, testimonials engineering, data presentation)

**Application:** 2-6 hours (depending on what you have):
- Already have testimonials: 1-2h (organize, edit, format)
- Need to collect them: 4-6h (client outreach, incentives, questioning, editing)
- Data already tracked: 1h (organize presentation)
- Case studies: 2-3h each (write, verify, format)

**Total:** 3-10 hours (to complete the proof section of your copy — an investment justified, as proof impacts conversion by 25-40%).

---

### **Expected Output:**

**A complete proof section** for a sales letter/VSL/promo:

- 3-5 testimonials (varied formats — 2-3 text, 1-2 video)
- Aggregated data (X clients, Y% success, Z timeframe, honest ranges)
- 1-2 detailed case studies (800-1,500w each, verified)
- Your authority profile (credentials with 7 components stacked)
- Integrated guarantee (multiple locations, visual boxes)
- Verification (links, third-party, transparency)

**Proof = 1,500-3,000 words** of a long-form copy (20-30% of a total 8-10k letter).

---

### **Connection to Other Docs:**

**Doc 8 (this one) = PROOF** (validates claims).

**Previous docs make claims:**
- Doc 3 (Big Idea promises a unique mechanism)
- Doc 4 (Offer promises a transformation)
- Doc 6 (Headlines promise benefits)
- Doc 7 (Bullets list benefits)

**Doc 8 PROVES the claims** (no more promises — validation).

**Subsequent docs:**
- Doc 9 (Language polishes the proof presentation)
- Doc 10 (Funnels deliver proof sequences)
- Doc 11 (Objections uses proof to overcome them)

**Proof = the bridge** (from claims → to credibility → to conversion).
