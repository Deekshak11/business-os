## **SECTION 1.2 — CATEGORY A: URGENCY WORDS (40 Words)**

---

**Function:** Create time pressure (act NOW, not later).

---

**Urgency Power Words (40):**

**Tier 1 — Extreme Urgency (10 words):**

1. **NOW** (maximum immediacy)
2. **TODAY** (deadline)
3. **IMMEDIATELY** (zero delay)
4. **INSTANTLY** (already/past due implication)
5. **URGENT** (explicit priority)
6. **LAST** (finality)
7. **EXPIRES** (deadline approaching)
8. **ENDS** (termination)
9. **LEFT** (scarcity quantity)
10. **NEVER AGAIN** (extreme finality)

---

**Tier 2 — High Urgency (15):**

11. Fast, 12. While, 13. Before, 14. Limit, 15. Deadline, 16. Final, 17. Rushing, 18. Hurry, 19. Close, 20. Only, 21. Just, 22. Exclusive, 23. Limited, 24. Vanishing, 25. Disappearing

---

**Tier 3 — Moderate Urgency (15):**

26. Soon, 27. Shortly, 28. Next, 29. Early, 30. Opportunity, 31. Window, 32. Moment, 33. Timing, 34. Lose, 35. Delay, 36. Hesitate, 37. Postpone, 38. Wait, 39. Procrastinate, 40. Act

---

**Usage example sentences:**

"Click NOW (ONLY 12 spots LEFT, expires TODAY at 11:59 PM — your LAST chance to get it)"

**Power words count:** 5 (now, only, expires, today, last) in a 15-word sentence = 33% density (creates high urgency).

---

**Optimal placement:**

✅ **Headlines:** "Last Chance," "Expires Today," "Only 12 Left"

✅ **CTA:** "Click NOW," "Get it INSTANTLY," "Act IMMEDIATELY"

✅ **P.S.:** "URGENT: Only 12 left, expires today, never this price again."

✅ **Bullets:** "Discover NOW before it's too late," "Only for the first 100"

---

**Frequency:** 1-2 urgency words per sentence in urgency-focused sections (CTAs, deadlines), 0 per sentence in educational sections (overuse feels spammy, dilutes power).
