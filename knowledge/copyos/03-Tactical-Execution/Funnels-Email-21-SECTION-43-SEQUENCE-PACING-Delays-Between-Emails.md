## **SECTION 4.3 — SEQUENCE PACING (Delays Between Emails)**

---

**Within a sequence:** How much time between emails? (immediate after 1h? Daily after 24h? 2-3 days?)

---

**Ladeira/AWAI guidelines:**

---

**INDOCTRINATION (Welcome Sequence):**

**Pacing:** Fast at the start (emails 1-3), a gradual slowdown (emails 4-7).

```
Email #1: Instant (0 min after opt-in — deliver the lead magnet immediately)
Email #2: +4-8h (same day, introduce yourself, a quick win)
Email #3: +24h (day 2 — social proof, testimonials)
Email #4: +48h (day 4 — set expectations, resources)
Email #5: +48h (day 6 — first soft mention of an offer)
Email #6-7: +72h each (days 9, 12 — community invite, transition to nurture/broadcast)
```

**Reasoning:** Fast at the start (momentum, engagement while interest is high), slowdown (to transition to a normal daily/3x rhythm).

---

**SOAP OPERA (Story Sequence):**

**Pacing:** Daily (exactly 24h — yesterday's cliffhanger, today's resolution, a new cliffhanger — the rhythm of a TV soap opera).

```
Email #1: Day 1
Email #2: Day 2 (+24h)
Email #3: Day 3 (+24h)
Email #4: Day 4 (+24h)
Email #5: Day 5 (+24h)
Email #6-7: Days 6-7 (optional)
```

**Reasoning:** Cliffhangers must be resolved quickly (waiting 48-72h = frustration, interest cools — daily = peak engagement is maintained).

**Chaperon:** "The Soap Opera has a daily rhythm (it's non-negotiable — story addiction requires consistency, breaks of 48h+ = momentum is lost, engagement drops 40%)."

---

**NURTURE (Educational Sequence):**

**Pacing:** 3x/week (Tuesday 09:30, Thursday 09:30, Saturday 10:00 AM BRT).

```
Email #1: Tuesday
Email #2: Thursday (+48h)
Email #3: Saturday (+48h)
Email #4: Tuesday (+72h)
[Continue the pattern — 48-72h delays, a consistent rhythm]
```

**Reasoning:** Balance (frequent enough for momentum, spaced enough not to be overwhelming, higher quality for each of the 156/year emails vs. 365 daily).

---

**LAUNCH (PLF Sequence):**

**Pacing:** Variable (different phases, different rhythms).

```
Pre-Pre-Launch (days 1-3): Daily (building awareness quickly)
Pre-Launch (days 4-9): Every 2 days (deep education, breathing room to absorb)
Launch (days 10-12): MULTIPLE times a day (urgency — morning, afternoon, and evening emails in the 72h window, aggressive but acceptable because of the value from the previous 10 days + real scarcity)
Post-Launch (days 13-14): Final emails (12h before, and immediately after the close)
```

**Reasoning:** Different phases have different purposes (education = slower pacing to absorb, urgency = faster pacing for pressure).

---

**RE-ENGAGEMENT (Win-Back):**

**Pacing:** Spaced out (don't rush — inactives are disengaged, bombarding them makes it worse).

```
Email #1: Day 1 (first contact after 90+ days of inactivity)
Email #2: +5-7 days (breathing room, no pressure)
Email #3: +7 days (a survey/incentive)
Email #4: +10-14 days (a final offer OR removal)
```

**Reasoning:** Inactives are delicate (bombarding them every 24h is annoying, spacing them 5-7 days apart is respectful, better chances of re-engagement).
