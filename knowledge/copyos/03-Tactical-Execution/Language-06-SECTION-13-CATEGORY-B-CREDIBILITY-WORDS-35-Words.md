## **SECTION 1.3 — CATEGORY B: CREDIBILITY WORDS (35 Words)**

---

**Function:** Build trust, authority, proof (boosts believability).

---

**Credibility Words (35):**

1. **PROVEN** (testing implied)
2. **GUARANTEED** (certainty)
3. **VERIFIED** (third-party checked)
4. **TESTED** (validation)
5. **VALIDATED** (confirmation)
6. **CERTIFIED** (authority)
7. **DOCUMENTED** (records exist)
8. **TRACKED** (data collected)
9. **SCIENTIFIC** (research-backed)
10. **RESEARCH** (study-based)

11-20: Study, Data, Facts, Evidence, Authentic, Genuine, Real, True, Honest, Transparent

21-30: Official, Legitimate, Authorized, Approved, Endorsed, Recognized, Award-Winning, Leader, Specialist, Expert

31-35: Professional, Reliable, Respected, Established, Demonstrably

---

**Usage:**

"A PROVEN system for 47 companies (TESTED for 23 months, with results TRACKED by VERIFIED analytics and data DOCUMENTED in case studies — total TRANSPARENCY)"

**Credibility words:** 6 in one sentence (proven, tested, tracked, verified, documented, transparency) = multiple trust signals, overwhelming credibility.

---

**Placement:**

✅ **Proof section:** Heavy concentration (verified, tested, tracked)

✅ **Headlines:** "Proven Method," "Tested System," "Guaranteed to Work"

✅ **Bullets:** "Technique validated by Johns Hopkins," "Data tracked from 12k users"
