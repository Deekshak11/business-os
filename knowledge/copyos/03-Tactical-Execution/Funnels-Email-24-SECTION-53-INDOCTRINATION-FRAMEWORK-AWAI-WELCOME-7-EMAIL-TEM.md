## **SECTION 5.3 — INDOCTRINATION FRAMEWORK (AWAI WELCOME — 7-EMAIL TEMPLATE)**

---

**AWAI** (American Writers & Artists Institute — an indoctrination sequence methodology, tested on 1,000s of students):

> *"The first 7 days after an opt-in are CRITICAL (they decide to stay engaged OR disengage).*
>
> *The goal of indoctrination: Solidify permission (the opt-in was tentative, a 1-click unsubscribe is easy if they get annoyed).*
>
> *Deliver value IMMEDIATELY (the promised lead magnet), set expectations (what to expect from the emails), build a relationship (a warm introduction), provide quick wins (actionable results fast).*
>
> *Done well: 70-85% engagement (ongoing opens, a relationship is established).*
>
> *Done poorly: 40-60% disengagement (immediate unsubs, never opening after emails 2-3)."*

---

### **INDOCTRINATION TEMPLATE (7 Emails, First Week):**

---

**EMAIL #1 — DELIVER THE LEAD MAGNET + SET EXPECTATIONS (Instant, Automated):**

```
Subject: "✅ Your Copywriting Guide (Download + What's Next)"

Body (250w):

Hi [Name]!

THANKS for registering! (Excited to have you — part of a growing community of 8,347 copywriters).

THE COPYWRITING GUIDE (127 pages, 50+ templates):

[LARGE BUTTON: DOWNLOAD THE GUIDE NOW]

OR: Check your email inbox (subject "Your Copywriting Guide — Download Inside" — sent 60 seconds ago, some emails are delayed, the button above is a backup).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT'S NEXT (Over the Next 7 Days — Expectations):

Daily emails (at 09:30 AM BRT — consistent timing):

Tomorrow (email #2): Introduction (who I am, why I teach copywriting, a brief on my credibility)

Day 3: A quick win (a headline technique to apply immediately, for results today)

Day 4: Social proof (transformations of others — you can too)

Day 5: Resources (a library of templates, free tools to access)

Day 6: Community (a group of 800+ copywriters, networking, jobs posted)

Day 7: Next steps (options — continue with free DIY OR a complete system if you want it)

VALUE: Each email is actionable (learn, apply, get results — not passive theory).

PROMISE: Zero spam (only valuable education, RARE pitches — approximately 1 for every 10 emails, Abraham's 90% education rule).

UNSUBSCRIBE: Anytime with 1-click (at the bottom of each email — an easy exit, it's always your permission, zero hard feelings if you decide it's not a fit).

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

QUICK START (While the Guide Downloads):

Page 23: Headline Formula #1 (How-To — use it in the next 5 min, write 3 headlines of your own).

Action: Try it now (5 min, an immediate result, start with momentum).

See you tomorrow at 09:30! — [Name]

P.S. Questions already? (Reply to this email — a response in <24h, here to help, a genuine welcome aboard).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Email #1: Immediate delivery (the promised lead magnet, satisfaction), expectations set transparently (what to expect for 7 days, the 09:30 timing, daily frequency, value in each, the 90/10 pitch ratio, easy unsubscribe — total honesty removes surprises), a quick start action (page 23 for an immediate action, momentum), a warm welcome (excited, gratitude, a mention of the community), P.S. for support (questions welcomed, accessible).**

**Open rate #1:** 60-80% (the highest of the sequence — they just opted in, peak interest, awaiting confirmation).

```
Subject: "Who I am (+ Why I teach copywriting)"

Body (300w):

Hi [Name],

Yesterday: The guide was sent (did you download it? 127p is substantial, worth the time).

Today: I'll introduce myself (quickly — so you know who's teaching, to establish credibility, and relatability).

[YOUR NAME]:

Background: 14 years in copywriting (started in 2010, was broke in a $28k/year job, discovered copywriting and it transformed my life — a relatable journey, maybe like yours).

Experience:
• 2,400+ sales letters written (over 26 years — volume)
• R$340M in revenue attributed (from tracked client campaigns — results)
• 340 students taught (2020-2024, 89% got their first sale in 60d — a track record)

NOT (honesty): A PhD academic, a native English speaker, a perfect guru (I failed 2 businesses early on, I'm constantly learning Portuguese, I have human flaws — a relatable real person, not a polished, distant expert).

WHY do I teach? (A genuine motivation):

Copywriting changed my life (from a R$28k job → R$60k/month as a freelancer, an 8-year transformation — I'm grateful for the skill, I want to pay it forward).

I saw a gap: Courses teach THEORY (features, structures, techniques — knowledge), NOT the business (how to GET clients, CLOSE projects, PRICE based on value — an implementation gap in 90% of courses).

My mission: To fill that gap (to teach implementation, not theory alone — doing it together, with accountability, and guaranteed results).

Enough about me (the emails are YOU-focused, not me-focused — the purpose was a brief intro, to establish credibility, tomorrow we're back to teaching).

Tomorrow: A quick win (write a headline in 10 min, a specific technique, an immediate result — your action).

— [Name]

P.S. Can you relate to the journey? (Reply and share yours — did you struggle at the beginning? Are you stuck now? Do you want a transformation? — for engagement, community, I personally read each reply).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Email #2: A warm introduction (a brief background, not a lengthy CV, quantified experience, a track record, honesty about flaws — a relatable human), why I teach (a genuine altruistic motivation, a mission to fill a gap — a clear purpose), you-focused (enough about me, the emails are about YOU, teaching resumes tomorrow), P.S. an invitation to share (personal engagement, community building).**

**Open rate #2:** 50-70% (interest in you is maintained, curiosity about who is teaching).

---

**EMAIL #3 — A QUICK WIN (Immediately Actionable, a Result Today):**

```
Subject: "A quick win: Write a headline in 10 min (A result today)"

Body (350w):

Hi [Name],

TODAY: A quick win (a technique to apply in the next 10 minutes, write a headline, get an immediate result — momentum).

THE TECHNIQUE: Number Headlines (a tested formula, high conversion, easy to execute).

THE TEMPLATE:
"[Odd Number] Ways/Secrets/Steps to [Specific Benefit] ([Proof element])"

EXAMPLE:
"7 Ways to Get Your First Copywriting Client in 60 Days (Even with Zero Portfolio — Outreach Templates Included)"

Why it works:
• Number (7 is specific, countable, concrete — not a vague "many ways")
• Odd (7, 5, 13 > even 10, 20 — the perception is that it was counted exactly, not estimated)
• Benefit (first client in 60d — specific, a timeframe, desirable)
• Even-if (zero portfolio — removes an objection, makes it accessible)
• Proof (templates included — a tangible deliverable)

YOUR TURN (10 Minutes):

STEP 1: Choose an odd number (3, 5, 7, 9, 13 — the sweet spot is preferably 5-7)

STEP 2: The benefit of your product (what the client gets — be specific, quantify it)

STEP 3: A proof element (mention testimonials, data, an included tool — credibility)

STEP 4: Write the headline (fill in the template — "[N] Ways to [Benefit] ([Proof])")

STEP 5: Score it with the 4 U's (from yesterday's email — aim for 30+)

EXAMPLE OF YOUR TURN:
Product: Keto weight loss
Headline: "5 Keto Meals That Burn Fat for 24h (Recipes Inside, 12,000 Tested It, 78% Lost 15+ Lbs)"

DONE: A headline is written (in 10 min, an immediate result — momentum, a confidence boost of "I CAN write headlines," a tangible skill).

Tomorrow: Bullets (80%+ curiosity — the technique from page 47).

— [Name]

P.S. Want to share your headline? (Reply — I'll give specific feedback, we can refine it together, community learning, engagement).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Email #3: An actionable quick win (10 min, write a headline immediately), a simple technique (the Number formula is easy), an example is shown (yours written to demonstrate), YOUR turn (steps 1-5 are clear, execute now), an immediate result (a headline is done, a confidence boost), tomorrow (bullets are next), P.S. to share (an invitation for engagement, an offer of feedback).**

**Action rate #3:** 15-30% (recipients actually DO the exercise — they write a headline, they reply to share it, active not passive engagement).

• Copy reviews (specific feedback for you, iteration)
• Accountability calls (to get unstuck, you're not alone)

• Investment: R$2,997 (a system vs. DIY — for convenience, speed, support, and a guarantee)
• [Details link]: Transparent pricing, FAQ, case studies, so you can decide informed

BOTH are valid: I celebrate route A (succeeding with free DIY — I'm proud), I support route B (choosing the system — I'll help you implement it).

The default: Route A (the emails will continue 3x/week with educational content, zero pressure, an ongoing relationship).

Interested in Route B? [Link to explore] (always optional, zero obligation, just for information).

Gratitude: For these 7 days (you engaged, you learned, you joined the community — the beginning of a valued relationship, I wish you success whichever route you choose).

Officially welcome. See you Tuesday at 09:30! — [Name]

P.S. Feedback on the sequence? (The 7 emails — useful? Too much? Just right? — an honest reply, for continuous improvement based on your input).

**Email #7: A transition (indoctrination is complete, the rhythm is established), clear options (DIY for free continues as the default and is celebrated OR the system is an optional exploration), autonomous (it's your choice, both are valid), the default is route A (the emails continue, zero pressure), gratitude (the relationship is valued), P.S. for feedback (continuous improvement, subscriber input is valued).**

**Indoctrination → ongoing engagement conversion:** 70-85% (subscribers continue opening emails 8+, the relationship is solidified, indoctrination succeeded).
