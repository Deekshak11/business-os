## **SECTION 2.4 — WEBINAR FUNNEL (BRUNSON'S PERFECT WEBINAR)**

---

**Russell Brunson** (Expert Secrets, Perfect Webinar — a 90min live/automated structure):

---

### **WEBINAR FUNNEL STRUCTURE (5 Stages):**

---

**STAGE 1 — REGISTRATION PAGE (Capture Attendee):**

**Elements:**

- **Headline:** Specific promise (what they will learn on the webinar — "3 Secrets to Scale to R$100k")
- **Bullets:** 5-8 (tease the 3 secrets + bonuses for attending)
- **Social proof:** X registered (scarcity), testimonials from previous attendees
- **Date/Time:** Specific (creates urgency — "Tuesday 8 PM BRT, 90 min")
- **Form:** Name + Email (minimum — phone is optional with an incentive for SMS reminders)
- **CTA:** "Reserve Your Free Spot" (free is emphasized, limited seats are implied)

**Registration conversion:** 30-60% (page visitors → registered).

---

**STAGE 2 — INDOCTRINATION EMAILS (3 Emails Pre-Webinar):**

**Email #1 — Confirmation (Instant, Automated):**

```
Subject: "✅ Confirmed: Webinar Tuesday 8 PM (Add to Calendar)"

Body (150w):
"Confirmed! Your spot is reserved for the '3 Secrets to Scale to R$100k' webinar — Tuesday 8 PM BRT.

[BUTTON: Add to Google Calendar] [Button: Add to Outlook]

What you will learn (in 90 minutes):
• Secret #1: Why you're stuck (the 3 missing pillars, identify which one)
• Secret #2: How to integrate them (10x synergy, not isolated tactics)
• Secret #3: Implementation (a 12-week roadmap, hands-on not theory)

BONUS for attending live: 15-min Q&A (questions answered in real-time, personalized).

Reminder: I will send emails 24h before, 1h before, and 10 min before (so you don't miss it).

Prepare: Paper + pen (take notes, actionable content, immediate implementation).

See you Tuesday! — [Your Name]"

[Email: Confirms, add to calendar (commitment device), bullets tease value, live bonus (incentive to attend, not watch the replay), reminders (reduces no-shows), prepare instruction (active engagement), friendly close]
```

---

**Email #2 — Value Delivery (24h Before Webinar):**

```
Subject: "Tomorrow 8 PM: Webinar (+ Specific Prep for You)"

Body (200w):
"Tomorrow, Tuesday at 8 PM: The '3 Secrets to Scale to R$100k' webinar (your spot is confirmed).

Quick prep (to maximize the value of the 90 min):

BEFORE the webinar, answer mentally (be honest):
1. Current monthly revenue: R$____
2. Hours you work/week: ____
3. How long have you been stuck: ____ months
4. How many solutions have you tried: ____

[Self-diagnostic — pre-webinar engagement, identifies your specific gaps]

The webinar will address EACH of these (a personalized diagnosis, implementation customized to your situation).

PLUS: Deciding between attending live vs. the replay?

Live advantages:
• 15-min Q&A (YOUR questions answered, personalized)
• Surprise bonus (live attendees only — not in the replay, exclusive)
• Community (340+ others live, chat for networking, group energy)

Replay: Available for 48h (BUT without the Q&A, without the bonus, without the community — a solo experience).

Recommendation: Live if possible (3-5x the value of the replay).

Link: [Webinar room — test your audio/video 5 min before to resolve tech issues]

Tomorrow at 8 PM. See you! — [Name]"

[Email: Tomorrow reminder (urgency), self-diagnostic prep (increases engagement, personalizes the experience), live vs. replay (incentivizes live with FOMO for bonuses), link to test tech (removes the "tech isn't working" barrier), friendly reminder]
```

---

**Email #3 — Final Reminder (1 Hour Before):**

```
Subject: "🔴 LIVE IN 1 HOUR: Webinar at 8 PM (Link Inside)"

Body (100w):
"🔴 GOING LIVE in 1 hour (Tuesday 8 PM BRT — it's 7 PM now).

Webinar room link: [LARGE LINK]

Test your audio/video: 5 min before (at 7:55 PM — a tech check for a zero-stress start).

Prepare: Paper, pen (for note-taking, implementation).

340 are already in the room (waiting, chat is active, high energy).

Your spot: Guaranteed (you registered, your seat is reserved).

See you in 10 minutes! — [Name]

P.S. Can't make it live? A replay email will be sent tomorrow (available for 48h — BUT live = exclusive bonuses + Q&A, I recommend attending if you can)."

[Email: 1h urgency (final push), prominent link (removes friction of finding it), test tech (reassurance), social proof of 340 inside (FOMO), spot is guaranteed (honors commitment), replay option (permission to miss if necessary without guilt), P.S. incentivizes live]
```

**No-show rate:** 40-60% is typical (100 register, 40-60 attend — indoctrination emails reduce no-shows by 20-30%; without emails = 60-70% no-show).

---

**STAGE 3 — LIVE WEBINAR (90 Min — Perfect Webinar Structure from Doc 5):**

**(Already developed in Doc 5 Section 2.B.1 — structure is min 0-10 Origin Story, min 10-55 Three Secrets, min 55-75 Stack, min 75-90 Q&A)**

**Live attendee conversion:** 10-25% (webinar → purchase, high because the 90-min time investment = commitment, sunk cost, live energy, personalized Q&A).

---

**STAGE 4 — REPLAY SEQUENCE (3-5 Emails, 48h Window):**

**For those who didn't attend live OR attended but didn't purchase:**

---

**Replay Email #1 (Instant Post-Webinar):**

```
Subject: "Replay Available: '3 Secrets to Scale to R$100k' Webinar (48 Hours Only)"

Body (150w):
"The webinar just ended (8:00-9:30 PM, 90 min — 340 attended live, incredible energy).

REPLAY: Available now (for the next 48 hours only — expires Thursday at 9:30 PM).

[BUTTON: WATCH THE REPLAY NOW]

Missed it live? (78 registered didn't show up — no problem, the replay has the same content).

Attended BUT didn't purchase? (Thinking about it? Re-watch sections with the replay, make an informed decision).

DIFFERENCE between replay vs. live:
• Content: Identical (3 secrets, stack, proof — all the same)
• Bonuses: Live-only bonuses removed (the R$700 surprise bonus was exclusive to live attendees)
• Q&A: An edited version is included (15 min of common questions — not personalized to you, BUT useful answers)
• Urgency: 48h window (deadline on Thursday — after that, it's permanently gone)

Worth the 90 min? (12,000 have watched variations of this webinar, 4.8/5 average rating, '3-5 actionable takeaways' is common feedback — free educational value even if you don't purchase).

[REPLAY LINK]

Expires: Thursday 9:30 PM BRT (exactly 48h).

— [Name]"

[Email: Replay available (no guilt for missing it live), 48h deadline (urgency window), differences between live vs. replay (honest — bonuses removed for transparency, edited Q&A included), worth framing (social proof of 12k, ratings, free value), prominent link, deadline repeated]
```

---

**Replay Email #2 (24h After Webinar — Mid-Point):**

```
Subject: "24 Hours Left: Webinar Replay (+ Decision Framework)"

Body (200w):
"Replay: 24h left (expires tomorrow, Thursday at 9:30 PM — half the window is gone).

A quick decision framework (if you're hesitating):

DID YOU WATCH the replay?
☐ YES: Did you like the 3 secrets? (actionable? Clear? Applicable to YOU?)
  → If YES: Does the offer make sense? (R$2,997 investment, ROI calculated shown in the webinar at min 60-65, guarantee protects you — is it worth it?)
  → If NO: Okay (not a fit for everyone, that's honest — no pressure, grateful you watched)

☐ HAVEN'T watched yet: Watch in the next 24h (90 min, free education with R$2k+ in consulting value — worth it even if you don't purchase, learn the 3 secrets to apply on your own for free).

DECIDING on the offer?

A quick checklist:
☑ Stuck at the R$20-60k plateau? (the offer addresses this specifically — the 3 pillars)
☑ Tried 3+ solutions that failed? (validation at min 18-22 of the webinar, justification)
☑ Ready to invest R$2,997 + 12 weeks of work? (commitment required, a conditional guarantee)
☑ Does the guarantee protect you? (scale 2x OR get R$7,997 back — zero risk for you)

4/4 checks: The offer is a fit (click the link, 5-min application, 30-min diagnostic call).

<4: Maybe not a fit (wrong timing, different expectations — that's okay, grateful for your consideration).

[REPLAY LINK] [APPLICATION LINK]

24h. Decide informed (use the replay, framework, checklist — it's your autonomous decision, which I respect).

— [Name]"

[Email: 24h urgency (mid-point of the window), decision framework (helps those hesitating, structured thinking), checklist (self-qualify, autonomous), links to both (replay for education, application for the offer), respects the decision (no pressure, gratitude regardless)]
```

---

**Replay Email #3 (2h Before Expiration — Final Push):**

```
Subject: "🔴 2 HOURS: Replay Expires at 9:30 PM (Last Chance)"

Body (120w):
"🔴 Final 2 hours: The replay expires today at 9:30 PM BRT (gone permanently after).

Watched it? (Decide — yes/no on the offer, it's your informed choice).

HAVEN'T watched? (Still possible to watch the 90 min — start now at 7:30 PM, finish at 9 PM, 30-min buffer to decide).

[REPLAY LINK — LAST ACCESS]

Deciding on the offer?

Spots: 27 left (out of 100 — others decided during the webinar/replay, seats are filling).

Guarantee: Zero risk (R$7,997 back if you don't 2x — totally protected).

Deadline: Today at 9:30 PM (after that, you wait for the next cohort in March — a 4-month wait).

2 hours. Last chance (literally — a real countdown, not fake, historically enforced).

Act or not (your decision, autonomous, respected) — BUT decide INFORMED (not by default procrastination, a conscious choice).

[LINK] — [Name]"

[Email: Final 2h (extreme urgency), addresses both watched/not watched, replay link (last access), brief decision checklist (spots, guarantee, deadline), autonomy (respects the decision, doesn't manipulate, emphasizes an informed choice), literal countdown (credibility of enforcement mentioned)]
```

---

**Replay sequence conversion:** 3-10% (replay viewers → purchase, lower than live at 10-25%, BUT the volume compensates — 100 registered, 40 attended live, 60 watch the replay, total conversions = 40×20%=8 live + 60×6%=3.6 replay = 11.6 total vs. 8 from live only).
