## **SECTION 4.3 — TECHNIQUE #3: DATA/PROOF (STATISTICS TO OVERCOME DOUBT)**

---

**The objection:** "Does it really work? (I'm skeptical of the claims, I want proof)."

---

**Overcoming it with Data:**

```
"'Does it work?' — A valid question (skepticism is healthy, you've been burned before, demanding proof is justified).

THE DATA (Not claims — verified tracking):

47 companies have implemented it (from January 2023 - November 2024, over 23 months):

THE SUCCESS RATE: 89% (42 out of 47 achieved a minimum of 2x revenue growth in 12 months).

The distribution:
• 2-3x: 18 companies (38%)
• 3-5x: 19 companies (40%)
• 5x+: 5 companies (11%)
• Below 2x: 5 companies (11% — they were refunded + paid a R$5k penalty, transparently)

The average: 4.1x growth (a 3.8x median — a conservative estimate).

The timeframe: A 9.2-month median (a range of 6-18 months — an honest variance).

THE VERIFICATION (Not anecdotal):
• Case studies: 47 (each is documented with analytics screenshots, invoices, and timelines)
• Video testimonials: 12 (on-camera, authentic, cannot be faked)
• Third-party: 4.8/5 stars on Google reviews (89 reviews on an independent platform)

[Data links]: A folder of case studies, a playlist of testimonials, a link to the Google reviews — verify it yourself (total transparency, check the claimed data, it's not just 'trust me').

'Does it work?' → YES (89% data-proven, the 11% of honest failures are shown, abundant independent verification — it's been validated statistically not anecdotally, you can check it yourself with confidence)."
```

**250 words** — specific data (47, 89%, 4.1x, 9.2m — abundant numbers), a distribution (a breakdown of the tiers, not suspiciously uniform), the failures (the 11% are shown transparently), the verification (links to check for yourself — transparency), the conclusion (it works = proven by data not a claim).

---

**Effectiveness:** 9/10 (data is powerful against rational skepticism — numbers overcome doubt with logic, verification seals it).
