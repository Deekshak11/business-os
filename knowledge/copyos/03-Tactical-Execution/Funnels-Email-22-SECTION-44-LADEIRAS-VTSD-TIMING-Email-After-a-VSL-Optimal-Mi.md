## **SECTION 4.4 — LADEIRA'S VTSD TIMING (Email After a VSL — Optimal Minutes)**

---

**Ladeira specific to VTSD:**

"A person watches a VSL (45 min) and does NOT buy (70-95% of viewers — the majority don't purchase immediately).

When do you email a follow-up? (Timing is critical — too soon is annoying, too late and they forget)."

---

**Tested on 2,400 VSLs (Ladeira's data):**

---

**IMMEDIATE follow-up email (0-30 min after VSL completion):**

**Conversion:** 2.1% (low — too soon, they feel pressured, "I already saw the 45-min VSL pitch, another email immediately is pushy and annoying").

---

**Follow-up email 2-4 HOURS after:**

**Conversion:** 4.8% (better — breathing room, they've thought about it, the email is a gentle reminder not pressure).

---

**Follow-up email 24 HOURS after:**

**Conversion:** 6.2% ⭐ (PEAK — they thought about it overnight, considered it during the day, the email timing is a perfect reminder + soft urgency).

---

**Follow-up email 48-72 HOURS after:**

**Conversion:** 3.9% (declining — interest is cooling, they forget the VSL details, momentum is lost).

---

**Follow-up email 7+ DAYS after:**

**Conversion:** 1.2% (extremely low — they are cold again, they forget they watched the VSL, relevance is lost).

---

**OPTIMAL TIMING (Ladeira's VTSD Rule):**

**Email #1:** +24h after VSL completion (the sweet spot — 6.2% conversion).

**Email #2:** +48h total (day 3 after the VSL — a second reminder, increasing urgency).

**Email #3:** +72h total (day 4 — a final push, the deadline is approaching if applicable).

**Email #4+:** If it's a long campaign (days 5-7), BUT conversion diminishes (emails 1-3 = 80% of the total conversions, 4+ = 20% incremental).

---

**VSL follow-up sequence (Ladeira's Template):**

```
Watched VSL (Didn't Buy):

Email #1 (+24h): "Watched the VSL yesterday? (A quick thought for you)"
  - A brief recap (the 3 pillars), a decision framework (is it worth it?), a soft CTA, 6.2% conversion

Email #2 (+48h total): "48h to think (Decision framework + FAQ)"
  - Addresses common objections (price, time, will it work?), repeats the guarantee, gives a spot update, 3.1% conversion

Email #3 (+72h total): "Final 24h: The deadline is tomorrow (Last chance)"
  - Extreme urgency, a countdown, quantified loss, FOMO for the spots, 2.4% conversion

TOTAL: 3 emails, 72h window, cumulative conversion of 11.7% (6.2% + 3.1% + 2.4% — VSL viewers who didn't purchase immediately → 11.7% purchase from the follow-up sequence)

vs. NO FOLLOW-UP: 4.7% conversion (VSL alone, zero emails after)

THE DIFFERENCE: +7 points (11.7% vs. 4.7% — the follow-up sequence = +149% more conversions, it's critical not to abandon VSL viewers who don't purchase immediately).
```
