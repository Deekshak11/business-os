## **SECTION 7.3 — THE TOP 10 OBJECTIONS TO ALWAYS PRE-EMPT (A Universal Checklist)**

---

**Regardless of the product/niche:** 10 objections appear in 80%+ of cases (they are universal, always address them).

---

**THE PRE-EMPTION CHECKLIST (To Validate Your Copy):**

```
☐ 1. PRICE (Expensive, can't afford, ROI)
   Pre-empted in: The offer section (with an ROI calculation, daily framing, payment plans, a comparison of alternatives)

☐ 2. TIME (Busy, a long implementation, when will I see results)
   Pre-empted in: The bullets (20 min/day), with quick wins (results in weeks 1-2), and testimonials (from busy people who managed)

☐ 3. TRUST (Does it work?, are you credible?, is it a scam?)
   Pre-empted in: The proof section (with 5 tiers — testimonials, data, case studies, credentials, an extreme guarantee)

☐ 4. FIT (Is it different for my situation, my niche, is it for me?)
   Pre-empted in: The testimonials (with a diversity of 12 niches, varied situations, ages 29-65)

☐ 5. TIMING (Not now, later is better, urgency is absent)
   Pre-empted in: The urgency section (with a deadline, a price increase, and a quantified opportunity cost of delay)

☐ 6. COMPLEXITY (Is it too complex?, will I understand?, will I be overwhelmed?)
   Pre-empted with: Simplicity (20-30 min modules, fill-in-the-blank templates, support when you're stuck)

☐ 7. SUPPORT (Will I be alone?, is help available?, what if I get stuck?)
   Pre-empted with: Details of the support (bi-weekly calls, email in <24h, a group of 800+, you're never alone)

☐ 8. GUARANTEE (Is it real?, are the terms unclear?, is it enforced?)
   Pre-empted in: The guarantee section (with crystal clear terms, proof of enforcement with R$37k paid out, a highlighted visual box)

☐ 9. RESULTS (Are they realistic?, proven?, exaggerated?)
   Pre-empted with: The data (a range of 2.1x-9.7x variance, a conservative 3.8x median, the 11% of failures are shown honestly)

☐ 10. COMPARISON (Are there better alternatives?, will cheaper ones work?)
   Pre-empted in: The comparison section (each alternative is compared — an agency, coaching, DIY, doing nothing, each is worse/more expensive)

SCORE: __/10 objections pre-empted

10/10: ✅✅ Comprehensive (all the universal barriers are addressed, resistance is minimized)
7-9/10: ✅ Good (the major objections are covered, minor gaps are acceptable)
<7/10: ⚠️ Gaps (critical objections are missing, add them to the FAQ/body sections to address them)
```
