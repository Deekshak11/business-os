## **SECTION 6.1 — STATE 1-2 OBJECTIONS (Transactional — Price, Deal, Timing)**

---

**State 1-2 (Most-Aware/Product-Aware):** They know the product/category, they are comparing, their objections are transactional (price, the quality of the deal, the timing of the purchase).

---

**Typical State 1-2 objections:**

1.  **"The price is too high"** (a comparison to competitors, bargain hunting)
2.  **"Is there a discount available?"** (deal-seeking, transactional)
3.  **"When is the best time to buy?"** (timing optimization, waiting for a better deal)
4.  **"Are payment plans available?"** (for immediate affordability)
5.  **"What exactly is the guarantee?"** (the specific terms, the extent of the protection)

---

**Overcoming State 1-2 (Direct, Fast — No Need for Deep Education):**

```
Q: "R$1,997 vs. a competitor at R$997 — why is the price 2x?"

A (120w — brief, State 1-2 is already aware, a direct comparison):

"The price difference = a difference in DELIVERY:

The R$997 competitor:
• Theory modules (15h of passive videos, you watch alone)
• Basic templates (10-15, generic fill-in-the-blanks)
• Support: Email only (a 3-5 day response, impersonal)
• Guarantee: A basic 30-day refund

Our R$1,997:
• Implementation (80% doing, 20% watching — accountability calls, reviews of your copy, real-time help when you're stuck)
• Advanced templates (50+ specific, tested winners)
• Support: Email in <24h + bi-weekly calls (personal, ongoing)
• Guarantee: Your first sale in 60d OR R$2,497 back (results-based, we have skin in the game)

The R$1,000 difference = a R$10-30k/month difference in outcome (implementation vs. theory, support vs. solo — the ROI pays for the premium 10-30x over, it's worth it).

[The answer: A direct comparison (our features vs. theirs side-by-side), the value difference justifies the price difference, the ROI is shown (a R$1k premium = a R$10-30k difference in return), a brief 120w as State 1-2 doesn't need a lengthy education, they are already aware]"
```
