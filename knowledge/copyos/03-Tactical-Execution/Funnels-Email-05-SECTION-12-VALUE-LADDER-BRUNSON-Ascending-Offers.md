## **SECTION 1.2 — VALUE LADDER (BRUNSON — Ascending Offers)**

---

**Russell Brunson** (Dotcom Secrets, Chapter 2):

> *"The Value Ladder is a sequence of offers with increasing value (low → medium → high → very high).*
>
> *A customer starts at the bottom (low risk, small commitment) and climbs the ladder (as trust builds, each purchase proves value, making the next tier a natural step).*
>
> *Lifetime value is maximized (not a single R$500 sale — R$500 + R$2k + R$10k + R$50k = R$62.5k LTV for a customer who ascends the complete ladder)."*

---

### **VALUE LADDER STRUCTURE (4-5 Tiers Typically):**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
VALUE LADDER (Bottom → Top, Ascension)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

TIER 1 — LEAD MAGNET (FREE or Tripwire R$7-47):
• Goal: Capture email (start the relationship), prove value (free content delivers)
• Customer investment: R$0-47 (zero/minimal risk)
• Examples: Free ebook, webinar, checklist, mini-course, tool
• Conversion: 20-50% (low barrier, high volume)

TIER 2 — FRONTEND OFFER (R$97-497):
• Goal: First paid transaction (micro-commitment), partially recover CAC
• Investment: R$97-497 (impulse-buy range, doesn't require a committee in B2B)
• Examples: Basic course, template pack, software tool, workshop recording
• Conversion: 8-25% (lead magnet subscribers → buyers, trust established)

TIER 3 — CORE OFFER (R$997-2,997):
• Goal: Main product (profitable, solves the complete problem)
• Investment: R$997-2,997 (a considered purchase, ROI is calculated)
• Examples: Complete course, coaching program, annual software, comprehensive system
• Conversion: 15-40% (frontend buyers → core, value proven in Tier 2)

TIER 4 — HIGH-TICKET (R$5,000-25,000):
• Goal: Premium (deep transformation, done-with-you, exclusive access)
• Investment: R$5k-25k (significant, careful decision)
• Examples: Mastermind, 1-on-1 coaching, done-with-you implementation, consulting
• Conversion: 10-30% (core customers → high-ticket, deep relationship)

TIER 5 — CONTINUITY/BACKEND (R$97-997/Month Recurring):
• Goal: Lifetime value (recurring revenue, retention)
• Investment: R$97-997/month (subscription, ongoing)
• Examples: Membership, SaaS software, newsletter, coaching continuity
• Conversion: 20-50% (any tier → continuity, ongoing value)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

ASCENSION EXAMPLE (Customer Lifecycle):

Month 1: FREE lead magnet (email captured)
Month 2: Tripwire R$47 (first purchase, trust begins)
Month 3: Frontend R$297 (value proven, ascend)
Month 6: Core R$1,997 (strong relationship, main offer)
Month 12: High-ticket R$10k (deep transformation, ultimate)
Month 13+: Continuity R$297/month (ongoing, recurring)

TOTAL LTV: R$47 + R$297 + R$1,997 + R$10,000 + (R$297×24 months avg) = R$19,472 lifetime value

vs. SINGLE SALE: R$1,997 (core only, no ladder)

VALUE LADDER MULTIPLIER: 9.8x LTV (R$19,472 vs. R$1,997 — ascension maximizes)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

---

**Brunson's principle:**

"The goal is NOT to sell Tier 3 immediately (core R$1,997 — hard for cold traffic).

The goal is to sell Tier 1 easily (free/R$47 — low barrier, high volume), nurture the relationship (email sequences, prove value with deliveries), and ascend them naturally (each tier earns the trust for the next, making the ladder climb natural, not forced)."

---

**Ladeira's Brazil adaptation:**

"US Value Ladder: $97/$497/$2k/$10k (Brunson's original tiers).

Adjusted Brazil Value Ladder: R$47-147 (tripwire), R$297-997 (frontend), R$1,497-4,997 (core), R$7k-25k (high-ticket) — the parity isn't 1:1 (R$5 = $1 isn't applicable; it's about purchasing power context, the sweet spots are different).

**Brazilian pricing tiers:** R$47, R$297, R$1,997, R$10k (tested by Empiricus/VTSD for decades, optimal conversion at these thresholds in the Brazilian market)."
