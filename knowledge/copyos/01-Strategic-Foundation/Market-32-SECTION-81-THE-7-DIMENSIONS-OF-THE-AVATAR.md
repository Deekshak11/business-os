## **SECTION 8.1 — THE 7 DIMENSIONS OF THE AVATAR**

**Words:** 1,400

**Template (each dimension developed with 200 words):**

1. **Demographics:** Age, gender, location, income, education, profession
2. **Psychographics:** Values, beliefs, personality, lifestyle
3. **Behavioral:** What they buy, where, when, how they decide
4. **Pain:** Their top 3 problems (in their EXACT language)
5. **Desire:** Their top 3 objectives (in their EXACT language)
6. **Objections:** Their top 5 reasons for NOT buying
7. **History:** Their previous attempts, why they failed
