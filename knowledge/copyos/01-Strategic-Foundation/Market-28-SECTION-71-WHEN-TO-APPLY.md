## **SECTION 7.1 — WHEN TO APPLY**

**Words:** 500

**Applicable IF:**
- The product is >$1,000 (high-ticket)
- The avatar's income is >$200,000/year (the affluent threshold)
- They make decisions differently (time>money, exclusivity, a paradoxical risk aversion)

**Not applicable:** For a mass market (<$500 products, <$100k avatar income).
