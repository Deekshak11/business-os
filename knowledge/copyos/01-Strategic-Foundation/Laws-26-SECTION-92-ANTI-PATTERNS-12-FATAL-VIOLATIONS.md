## **SECTION 9.2 — ANTI-PATTERNS: 12 FATAL VIOLATIONS**

---

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
ANTI-PATTERNS — NEVER DO THESE (Violations of the Laws)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ #1: REQUIRING A COMPLEX ANALYSIS (S2)
Avoid: Tables with 10+ variables, unexplained jargon, mental calculation
Use: Simplicity, "we've calculated it for you"
It's fatal because: S2 gets tired → abandonment (Kahneman: "S2 is lazy")

❌ #2: BLAMING THE PROSPECT FOR THEIR FAILURES
Avoid: "You failed because you didn't try hard enough"
Use: "It wasn't your fault — the system was flawed" (Warren #2)
It's fatal because: The ego doesn't accept blame → resistance

❌ #3: FAKE SCARCITY (BRAND SUICIDE)
Avoid: A countdown that resets, "last units" perpetually
Use: REAL urgency (a cohort is closing, the price is genuinely going up)
It's fatal because: The customer notices → trust is PERMANENTLY dead
(Ladeira: "It burns the brand irreversibly")

❌ #4: UNNECESSARILY MONETIZING THE SOCIAL
Avoid: "A community (a $500 value)," mixing social+market norms
Use: "A community (priceless)" OR a pure transaction
It's fatal because: Ariely — money kills a social norm (the Daycare study)

❌ #5: IRRELEVANT AUTHORITY
Avoid: A PhD in physics on diet, a tech CEO on investments
Use: An expert IN THE DOMAIN (a cardiologist on heart health)
It's fatal because: Obvious irrelevance → zero credibility

❌ #6: A PRICE WITHOUT AN ANCHOR
Avoid: "$997" alone
Use: "A $3k value → Today for $997" (a high anchor first)
It's fatal because: No reference = it can seem expensive

❌ #7: ABSTRACT/GENERIC
Avoid: "Excellent results," "very effective"
Use: "23 lbs in 90 days," "+347% ROI"
It's fatal because: Hopkins — "Platitudes roll off like water from a duck" (it's forgotten)

❌ #8: A LARGE COMMITMENT FIRST
Avoid: A $10k ask from cold traffic (zero micro-commits)
Use: FREE → $27 → $497 → $3k → $10k (a Ladder)
It's fatal because: Maximum resistance (Freedman: 17% vs. 76%)

❌ #9: IGNORING THE LF8
Avoid: A product with no biological appeal
Use: Identify 1-2 LF8 drives and write the copy around them
It's fatal because: No LF8 = no biological desire = impossible

❌ #10: DATA KILLING EMOTION
Avoid: Stats + a story together (the stats kill the emotion)
Use: A story OR data (Heath: Rokia alone > Rokia+stats)
It's fatal because: Activating S2 kills the emotional S1

❌ #11: NO "BECAUSE"
Avoid: Claims without a reason
Use: "Because [a reason]" always (even a simple one)
It's fatal because: Langer — no "because" = -57% compliance

❌ #12: COPY THAT DOESN'T SELL BUT "WINS AWARDS"
Avoid: Optimizing for creativity/awards
Use: Optimize for SALES (the only metric — Axiom 1)
It's fatal because: Hopkins — "Fine writing is a disadvantage"
Awards ≠ revenue

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

IF 3+ ARE VIOLATED → REWRITE URGENTLY
IF 1-2 ARE VIOLATED → CORRECT before publishing
IF 0 ARE VIOLATED → PROCEED (also validate against checklist 9.1)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
