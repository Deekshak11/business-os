# **SECTION 0 — PREREQUISITES & HOW TO USE**

---

### **CRITICAL Prerequisites:**

**→ REQUIREMENT: Docs 2-3 completed**

**Specifically, you need:**

✅ **Doc 2 (Diagnosis):**
- Intensity score (7-10 — informs pricing: Intensity 9 = can charge $2-5k, Intensity 6 = $200-500 max)
- State/Stage (informs offer complexity, guarantee strength)
- Top 5 objections (each objection = potential bonus to address it)
- Detailed Avatar (informs what they value — time? Money? Status?)

✅ **Doc 3 (Positioning):**
- Big Idea (offer stacks AROUND the Big Idea — not a generic offer)
- Mechanism (offer delivers the mechanism, explains HOW)
- Touchstone (integrated into offer presentation)
- Voice (offer tone consistent with your voice)

---

**Product exists:** It works, it delivers a result (do not create an offer for a non-existent/non-functional product).

---

### **Time Investment:**

**Reading:** 3-4h (offer engineering frameworks)

**Creation:** 2-4h (filling out the Offer Document — identifying obstacles, creating bonuses, pricing, guarantee, urgencies)

**Total:** 5-8h (for a complete, defensible offer).

**ROI:** Offer > Copy (Halbert Doc 2 — offer 25% of success, copy 25%, market 50%).

Wrong offer + brilliant copy = failure.

Right offer + mediocre copy = success.

**2-4h of offer engineering = critical** (don't skip, don't rush).

---

### **Output:**

**COMPLETE OFFER DOCUMENT** (Part VIII, 8 pages).

Contains: Core product, bonuses (5-7), pricing (tiers, payment plans), guarantee (level 3-5), urgencies (3-4 types stacked), value calculation, ROI justification.

**Input for:** Docs 5-13 (copy presents the offer, it doesn't create it — the offer is engineered here).

---

### **Connection:**

Doc 2 (diagnosis) → Doc 3 (positioning Big Idea) → **Doc 4 (offer uses Big Idea + Intensity)** → Docs 5-13 (copy communicates the offer).

**Flow:** Intensity 9 (Doc 2) + "3 Pillars" Big Idea (Doc 3) → Offer pricing at R$2,997 (high-ticket appropriate for Intensity 9), results-based guarantee (Stage 5 skepticism), bonuses solve obstacles (identified in Doc 2 research) → Copy (Docs 5-13) presents the engineered offer.
