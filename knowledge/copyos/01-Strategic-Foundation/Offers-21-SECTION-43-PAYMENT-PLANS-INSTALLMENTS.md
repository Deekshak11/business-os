## **SECTION 4.3 — PAYMENT PLANS (INSTALLMENTS)**

---

### **THE PRINCIPLE:**

**Hormozi tested** (1,000+ offers, gyms + software):

**Pay-in-full:** R$997 → 100 conversions (baseline)

**Installments:** 3 × R$397 (R$1,191 total) → **142 conversions** (+42%)

**Same offer.** Different payment structure.

**Result:** +42% conversions AND +19% total revenue (R$1,191 vs. R$997 — you earn more).

---

**Why it works (Psychological):**

**A. Lower barrier:** R$397 seems "smaller" than R$997 (even if the total is higher long-term — mental accounting, Kahneman).

**B. Spread the pain:** 3 small pains (R$397 each) < 1 big pain (R$997 lump sum) — pain distribution is easier psychologically.

**C. Gradual commitment:** The first payment is a small commitment (R$397 is manageable), not a full upfront commitment (R$997 is scary).

---

### **OFFER STRUCTURE:**

```
OPTION 1 — PAY IN FULL (Incentivize):
R$997 one-time (save R$194 vs. installments)

OPTION 2 — INSTALLMENTS (Facilitate):
3 × R$397 = R$1,191 total (no interest, spread over 90 days)

OR (if longer):
12 × R$107 = R$1,284 total
```

**Framing:** Pay-in-full = "save" (a savings incentive — some will choose it, saving R$194).

Installments = "makes it easier" (accessibility — the majority will choose this, and you earn an extra R$194-284).

---

**Typical conversion:**

65-75% choose installments (ease > savings for the majority).

25-35% pay-in-full (savings-oriented, have the cash).

**Your revenue:** The weighted average is HIGHER than if you only offered pay-in-full (the installment premium compensates for the lower pay-full %).

---

**Best practice:**

**Always offer BOTH** (choice increases conversions by +30-50% vs. a single option — Hormozi validated).

**Installments price is 15-20% higher** than pay-in-full (in total) — this is justified (you assume the risk of non-payment, which is worth a premium).
