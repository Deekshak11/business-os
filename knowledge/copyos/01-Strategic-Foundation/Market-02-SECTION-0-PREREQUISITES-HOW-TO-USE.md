# **SECTION 0 — PREREQUISITES & HOW TO USE**

---

### **Prerequisites:**

**→ REQUIREMENT: Doc 1 complete** (Universal Laws of Persuasion)

Especially Axiom 2 (Copy channels pre-existing desire) — to understand WHY market diagnosis matters.

**Optional but recommended:** A product or service already exists (to diagnose real vs. hypothetical market-fit).

---

### **Time Investment:**

**Complete reading (first time):** 3-4 hours (all Parts I-VIII)

**Research + completion:** 3-5 additional hours (Part IX — Campaign Brief)

**Total:** 6-9 hours (for a complete market diagnosis)

**ROI:** Prevents 20-40 hours of writing copy for the wrong market (Schwartz/Halbert validated — market choice = 80% of success).

---

### **How to Use This Document:**

**FIRST TIME (Linear):**

1. Read Parts I-VIII sequentially (to understand the diagnostic frameworks)
2. During your reading: Take notes about YOUR product/market
3. Afterward: Part IX (fill out the Campaign Brief using the frameworks you've learned)

**SPECIFIC REFERENCE (Experienced Users):**

Use the mini-index → Go to a specific framework (e.g., "I need to diagnose the Schwartz State" → Part I).

---

### **Critical Output of This Document:**

**A COMPLETE CAMPAIGN BRIEF** (Part IX, Sections 9.1-9.2, 6 filled-out pages).

**This document = MANDATORY INPUT for ALL subsequent documents:**

- → REQUIREMENT: Doc 3 (Positioning) — The Big Idea is chosen based on the State/Stage diagnosed here
- → REQUIREMENT: Doc 4 (Offers) — Pricing is based on Intensity, guarantee is based on the skepticism level (Stage)
- → REQUIREMENT: Doc 6 (Headlines/Leads) — The type is chosen based on State/Stage
- Docs 5, 7-13 — All use the avatar, pain, language, and objections identified here

**Without a completed Campaign Brief = writing blind copy** (guesswork, not strategy).

Schwartz: "Time spent in diagnosis is never wasted. Time spent writing copy for the wrong market is ALWAYS wasted."

---

### **Connection to Other Documents:**

**Doc 1** (Universal Laws) = WHY the tactics work

**Doc 2** (this one) = WHO your market is, WHERE they are (awareness/sophistication)

**Doc 3** (Positioning) = HOW to differentiate (Big Idea for a specific State/Stage)

**Doc 4** (Offers) = WHAT to sell (offer engineering for Intensity/skepticism)

**Docs 5-13** (Execution) = HOW to communicate (copy structures, tactics)

---

**Workflow:**

Doc 2 (diagnoses) → Completed Campaign Brief → Docs 3-13 (use the diagnosis)

Without Doc 2: Docs 3-13 are generic (not customized for YOUR specific market).
