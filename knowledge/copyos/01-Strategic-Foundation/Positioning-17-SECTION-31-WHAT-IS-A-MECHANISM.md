## **SECTION 3.1 — WHAT IS A MECHANISM**

**Words:** 800

**Content:**

Schwartz's definition (Breakthrough, p.58): "The Mechanism = HOW the product delivers the benefit."

The benefit can be generic (everyone promises it).

The mechanism can be UNIQUE (differentiation).

Examples: "Lose weight" (a common benefit) via "ketosis" vs. "a calorie deficit" vs. "hormonal optimization" (diverse mechanisms).

Why it matters: Credibility (it explains the HOW, the logic), differentiation (a unique mechanism for a common benefit), it's proof-friendly (you can demonstrate that the mechanism works).
