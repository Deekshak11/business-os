## **SECTION 7.3 — SURPRISE BONUS TIMING (Why Post-Price):**

---

**Timing options tested:**

**Option A — The surprise BEFORE the price:**

The slides: Value stack → Surprise bonus → Total → Price

The effect: The bonus is integrated into the value (R$5,100 includes the surprise) — not really a surprise, it's expected.

The conversion boost: +8-12% (marginal).

---

**Option B — The surprise AFTER the price:**

The slides: Value stack → Total of R$5,100 → Price of R$997 → **THEN the surprise bonus of R$700**

The effect: It **genuinely surprises** them (they thought the package was done, BUT there's MORE — it exceeds their expectations).

The conversion boost: **+35-50%** (massive — a tipping point effect).

---

**Why B >>> A:**

**Heath** (Doc 1 Section 5.0 — SUCCESs): The **Unexpected = memorable, impactful**.

**The expectation:** R$5,100 in value, a price of R$997 (the decision is forming).

**The surprise:** + a R$700 bonus for FREE (their expectations are exceeded — delight).

**Reciprocity** (Doc 1 Section 2.1): You gave MORE than you promised (you said R$5,100, you delivered R$5,800) — the obligation to reciprocate intensifies (buying = reciprocation).

**A tipping point:** For fence-sitters ("R$997... I'm thinking... maybe...") → the surprise = pushes them over ("OK that's INSANE value, I can't say no").

---

**The 30-minute timer (for the surprise bonus):**

**Why 30 minutes** (not 24h, not "today"):

It's short enough: The urgency is immediate (they can't procrastinate — 30 minutes forces a decision NOW).

It's long enough: It's not a panic (they have time to think for 30 minutes, not a rushed and unfair 5 minutes).

**A visible countdown:** Creates a crescendo of anxiety (watching 29:47... 29:46... each second = closer to losing the surprise bonus).

**Temporal scarcity:** In addition to the main offer's deadline (a double urgency — a deadline on Friday AND a 30-minute timer).

---

**Brunson tested:**

A surprise bonus with NO timer: +35% conversions.

A surprise bonus WITH a 30-minute timer: +52% conversions.

**The timer adds:** +17 additional percentage points (an urgency layer on top of the surprise).
