## **SECTION 8.2 — A COMPLETE FILLED-OUT EXAMPLE (A Real Product)**

---

**EXAMPLE PRODUCT:** An Online Copywriting Course (in Brazil, a typical info product)

**ALL 8 PAGES ARE FILLED OUT** (a complete demonstration of how to use the template):

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
OFFER DOCUMENT — COPYWRITING PRO COURSE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

PAGE 1 — THE CORE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NAME: "Copywriting Pro — The First Sale in 60 Days System"

DESCRIPTION:
A complete online copywriting course (from beginner → a sellable professional).
12 video modules (40h total), practical exercises in each, and copy reviews
are included. For entrepreneurs/aspiring copywriters wanting to make a freelance
income of R$10-30k/month.

DELIVERY: ✓ Digital (instant access to the portal after purchase, streaming)

COMPONENTS:
• 12 HD video modules (headlines, bullets, emails, pages, funnels,
  client acquisition, pricing, each 3-4h of content)
• PDF workbooks for each module (exercises, fill-in-the-blank templates)
• 3 copy reviews (submit your first 3 pieces, you get specific
  written feedback + a 10-15min video for each)
• Email support (a response in <48h, questions answered)

THE CORE OBSTACLES IT SOLVES:
• The obstacle: They don't know how to WRITE good copy → Modules 1-8 teach it (headlines,
  bullets, structures, language — a complete skill)
• The obstacle: They don't know how to GET clients → Modules 9-10 (outreach, a portfolio,
  pricing, closing)
• The obstacle: The fear of "not being good enough" → The 3 copy reviews (validation,
  feedback, a confidence boost)

STANDALONE VALUE: R$2,500

Based on: Competitor pricing (similar courses are R$1,997-3,497, an average of R$2,700)
+ The value of coaching (the reviews = 3h of a coach's time @ R$300/h = R$900, if separate).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BONUSES:

BONUS #1: The Templates Library (200+ Fill-in-the-Blank)
• Headlines (50 templates), Bullets (40), Emails (60), Pages (30), Ads (20)
• It solves: Not knowing where to start (an intimidating blank page) → The templates = a starting point
• Format: Digital PDFs + editable Docs
• Cost: R$0 (digital)
• Value: R$500 (if sold standalone — comparable products are R$400-600)
• Margin: R$500 (an infinite ratio)
• Score: 6/6 ✓

BONUS #2: The Annotated Swipe File (150+ Examples of Winners)
• 150 high-performing pieces of copy (sales letters, emails, ads — annotated
  with why they work, a psychological breakdown)
• It solves: Not having examples to study (20h+ of research alone) → The swipes are curated and ready
• Format: A digital PDF
• Cost: R$0
• Value: R$400
• Score: 6/6 ✓

BONUS #3: The Private Facebook Group (Lifetime Access)
• 800+ copywriter members (peer support, networking, feedback, jobs are posted)
• It solves: Being alone (no accountability, no community) → The group = belonging + help
• Format: Access (to a Facebook group)
• Cost: R$0 (after setup)
• Value: R$800 (community access, networking is worth it)
• Score: 6/6 ✓

BONUS #4: The Client Scripts Pack (Cold Email, DM, Pitch Call)
• Outreach templates (10 tested cold emails, 5 Instagram DM scripts, 3 pitch
  call frameworks)
• It solves: Not knowing HOW to approach prospects (anxiety, fear of rejection) → The scripts
  = confidence, tested language
• Format: Digital
• Cost: R$0
• Value: R$300
• Score: 6/6 ✓

BONUS #5: The Pricing Calculator & Proposal Templates
• To calculate hourly vs. value-based pricing (a tool), and proposal templates (3
  formats: hourly, project, retainer)
• It solves: Not knowing what to CHARGE (undercharging R$500 when it's worth R$5k) → The calculator
  = pricing confidence
• Format: Digital (Excel + Google Sheets + PDF templates)
• Cost: R$0
• Value: R$200
• Score: 5/6 ✓

TOTAL BONUSES: 5
Total value: R$2,200
Total cost: R$0
Margin: R$2,200 (infinite)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

VALUE EQUATION SCORED:

Dream Outcome: 8/10
(R$10-30k/month, freelance, remote, autonomy — stacked, quantified, and emotional with
LF5+LF6+LF8, it could be improved: by adding the identity transformation explicitly)

Perceived Likelihood: 9/10
(340 students tracked, 89% got their first sale in 60d, a results-based guarantee + R$500,
5 testimonials, it's strong)

Time Delay: 3/10 (lower is better)
(First results in 14 days of outreach, a sale in 45-60d on avg, it's quick)

Effort: 4/10 (lower is better)
(15min/day of videos, 30min of practice, 45min total is manageable, DFY templates,
support is available)

OVERALL: (8 × 9) ÷ (3 × 4) = 72 ÷ 12 = 6:1

Target of 5:1+: ✓ Achieved (a good value perception)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PRICING:

Cost to deliver: R$180/student (platform R$50/month ÷ 100, support 2h @ R$50/h,
5% refunds)

10x minimum: R$1,800

Chosen: R$2,997

Ratio: 16.6x ✓ (excellent margins, sustainable scaling)

ROI: (R$4,700 value - R$2,997) ÷ R$2,997 = 0.57 = insufficient

WAIT — recalculate the value perception issue:

Actual perceived value: Core R$2,500 + Bonuses R$2,200 = R$4,700

ROI: 1.57:1 (below the 3:1 minimum — A PROBLEM)

THE FIX: Raise the perceived value (add a bonus, raise the value assignments) OR lower the
price (a last resort).

THE SOLUTION: Add Bonus #6 (A Recorded Masterclass, R$600) → Value is R$5,300

ROI: (R$5,300 - R$2,997) ÷ R$2,997 = 0.77 = 2.77:1

Still below the 3:1 ideal.

AN ALTERNATIVE: Lower the price to R$1,997 (a slight sacrifice of margin).

ROI: (R$4,700 - R$1,997) ÷ R$1,997 = 1.35:1 = still weak.

THE BEST FIX: Raise the value to R$6,500+ (add R$1,800 more in bonuses OR raise
the assignments of the current ones).

[Iteration — balance the pricing/value until a 3:1+ ROI is achieved]

Final decision: A price of R$2,997, a value of R$7,500 (core is R$3,000, bonuses are R$4,500)

ROI: 2.5:1 (acceptable, close to 3:1)

PRICING OPTIONS:
• Full: R$2,997 (save R$300)
• Installments: 3 × R$1,099 = R$3,297 (+R$300 total, +10%)

FRAMING:
Daily: R$8.20/day (R$2,997 ÷ 365)
The copy: "Just R$8/day (2 coffees) for a skill that generates R$10-30k/month"

CHARM PRICING: R$2,997 ✓ (not R$3,000)

ANCHORING: A value stack of R$7,500 BEFORE the price reveal

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

GUARANTEE:

LEVEL: 4 (Results-Based Conditional)

THE COPY:

"90-Day Results-Based Guarantee:

If you:
• Complete modules 1-12 (100% progress on the platform)
• Submit 3 pieces of copy for review (participation is validated)
• Do outreach to a minimum of 30 prospects (activity is tracked — emails/DMs sent)

And you do NOT get:
• Your first copywriting sale of a minimum of R$500 (with invoice/screenshot proof)
• Within 90 days of your purchase

THEN:
• You get a 100% refund: R$2,997 back
• You KEEP the bonuses: R$4,500 in value (the templates, swipes, lifetime group access, scripts,
  and calculator — all of them are yours permanently)

What you get in total if you don't make a sale: R$7,497 (the refund + the kept bonuses)

Your investment was: R$2,997

NET: You GAIN R$4,500 (the value of the bonuses) even with a refund.

You literally cannot lose (the worst case = R$4,500 in bonuses for free, the best case = a sale
+ the bonuses + the skill for life)."

The reason I'm offering this: 89% of students get a sale in 60d (tracked with 340 students). I have
high confidence the system works. I want to totally remove the risk for you.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

URGENCY (A Stack of 4 Types):

1. DEADLINE:
   Friday, December 6, 2024, at 11:59 PM, BRT
   The reason: The group coaching class starts on Monday (late entry = disruptive)
   REAL: ✓ (enrollment closes on Friday, it's enforced)

2. PRICE INCREASE:
   Current: R$2,997
   Afterward: R$4,997 (+R$2,000)
   REAL: ✓ (it will genuinely be raised on Monday)

3. BONUS SCARCITY:
   Bonus #6 The Masterclass (R$600) is for the first 80 only
   Current: 52 sold, 28 remaining
   REAL: ✓ (it will be removed after 80)

4. QUANTITY:
   A max of 100 students, 52 sold, 48 remaining
   The reason: Quality (>100 = support degrades)
   REAL: ✓ (it stops at 100)

THE STACK: A deadline + a price increase (+R$2k loss) + a bonus removed (R$600) + the spots (48) +
the opportunity (the R$2k/week the system generates on avg, a delay = a loss)

════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE ONE-LINER OFFER SUMMARY:

"Master copywriting → your first sale is guaranteed in 90d for R$2,997 (a
R$7,500 value, a 2.5:1 ROI), only 48 spots remaining, it expires on Friday — A Guaranteed Sale
Or You Gain R$4,500."

════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
