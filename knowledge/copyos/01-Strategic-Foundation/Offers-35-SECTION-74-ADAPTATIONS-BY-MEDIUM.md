## **SECTION 7.4 — ADAPTATIONS BY MEDIUM**

---

**A WEBINAR (live or automated):**

Slide-by-slide exactly as described (17-19 slides, precise timing).

**The pace:** An average of 45-90 seconds per slide (don't rush — it totals 15-30 minutes for the stack section of a 60-90 minute webinar).

---

**A VSL (Video Sales Letter):**

Similar slides BUT:
- Visual text overlays (bullets appear on the screen)
- A voice-over explains (not just reading — conversational)
- B-roll footage (clips of the products, testimonials are interspersed)

**The timing:** Typically at the 25-40 minute mark (the stack section of a 30-45 minute VSL).

---

**A SALES LETTER/PAGE (Written):**

Sections replace the slides (the same sequence):

```
[SECTION: What You're Getting]
Core product description...

[SECTION: Bonus #1]
Templates... R$500

[Continue with the bonuses, each in its own section]

[SECTION: The Total Value]
R$5,100 [the anchor]

[SECTION: The Investment]
"Normally R$5,100... BUT you're not going to pay... not R$3k... not even R$2k...
Just R$997"

[SECTION: The Surprise Bonus]
"And IF you act in the next 30 minutes..."

[SECTION: The Urgency]
"It expires on Friday... 27 remaining..."
```

**Visual hierarchy:** Headers, bullets, bold numbers (scannable, even in long-form).

**Subheads:** Guide the progression (not a wall of text — it's structured).

---

**An EMAIL (Ultra-Condensed BUT the Sequence Is Maintained):**

```
Paras 1-2: "Here's the package:
• Core (R$2,500)
• Bonuses 1-5 (R$2,600)
Total: R$5,100"

Para 3: "The investment: R$997 (you save R$4,103)"

Para 4: "PLUS, in the next 30 minutes: A surprise bonus of R$700 for free"

Para 5: "It expires on Friday at 11:59 PM. 27 spots remaining. Afterward: the price goes up by +R$1,000."

CTA: "Secure my spot now"
```

**Condensed** (a 200-300 word email vs. a 3,000+ word sales page) BUT **the essence is preserved** (value → price → surprise → urgency → action).
