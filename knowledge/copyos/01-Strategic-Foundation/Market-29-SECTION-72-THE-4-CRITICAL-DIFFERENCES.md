## **SECTION 7.2 — THE 4 CRITICAL DIFFERENCES**

**Words:** 1,100

**Difference #1 — TIME > MONEY:**

The affluent buy TIME (not a product).

The copy talks about the ROI of time: "$X saves you 5h/week = time with your family" (vs. the mass market: "$X saves you $Y" — a focus on money).

---

**Difference #2 — EXCLUSIVITY:**

They want what others CAN'T have.

Tactics: Limited (24 clients/year max), qualification (an application process), insider (members-only).

---

**Difference #3 — RISK AVERSION (A Paradox):**

The more $ they have = the more averse they are (not less) — they have more to lose.

A STRONGER guarantee is needed: 90-120 days + results-based + a penalty ($X for their time if it fails).

---

**Difference #4 — THE CONTRARIAN APPEAL:**

They love "insider knowledge" (a secret used by the elite).

Copy: "The secret used by..." works (vs. the mass market, where it can seem elitist).
