## **SECTION 1.5 — MINIMIZE EFFORT & SACRIFICE**

---

### **TACTICS:**

**TACTIC #1 — QUANTIFY EFFORT (Specific = Manageable):**

"Requires dedication" (vague — assumed massive) vs. "15-20 min/day" (specific — oh, that's manageable).

"Hard work" (scary) vs. "3 exercises of 10min each, 3x/week (30min total)" (concrete, doable).

**Specific effort:** = lower perceived barrier (you know exactly what's required, and it seems reasonable).

---

**TACTIC #2 — REMOVE COMMON SACRIFICES:**

The category typically requires sacrifices (identify through research — Doc 2, what conventional paths demand).

**You:** Explicitly state it's NOT required.

```
Conventional weight loss sacrifices:
- Gym membership ($50-100/month)
- Meal prep (10-15h/week)
- Giving up favorite foods (chocolate, pizza, etc.)
- Boring cardio (hours)

Your offer:
"No gym (20min bodyweight home workouts)
No meal prep (simple 15min recipes, or eat out keto-friendly)
No giving up chocolate (delicious, satisfying keto chocolate)
No boring cardio (a pleasant 20min walk, not HIIT torture)"
```

**Each "no":** = a sacrifice removed = lower perceived effort.

**Accumulated:** 4 sacrifices removed = a significantly easier path is perceived.

---

**TACTIC #3 — DONE-FOR-YOU ELEMENTS (Reduce Work):**

```
Templates (vs. creating from scratch — 10h saved)
Swipes (vs. researching/inventing — 8h saved)
Scripts (vs. improvising — anxiety removed)
Setup assistance (vs. figuring it out alone — 5h saved, frustration avoided)
```

**Each DFY:** = work YOU did (the client doesn't have to) = less effort on their side.

**Quantify:** "Templates save you 10h" (tangible), not just "templates included" (value is unclear).

---

**TACTIC #4 — CAPABILITY REASSURANCE:**

"If you [can do a familiar, easy task], you can do this."

Examples:
- "If you know how to use WhatsApp, you can do this" (zero tech barrier)
- "If you can read an email, this is for you" (literacy is the only requirement)
- "If you've driven a car, you have enough coordination for [this exercise]" (low physical capability required)

**Familiar comparison:** = perceived barrier demolished (if I can use WhatsApp — everyone can — I can do this).

---

**TACTIC #5 — SUPPORT/ACCOUNTABILITY (Not Alone):**

"Never alone: 24/7 support (email response <2h avg), peer group (500+ helping each other), bi-weekly calls (group coaching, live Q&A)."

**Alone:** = high effort (figure everything out yourself, getting stuck = frustration).

**Support:** = low effort (help is available, get unstuck fast, hand-holding).

Perceived effort: ↓↓ (a safety net exists).
