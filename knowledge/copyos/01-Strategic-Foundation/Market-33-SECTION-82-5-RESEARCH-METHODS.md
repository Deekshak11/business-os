## **SECTION 8.2 — 5 RESEARCH METHODS**

**Words:** 2,200

**Each method detailed (400-450 words):**

**Method #1 — 1-on-1 Interviews:**
- How to recruit (5-10 people)
- SPIN questions (a script)
- Record + transcribe (the tools)
- Mine their exact language

**Method #2 — Review Mining:**
- Amazon (competitors), G2/Trustpilot
- Ctrl+F for keywords ("disappointed," "love," "but")
- Categorize (pains, desires, objections)

**Method #3 — Forum/Group Lurking:**
- Reddit, Facebook groups, Quora
- The top discussions (upvotes, comments)
- Raw language (unfiltered)

**Method #4 — A Survey:**
- Google Forms, Typeform
- 8-10 open-ended questions
- An incentive (a $50 raffle)

**Method #5 — A Competitor Swipe:**
- Read the ads/copy of successful competitors
- Reverse-engineer it (what avatar, pain, angle)
