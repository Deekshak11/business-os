## **SECTION 3.4 — THE FEATURE → MECHANISM → BENEFIT TRANSLATION**

**Words:** 800

**The chain:** A technical Feature → The Mechanism (HOW it works) → The Functional Benefit → The Emotional Benefit → The Identity.

**Examples of 3 products** (a course, a SaaS, a physical product), with each chain fully developed (150-200w each).

An applicable translation template.

---

```
╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║ APPLICATION BOX — PART III (THE MECHANISM)        ║
╠═══════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                   ║
║ ✅ IDENTIFYING A UNIQUE MECHANISM:                ║
║                                                   ║
║ 1. List the product's features (15-20 of everything it has/does) ║
║                                                   ║
║ 2. For each feature: "HOW does this deliver the benefit? ║
║    (A mechanism extraction — HOW it works)"       ║
║                                                   ║
║ 3. Compare with competitors (the top 10):         ║
║    Research: Do they have the same mechanism?     ║
║    • IF YES (5+ competitors): It's not unique (a commodity) ║
║      → Find a different one OR present it differently ║
║    • IF NO (0-2 competitors): It has unique potential ║
║      → Validate its defensibility (the next step) ║
║                                                   ║
║ 4. The defensibility test:                        ║
║    "Can competitors copy this mechanism in <6 months?" ║
║    • IF YES: It's ephemeral (not sustainable differentiation) ║
║      → Find a deeper mechanism (harder to replicate) ║
║    • IF NO: It's defensible (a patent, years of expertise ║
║      to develop, proprietary, high complexity)    ║
║      → Leverage it (emphasize its uniqueness)     ║
║                                                   ║
║ 5. The communicability test:                      ║
║    "Can I explain this mechanism in 2-3 simple sentences?" ║
║    • IF YES: It's communicable (good — it can be sold) ║
║    • IF NO: It's too complex (simplify it or find a simpler ║
║      mechanism to communicate)                    ║
║                                                   ║
║ The output: 1-2 unique mechanisms (defensible,    ║
║ communicable, and differentiating).               ║
║                                                   ║
║ → REQUIREMENT: Doc 2 (the Stage informs the complexity — ║
║ for Stage 1-2 a simple mechanism is OK, for Stage 4-5 an elaborate ║
║ mechanism is needed to cut through)               ║
║                                                   ║
║ (Ref: Section 3.3 — The Drill, drill down the 5 levels, ║
║ always aim for levels 4-5 in the copy)            ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

**Application Box:** ~600 words
