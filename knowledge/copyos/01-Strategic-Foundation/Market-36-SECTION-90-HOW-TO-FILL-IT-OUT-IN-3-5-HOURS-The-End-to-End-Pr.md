## **SECTION 9.0 — HOW TO FILL IT OUT IN 3-5 HOURS (The End-to-End Process)**

**Words:** 900

**Step-by-step:**

**Hour 1:** Research (Method #2 reviews + #3 forums — 60min)

**Hour 2:** Interviews (2-3 people, SPIN questions — 60min)

**Hour 3:** Diagnose the State/Stage (questions + competitor analysis — 60min)

**Hour 4:** Calculate Mass×Intensity, validate with E5, identify the FBs (60min)

**Hour 5:** Fill out the complete template, review it (60min)

**Total:** 5 hours → A complete Campaign Brief (the input for all subsequent docs).
