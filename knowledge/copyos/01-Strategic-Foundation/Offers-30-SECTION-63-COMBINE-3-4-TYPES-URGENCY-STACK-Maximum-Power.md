## **SECTION 6.3 — COMBINE 3-4 TYPES (URGENCY STACK — Maximum Power)**

---

**The Ladeira/Agora principle:**

> *"Don't just use 1 type of urgency (it's single-dimensional, it can be ignored).*
>
> *STACK 3-4 types (it's multi-layered, overwhelming — multiple losses are avoided simultaneously, resistance crumbles)."*

---

**A COMPLETE STACK Example (5 types integrated):**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
MULTI-LAYER URGENCY (A Copy Section):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

"This offer EXPIRES on Friday, November 29, 2024, at 11:59 PM, Brasília time.

[TYPE #1: DEADLINE — temporal scarcity, inexorable]

Why does it expire on Friday? The group coaching class starts on Monday (late joiners = disruptive, the quality suffers for everyone — the pedagogy requires that we start together).

[REASON-WHY: Credible, logical]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

After Friday (if you haven't acted):

The PRICE: Goes up from R$997 to R$1,997.
The increase: +R$1,000.
You LOSE: R$1,000 in savings (gone forever).

[TYPE #4: PRICE INCREASE — tangible financial loss]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

The BONUS: The 'Premium Templates Vault' (a R$800 value, 150+ templates) will be PERMANENTLY REMOVED.

Only the first 100 buyers receive it.

Current status: 78 sold. 22 bonus spots remaining.

After 100 or after Friday (whichever comes first): The bonus is gone (never to be offered again).

[TYPE #3: BONUS SCARCITY — a loss of exclusivity]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

CLASS SPOTS: A maximum of 100 students (a quality limit — above 100, individual attention is <30min/student/month, support is degraded, and results drop by 40%+ as data shows).

Sold: 78
Remaining: 22 spots

First-come, first-served (after 22, you'll have to wait for the next class in March 2025 — a 4-month wait).

[TYPE #2: QUANTITY SCARCITY — limited slots]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

OPPORTUNITY COST:

The system generates an average of R$8,000/month in additional revenue (tracked across 47 companies, the median result).

Each WEEK you delay: R$2,000 in potential lost revenue.

1 month of "thinking about it": R$8,000 gone (time doesn't come back, the revenue is lost forever).

The investment: R$997.

The math: Do you lose R$8,000 to "save" R$997? (the logic is absurd)

[TYPE #5: OPPORTUNITY COST — the invisible loss made visible]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

IMAGINE: November 2025 (1 year from now).

You didn't act (you hesitated, you postponed, you "thought about it").

You're still: Stuck at R$30k/month (not the R$80k+ that's possible).

The regret: "Why didn't I act when I had the chance?"

"Today I would be transformed. I lost a year."

Don't let that happen.

[TYPE #7: REGRET — a future emotional state]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

5 REASONS to act NOW (not later):

1. The deadline is on Friday (the class starts, late entry is impossible)
2. The price is going up by +R$1,000 (the savings will be lost)
3. The bonus will be removed (the exclusivity will be gone)
4. There are only 22 spots (sold out = a 4-month wait)
5. The opportunity cost is R$8k/month for each month of delay (lost forever)

So many losses can be avoided by acting today.

There are zero logical reasons to wait (only procrastination, fear, and inertia — all of which are surmountable).

ACT NOW.

[THE STACK = overwhelming, multi-dimensional urgency]

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

---

**5 types combined** (Deadline + Price + Bonus + Quantity + Opportunity + Regret).

**Each type:** = an additional layer of loss to be avoided (acting today = avoiding 5+ losses).

**The cumulative effect:** The logic is overwhelming (so many reasons to act, zero reasons to wait — the decision is obvious).

Prospect: "If I wait: I lose R$1,000 on the price, I lose a R$800 bonus, I lose my spot (and have to wait 4 months), I lose an R$8k opportunity, and I'll have future regret. If I act: I avoid ALL of those losses. The action is obvious." (resistance is dissolved).

---

**Conversion of a stack vs. a single type:**

**A single urgency** (a deadline only): +60-80%

**A stack of 3 types:** +110-150%

**A stack of 4-5 types:** +140-200%

**It's not linear** (3 types ≠ 3× a single type) — it's **exponential** (synergy, reinforcement, it's overwhelming).

---

**Agora's practice:** A stack of a minimum of 3-4 types (Deadline + Price are almost always there, + 1-2 others that are context-dependent).

**Empiricus:** "We never launch with a single urgency (it's weak, it's ignorable). A stack of 3-4, always (the layers = irresistible)."

---

**The balance:**

3-4 types: Optimal (comprehensive, powerful, not excessive).

5-7 types: Potential overkill (it can be overwhelming and "too many reasons, it's suspicious" — diminishing returns).

**The sweet spot:** 3-4 well-executed types (each one real, each one developed in the copy).
