# **PART I — THE 3 FOUNDING AXIOMS**

---

Before tactics, formulas, templates, swipe files.

There are **3 absolute truths** about human persuasion.

---

Discovered empirically by masters (1904-1966):
- John E. Kennedy (1904)
- Claude C. Hopkins (1923)
- Eugene M. Schwartz (1966)

Validated scientifically (1974-2011):
- Robert Cialdini (1984)
- Daniel Kahneman (2002, Nobel Prize in Economics)
- Dan Ariely (2008)

---

**As immutable as the laws of physics.**

They do not change with:
- Technology (direct mail → email → ads → the unknown future)
- Culture (Brazil, USA, Japan, Africa — they are universal)
- Time (1904, 2024, 2100)

---

**Violate them = Copy fails** (no matter how creative)

**Respect them = Copy works** (even if it's "ugly")
