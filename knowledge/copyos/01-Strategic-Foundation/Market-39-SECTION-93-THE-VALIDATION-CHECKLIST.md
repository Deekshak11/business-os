## **SECTION 9.3 — THE VALIDATION CHECKLIST**

**Words:** 450

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE CHECKLIST — BEFORE PROCEEDING TO DOCS 3-13
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

☐ The State is diagnosed (1-5, validated via questions)
☐ The Stage is diagnosed (1-5, 2-3h of competitor research)
☐ Mass × Intensity is calculated (a score >100k is ideal, Intensity 7+)
☐ The Starving Crowd is validated (4-5 signs are present)
☐ The 3 False Beliefs are identified (The Vehicle, The Internal, The External — they're specific)
☐ The E5 checklist is met (4/4 criteria)
☐ The Avatar is complete (all 7 dimensions are filled out)
☐ Their exact language is collected (transcripts, reviews, quotes)
☐ The top 5 objections are listed (research-based)
☐ The LF8 is identified (1-2 primary appeals)

SCORE: __/10

8-10/10 ✅ → PROCEED to Doc 3 (a solid diagnosis)
6-7/10 ⚠️ → Fill in the gaps (the missing items)
<6/10 ❌ → MORE RESEARCH is needed (the diagnosis is incomplete,
             the copy based on it will be weak)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
