## **SECTION 2.3 — THE OFFER TEST (JOYNER — 10-Person Validation)**

---

### **THE PROCESS:**

**BEFORE investing in copy/ads ($):**

Test the offer with 10 people from your avatar group (friends, list segment, community members).

---

**The question:**

"If I offered you [COMPLETE OFFER: stack, pricing, guarantee, everything] for [PRICE], would you buy it?"

**No:** "Would you like this product?" (vague interest).

**Yes:** "Would you BUY it for R$X?" (a question of real commitment).

---

**Interpreting the results:**

**8-10 "YES":** Irresistible offer ✅ (launch immediately, high confidence)

**5-7 "YES":** Good offer ⚠️ (launch is possible, or iterate to improve to 8+)

**0-4 "YES":** Weak offer ❌ (DO NOT launch, redesign the offer before spending $ on copy/ads)

---

**Joyner (rule of thumb):**

> *"If the offer doesn't sell in a face-to-face CONVERSATION (a free test, just asking),*
>
> *Copy will NOT save it.*
>
> *Fix the offer FIRST (until 8+/10 say yes conversationally).*
>
> *THEN write the copy (to present a strong offer, not try to sell a weak one).*
>
> *Halbert's echo: Offer > Copy. Always."*

---

**Bonus — Collect the "Why nots?" (If <8 yes):**

For people who said "no" or "maybe": "What's causing the hesitation?"

Their answers reveal: Objections (price too high, don't trust it, a feature is missing, etc.).

**Use the insights:** Adjust the offer (add a stronger guarantee, add a bonus that addresses the objection, adjust the pricing).

**Re-test:** with 10 new people (iteration).

**Repeat** until you get 8+/10 (the offer is validated as strong).
