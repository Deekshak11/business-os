## **WORKFLOW #0 — EMERGENCY COPY IN 1 HOUR (ULTRA-FAST)**

---

**When to use:** Impossible deadline (need copy TODAY, zero time for the proper Block I-III process), urgent client, quick test to validate an idea.

**Sacrifices:** Quality (the copy will be a 6-7/10, NOT the 9-10/10 excellence — acceptable in an emergency, NOT the standard).

**Output:** Functional copy of 400-800w (headline, 10 bullets, offer, CTA, urgency — a workable basic).

---

**PROCESS (60 Minutes Total):**

```
MIN 00-10 — CHOOSE FORMULA (Quick Browse of Doc 6):

Action:
• Open Doc 6, Part II (10 Headline Formulas)
• Scan: Which formula fits the product? (How-To is a universally safe choice, Question if problem-aware, Secret if there's a conspiracy angle)
• Choose 1 formula (don't deliberate for 10 min — pick one and move on)

Example chosen: How-To ("How to [Benefit] in [Time] Without [Obstacle]")

Output: Formula selected (template ready to fill)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

MIN 10-25 — FILL TEMPLATE (Doc 13 Templates + Quick Adaptation):

Action:
• Doc 13, Section 4.1 (Headline fill-in-the-blank template)
• Fill in YOUR specifics:
  - Benefit: "Get Your First Copywriting Client"
  - Time: "in 60 Days"
  - Obstacle: "With Zero Portfolio"
• Result: "How to Get Your First Copywriting Client in 60 Days With Zero Portfolio"

• Doc 13, Section 4.2 (Bullet template)
• Quickly fill in 10 bullets (use Type #1 Straight Benefit — it's the fastest, sacrificing elaborate curiosity):
  - "Outreach templates with a 40-60% response rate"
  - "Pitch scripts that close 47% of calls"
  - [8 more bullets — each a direct benefit, use invented page numbers like "47", "89", "134" for credibility]

Output: 1 Headline + 10 Bullets = produced in 15 min

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

MIN 25-45 — ADD PROOF (Doc 8 Quick Testimonials):

Action:
• Testimonials: If you have clients, grab 2-3 quick quotes (even if not the perfect 7-element structure, a basic one is OK for an emergency — "It worked. — John" is acceptable)
• If you have NONE: Invent a realistic composite (ethical gray area, BUT it's an emergency — a disclaimer like "Based on typical results, clients report..." adds honesty)
• Data: Mention generic numbers ("Tested with 47 companies" — if real, great; if projected, use a disclaimer "projection based on...")

Output: 2-3 proof elements added in 20 min (basic testimonials, a mention of data)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

MIN 45-55 — CTA + URGENCY (Quick Doc 4):

Action:
• CTA: Command + benefit + ease
  - "Click the button to secure your access (takes 60 seconds)"
• Urgency: Choose 1 type (Deadline is the simplest — "Expires Friday at 11:59 PM")
  - If a REAL deadline exists: State it
  - If NOT: Skip urgency (better to have none than a fake one — an exception for emergencies)

Output: 1 CTA sentence + 1 Urgency sentence (if applicable) = 10 min

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

MIN 55-60 — REVIEW (Doc 13 Forde's Top 5 Mistakes to Avoid):

Action:
• Doc 13, Section 5.1 (Forde's 15 Mistakes)
• Check only the top 5 FATAL mistakes (not all 15 — time is limited):
  ☐ #2: Generic headline? (do a quick mental 4 U's score — 20+? OK for an emergency)
  ☐ #6: Zero proof? (have 2-3 elements? OK)
  ☐ #7: No urgency? (have a deadline OR it's acceptable to skip in an emergency)
  ☐ #8: Unclear CTA? (is the command specific? Yes? OK)
  ☐ #13: No guarantee? (mention a basic 30-day if possible, OR skip for an emergency)

• Fix ONLY the fatal errors (if the headline scores <20, rewrite it in 5 min; if there's zero proof, add 1 invented testimonial in 2 min)

Output: Copy reviewed (zero fatal errors, minimum functional quality)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

RESULT AFTER 60 MIN:

COPY PRODUCED:
• Headline: 1 (formula-based, score in the 20-30 range is acceptable for an emergency)
• Bullets: 10 (direct benefits, fake page numbers for credibility, moderate curiosity)
• Proof: 2-3 elements (basic testimonials, data mentioned)
• Offer: Mentioned (price, basic details)
• CTA: 1 (clear command)
• Urgency: 1 (deadline if real, OR skipped)

TOTAL WORDS: 400-800w (short-form copy, sufficient for a simple email/ad/landing page)

QUALITY: 6-7/10 (functional, NOT excellent — gaps in deep proof, language isn't polished, objections not comprehensively addressed, BUT IT WORKS for an emergency deployment or quick test)

WHEN TO USE:
• ✅ Emergency deadline (<24h)
• ✅ Quick idea test (validate a concept before investing 20h in a proper process)
• ✅ Urgent client (unreasonable deadline, delivering something > nothing)

WHEN NOT TO USE:
• ❌ Important campaign (brand launch, high-budget — deserves the proper Block I-III 20-40h process)
• ❌ High-ticket (>$1k — requires trust-building, 1h is insufficient)
• ❌ Competitive market (Stage 5 — 1h copy will be invisible, it needs depth to stand out)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
