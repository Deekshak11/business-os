## **WORKFLOW #5 — HIGH-CONVERSION LANDING PAGE (90 Min):**

```
OBJECTIVE: An opt-in landing page with 30-50% conversion (above the 20-40% benchmark, excellent performance)

PROCESS:

MIN 00-20 — HEADLINE (Condensed Workflow #1 from Doc 6):
• Generate: 5 headlines (using formulas like How-To, Question, Number — relevant to the lead magnet)
• Score: 4 U's for each (aim for 30+)
• Choose: The highest-scoring one (if there's a tie, get a quick peer vote from 2 people)

MIN 20-35 — BULLETS (Quick 8 Bullets from Doc 7):
• Extract: The lead magnet's benefits (what's inside? List 8-10)
• Write: Using Type #1 Straight Benefit (the fastest — "Discover X," "Learn Y," each benefit is direct)
• Tease: Add slight curiosity ("the technique on page 23," "the 4-step formula" — maintain moderate gaps, NOT extreme fascinations for a free lead magnet)

MIN 35-50 — PROOF (Quick Social Proof from Doc 8):
• Numbers: "8,347 downloads" (if real; if not, a realistic estimate like "2,400+ downloads in 18 months")
• Rating: "★★★★★ 4.8/5" (if you have reviews; if not, skip or use a generic "highly rated")
• Quote: 1 micro-testimonial ("Best free resource. — Carlos M." — 10 words is sufficient)

MIN 50-65 — FORM + CTA (Doc 5 Section C.3 + Minimize Friction):
• Form: EMAIL ONLY (zero other fields — minimal friction, each field reduces conversion by 10-15% according to Doc 4)
• CTA button: "DOWNLOAD FREE NOW" (command + benefit + soft urgency, green color, large, above the fold)
• Reassurance: "Zero spam. Unsubscribe with 1-click anytime." (removes hesitation, GDPR compliance)

MIN 65-75 — VISUAL LAYOUT (Above the Fold is Critical):
• Headline (top, large, bold)
• Subhead (expands on the promise, 20-40w)
• Bullets (8, scannable, with white space)
• Form (email field, green button — ALL visible without scrolling)
• Proof (acceptable below the fold — a logo bar for "As Seen In," testimonials, social numbers)

MIN 75-90 — QC + TEST (Doc 13 Checklist + Mobile):
• Checklist: Headline 30+? 8 bullets? Email-only form? Clear CTA? Everything above the fold?
• Mobile test: View on a phone (60-70% of traffic is mobile — is it responsive? Readable? Is the button large and tap-friendly?)
• Load speed: <3 sec (compress images, use clean code — every extra second increases bounce by 7%)

OUTPUT AFTER 90 MIN:
• Landing page (300-600w of copy — headline, 8 bullets, proof, form, CTA)
• Above the fold: 100% (headline, top 3-4 bullets, form, CTA — visible without scrolling)
• Quality: 8-9/10 (optimized for high conversion, likely to achieve 30-50% opt-in benchmarks)

DEPLOY: Drive traffic (ads, organic — monitor conversion, target 30%+, if below, diagnose with Doc 12 to fix the bottleneck)
```
