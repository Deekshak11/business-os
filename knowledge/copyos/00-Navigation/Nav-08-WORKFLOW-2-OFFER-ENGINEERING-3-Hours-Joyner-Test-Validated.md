## **WORKFLOW #2 — OFFER ENGINEERING (3 Hours — Joyner Test Validated):**

```
OBJECTIVE: An irresistible offer (8-10/10 people say YES in the Joyner test)

PROCESS:

HOUR 1 — OPTIMIZE THE VALUE EQUATION (Doc 4 Part I):

00-20min: Score the current offer (if one exists) or the new offer plan:
• Dream Outcome (1-10): How big/desirable is it? (quantify, stack multiples, make it aspirational)
• Perceived Likelihood (1-10): Is there enough proof? (testimonials, data, guarantee)
• Time Delay (10-1, lower is better): How fast is the result? (quick wins, immediate access)
• Effort (10-1, lower is better): How easy is it? (DFY templates, support, automation)

20-40min: Identify the weakest component (the lowest score from 1-10)

40-60min: Fix the weakest:
• If Dream Outcome is low (5/10): Stack benefits (add a holistic dimension — business + family + health)
• If Likelihood is low: Add proof (2-3 testimonials, upgrade the guarantee to Level 3+)
• If Time is high (7/10, slow): Add quick wins ("results in 7 days," immediate access)
• If Effort is high: Add ease (templates, step-by-step guides, 24/7 support)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

HOUR 2 — STACK BONUSES (Doc 4 Part III):

00-30min: Brainstorm obstacles (what prevents the customer from achieving the Dream Outcome? List 10-20)

30-60min: Create bonuses (each obstacle = a bonus solution):
• Obstacle: "I don't know what to write" → Bonus: "50+ fill-in-the-blank templates"
• Obstacle: "I'll get stuck on my own" → Bonus: "Support group with 800+ members"
• [8-12 bonuses total — high perceived value of $100-500 each, low delivery cost of $0-50 for digital]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

HOUR 3 — TEST + DOCUMENT (Doc 4 Section 2.3):

00-40min: Joyner's 10-Person Test:
• Present the complete offer (Core + Bonus stack, total value, price, guarantee, urgency) to 10 people from your avatar group
• Ask: "Would you buy this?"
• Count: YES (target 8-10), NO (acceptable 0-2), MAYBE (refine if 3+)

40-60min: Iterate if <8 YES:
• Identify objections (why NO/MAYBE? Ask them)
• Fix (add a bonus to address the objection, upgrade the guarantee, reduce the price, clarify the value)
• Retest (quickly with 5 people, aim for 4-5/5 "yes" = 80%+ threshold)

60min: Document (use the Doc 4 Offer Document template — fill in the sections, save, and it's ready to be used in Docs 5-11 for the copy)

OUTPUT AFTER 3H:
• Offer Document (a complete 8p document)
• Validated (passed the 8-10/10 Joyner test)
• Ready to: Write copy that sells THIS offer (with high confidence, as the offer is tested and irresistible)
```
