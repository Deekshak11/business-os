## **WORKFLOW #1 — SYSTEMATIC HEADLINE (30 Min — High Quality):**

```
OBJECTIVE: A headline scored 30-40 points (excellent, tested, ready to scale)

PROCESS:

MIN 00-15 — GENERATE (Doc 6 Part II — 10 Formulas):
• Use 3-4 formulas (How-To, Question, Number, Secret — for diversity)
• For each formula: Write 3-5 variations (total of 12-20 headlines generated)
• Time: 15min (quantity > quality in this phase, brainstorm quickly)

MIN 15-25 — SCORE (Doc 6 Section 1.2 — 4 U's):
• Score EACH headline (20 headlines × 1min each for scoring = 20min)
• Useful (0-10), Urgent (0-10), Unique (0-10), Ultra-Specific (0-10)
• Eliminate those scoring <25 points (weak, not worth refining)
• Shortlist: 5-8 headlines (in the 25-35 point range)

MIN 25-30 — SELECT TOP 3 (Quick Peer Review):
• Show the top 5-8 to 2-3 people (colleagues, OR an avatar if available quickly)
• Ask: "Which one makes you want to read more?" (forced ranking)
• Top 3 by votes = finalists

OUTPUT AFTER 30 MIN:
• Headlines: Top 3 (scored 30-35+ points, peer-validated)
• Ready to: A/B test (with small traffic of 100-500 each), OR choose the highest-scored if immediate testing isn't possible

NEXT STEP: Implement the winner (the copy body, lead, and bullets will use the chosen headline)
```
