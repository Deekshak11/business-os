## **WORKFLOW #3 — EMAIL SEQUENCE (4 Hours — Write 5-7 Emails):**

```
OBJECTIVE: A complete sequence ready to send (Soap Opera with 5 emails, Nurture with 10 emails, or Indoctrination with 7)

PROCESS:

HOUR 1 — CHOOSE TYPE + OUTLINE (Doc 10 Part III):

00-15min: Decide on the sequence type (based on the goal):
• Soap Opera: If you want story-driven engagement (for a launch, or to warm up a cold list)
• 90/10 Nurture: If you want long-term educational trust-building (for an ongoing relationship)
• Indoctrination: If you're welcoming new subscribers (for the first 7 days)
• PLF Launch: For a product launch event (14-day sequences)

15-60min: Outline the emails (subjects + bullet-point body for each):
• Soap Opera: 5 emails (Set the Stage, Rising Action, Climax, Falling Action, Resolution + Lesson)
• Structure each: Subject line (quick 4 U's), Body bullets (3-5 main points for each email), Cliffhanger (where to end to drive them to the next one)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

HOUR 2-3 — WRITE EMAILS (Doc 10 Section 5 Templates):

Email #1 (30min):
• Subject: (Collier/Hodgson techniques, aim for 25+ on the 4 U's)
• Body: 250-350w (Paragraph 1 for the Hook, 2-3 for Expand, 4 for Transition, 5 for the Cliffhanger)
• Use Template from Doc 10 Section 5.1 (fill and adapt Soap Opera #1)

Emails #2-5 (30min each = 2h total):
• Similar process (subject, body of 250-350w, a cliffhanger for each except #5, which is the resolution)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────

HOUR 4 — EDIT + SCHEDULE (Doc 9 for Polish + Doc 10 for Timing):

00-30min: Editing pass (language from Doc 9 — add 5-10 power words per email, check for a 3:1+ YOU-ratio, read each email aloud for flow)

30-45min: Schedule (on a platform like ActiveCampaign/Klaviyo — upload emails, set triggers and timing for a 24h delay in a daily Soap Opera rhythm, send a test to yourself to verify)

45-60min: Final QC (checklist — do all subjects score 25+ on the 4 U's? Is the body conversational? Are the cliffhangers compelling? Do the links work? Is the unsub link present? GDPR compliance? — Doc 13 Section 5.3)

OUTPUT AFTER 4H:
• Email sequence: 5-7 emails (250-350w each, 1,250-2,450w total)
• Scheduled: On the automation platform (triggers set, timing correct for Ladeira's 09:30 BRT)
• Quality: 7-8/10 (good and functional, NOT a perfect 9-10 BUT strong and workable)

NEXT: Send the sequence (monitor opens/clicks/conversions, optimize based on data from Doc 12)
```
