## **SECTION 5.2 — HODGSON'S 3-PARAGRAPH FORMULA (A Universal Structure)**

---

**Richard Hodgson** (a Direct Mail classic — a universal 3-paragraph opening that is applicable to ANY Collier type):

---

**THE STRUCTURE:**

**PARAGRAPH 1 — THE HOOK (1-2 Sentences, 20-40 Words):**

Grab their attention (a question, a statement, a stat, the start of a story — choose a Collier type).

**An example:** "89% of people are stuck. Why? (They're missing 3 pillars — identify which in 2 min)."

---

**PARAGRAPH 2 — EXPAND (3-4 Sentences, 60-100 Words):**

Develop the hook (with context, light agitation, and the stakes).

**An example:** "It's not a lack of effort (you work TOO MUCH — 60-80h, validated). It's not a lack of intelligence (you are capable). It's the SYSTEM (isolated pillars = a partial 1×1×1=1; an integrated one = an exponential 10×3×5=150x — the math shows it, and 47 companies have proved it)."

---

**PARAGRAPH 3 — A TRANSITION (2-3 Sentences, 40-60 Words):**

Promise a revelation and transition to the body.

**An example:** "In the next few minutes: the 3 pillars will be explained (each one, with its implementation and integration). Identify which one YOU are missing (a self-check). Here's pillar #1..."

---

**THE TOTAL 3-PARA:** 120-200w (concise, universal, and applicable to any Collier type you choose).

---

**COMBINATION Examples (A Collier Type + the Hodgson Structure):**

**Example A — A Question (Collier #1) + Hodgson's 3-Para:**

```
[P1-Hook]: "Are you stuck even though you're working 60h?"
[P2-Expand]: "It's not the hours (more hours are actually worse, leading to burnout). It's the pillars (a weak offer, a missing funnel, unpredictable traffic — each is partial alone, but complete together)."
[P3-Trans]: "The 3 pillars will be revealed next. Identify your gaps. Here's..."
```

**Example B — A Story (Collier #3) + Hodgson:**

```
[P1]: "John, 43, has been stuck at $28k for 18 months."
[P2]: "He tried ads (and burned $12k), hiring (which collapsed his margins), and automation (which was complex and incomplete). Each was partial. He was frustrated."
[P3]: "Then: The 3-Pillar System (integrated, not isolated). In 9 months: he was at $112k (4x). How? Next..."
```

---

**Flexibility:** Collier is the CONTENT (what to say), and Hodgson is the STRUCTURE (how to organize it).

**A mix-and-match:** Any Collier type fits the Hodgson 3-para structure (a universal applicability).
