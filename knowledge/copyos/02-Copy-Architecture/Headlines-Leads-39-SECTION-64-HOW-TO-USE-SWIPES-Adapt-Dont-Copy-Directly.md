## **SECTION 6.4 — HOW TO USE SWIPES (Adapt, Don't Copy Directly)**

---

**A CRITICAL RULE:**

**Don't:** Copy a headline directly (it's plagiarism + it's not customized for your product).

**Do:** ADAPT the structure (extract the template and fill in YOUR specifics).

---

**The adaptation process (5 Steps):**

```
STEP 1 — Find a similar swipe (by State, niche, and type)

STEP 2 — Identify the STRUCTURE (the template)
An example swipe: "How [a Person] Got [a Result] in [a Time] Even with [an Obstacle]"
The extracted template: "How [X] Got [Y] in [Z] Even with [W]"

STEP 3 — Fill in YOUR specifics
[X] = Your avatar/client
[Y] = Your quantified result
[Z] = Your timeframe
[W] = The obstacle you remove

STEP 4 — Customize it (with YOUR voice, brand, and differentiation)

STEP 5 — Score it with the 4 U's (to validate that the adapted headline is strong)
```

---

**An example of adaptation:**

**The swipe:** "How a Shy Accountant Made $10k in 30 Days Using a 3-Step Method (Even with Zero Experience)"

**The extracted structure:** "How an [Unlikely Person] Made [$ Amount] in [a Time] Using a [Method] (Even with a [Limitation])"

**YOUR adaptation:** "How a Busy Parent Made $15k/Month with Copywriting in 90 Days Using a Template System (Even with Only 2 Hours/Day Available)"

**Customized:** The unlikely person (a busy parent vs. a shy accountant — YOUR avatar), the amount ($15k vs. $10k — YOUR market), the time (90 days vs. 30 — YOUR realistic timeframe), the method (a template system vs. a 3-step system — YOUR differentiation), the limitation (2h/day vs. zero experience — YOUR obstacle).

**The score:** U=9, U=7, U=8, U-S=10 = 34/40 ✅ (the adapted version is strong).
