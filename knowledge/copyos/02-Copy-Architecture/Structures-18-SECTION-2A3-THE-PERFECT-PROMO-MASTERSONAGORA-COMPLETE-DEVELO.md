---

### **WHEN TO USE A PERFECT PROMO (vs. a Standard Sales Letter):**

✅ **Info products** (newsletters, courses, memberships — where the education is a preview of the product)

✅ **State 3-4** (researching/problem-aware — the hunger for education is high)

✅ **Price R$1,000-10,000** (high-ticket — justifies a 10k+ word deep dive)

✅ **Authority positioning** (you are a teacher/expert via generous education, not a pushy vendor)

✅ **Stage 4-5** (sophisticated/saturated — education cuts through, a pitch alone is ignored)

✅ **An SEO strategy** (10k+ words is SEO gold, it ranks for multiple long-tail keywords, and it drives long-term organic traffic)

### **THE 12-SECTION MASTERSON STRUCTURE (Each One Developed):**

---

**SECTION 1 — THE HEADLINE (30-60 Words — Longer Than Standard, with an Educational Angle):**

**Perfect Promo headlines are comprehensive** (they pack multiple elements — authority, discovery, mechanism, proof, action).

**A typical formula:**

```
[A credible authority] Reveals/Discovers [a specific Mechanism/Secret] That
[a quantified Benefit] — [Why it's Hidden/Ignored: a conspiracy element] —
[A proof element: a Study, Numbers] — [Actionable: a List Inside, a Strategy Revealed]
```

---

**A developed example (Financial Newsletter):**

```
"A Former Goldman Sachs VP (22 Years on the Trading Floor, Managed $2.4 Billion in Institutional
Accounts, and Quit in 2019 over Ethics Because He Couldn't Hide It from Clients) Reveals the Complete List of 67
'Dividend Aristocrats' — Stocks That Have Paid GROWING Dividends for 25+ Consecutive Years and Beat the S&P 500 78% of the Time Over the Last 30 Years (The Backtested Data Is Public and Verifiable) — Why Your Broker NEVER Mentions Them: A Conflict of Commission (You Buy-and-Hold = Zero Commissions for Them, and They Lose an Average of $4,200/Client/Year, Documented) — A 3-Stock Starter Portfolio Is Inside (Works for a $5,000 or $500,000 Account, and You Rebalance 1x/Year in 10 Minutes)"
```

**60 words** (long, BUT densely packed):

**A breakdown of the elements:**
- **Authority:** Former Goldman VP, 22 years, managed $2.4B (instant credibility)
- **A quit story:** In 2019, over ethics (skin in the game, he can't hide it anymore — a pure motivation)
- **The mechanism:** Dividend Aristocrats, 67 stocks, 25+ consecutive years of INCREASES
- **The performance:** Beat the S&P 78% of the time (30 years of data — proof)
- **Verifiable:** The backtest is public (transparency — you can check it yourself)
- **A conspiracy:** The broker never mentions it, a conflict of commission (cui bono, Doc 3, a Conspiracy)
- **A quantified conflict:** Brokers lose an average of $4,200/client/year (specific, documented)
- **Actionable:** A complete list of 67 stocks, a 3-stock starter (a tangible deliverable)
- **Accessibility:** Works for $5k or $500k (a broad range, not just for the wealthy)
- **Ease:** Rebalance 1x/year, in 10 minutes (low effort — a Shortcut Big Idea, Doc 3)

**10 elements in the headline** (vs. the standard 3-4) = it's comprehensive, and self-contained (you could read the headline alone and get massive value — the Perfect Promo style).

**STEP 3 — THE JOHNSON BOX (Optional — A 100-150 Word Development):**

**What it is:** A box/square at the top of the letter (before the headline, with an ultra-brief summary of the offer).

**Its origin:** Frank Johnson (from direct mail in the 1950s) — he discovered that a box at the top increased opens by +40% (for an envelope, or a page scroll).

**Its function:** It's the first element that is seen (eye-tracking — the gaze goes to the top-center first), and it decides whether they open/read or discard it.

---

**The Johnson Box Structure:**

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ [A TEASER/BENEFIT: 1 compelling line]            │
│                                                  │
│ [THE CORE OFFER: 1-2 lines — what it is]         │
│                                                  │
│ [THE URGENCY: 1 line — a deadline or scarcity]   │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Total:** 40-80 words (ultra-concise, a punch).

**Example:**

```
┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ A SPECIAL INVITATION IS INSIDE                   │
│                                                  │
│ A System to Scale from R$30k → R$100k+ (in 9-12 Months) │
│ Validated by 47 Companies — with a 2x Guarantee or R$5k Back │
│                                                  │
│ Only 27 Spots Available — Enrollment Closes Friday │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**When to use it:** Direct mail (always — it's been tested, and it works), Landing pages (optional — some use it, at the top of the page before the headline, as a summary of the offer), and Email (rarely — the subject line is the equivalent).

**Hodgson's data:** Letters with a Johnson Box get +35-45% more opens (vs. those without — it's significant).
