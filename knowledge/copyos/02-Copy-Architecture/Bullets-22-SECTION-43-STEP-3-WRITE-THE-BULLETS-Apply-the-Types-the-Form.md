## **SECTION 4.3 — STEP 3: WRITE THE BULLETS (Apply the Types + the Formulas)**

---

**The process:**

For EACH benefit (now categorized):

**Choose:**
- **The bullet type** (one of the 10 Bencivenga types — which one fits the benefit? A Straight? A Tease? A Conspiracy?)
- **The fascination formula** (one of the 12 from Makepeace — which one maximizes curiosity? A Blind? A Reverse? An Insider?)

**Write:** The bullet, applying the template for the chosen type/formula.

**Validate:** That the 5 components are present (the benefit, curiosity, specificity, emotion, and proof).

---

**An example of the process:**

**A raw benefit:** "The client gets their first client using the outreach templates"

---

**Choose the type:** A Tease Mechanism (for curiosity — HOW the outreach works)

**Choose the formula:** A Specific Number (a 40-60% response is intriguing)

---

**Write the bullet:**

"• The 3-paragraph email that closes first clients with a 40-60% response rate on cold outreach (vs. 8-12% for long emails — the psychology of timing is on page 92, with the exact templates for each para, tested by 340 copywriters)"

---

**Validate the components:**

✓ The Benefit: Clients get a 40-60% response (a clear outcome)

✓ Curiosity: A 3-paragraph structure (it's mentioned, not explained — a gap), with the psychology of timing (teased on page 92)

✓ Specificity: A 3-paragraph email (a specific structure), 40-60% (quantified), a comparison to 8-12% (for context), page 92 (tangible), 340 (social proof)

✓ Emotional: First clients are a milestone (for confidence and income potential — LF5+LF6)

✓ Proof: Tested by 340 (validation), with psychology (science is implied)

**Score:** 5/5 ✅ (a strong bullet, publish it).

---

**Repeat:** For EACH benefit (50-100) → written into bullets (by applying the various types/formulas).

**The output:** A first draft of 50-100 bullets (rough, not yet edited).

**Time:** 2-4 hours (50-100 bullets × 2-3 min each = 100-300 min).
