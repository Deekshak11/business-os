## **SECTION 5.1 — BULLETS APPROPRIATE FOR EACH STATE**

---

**(FOR EACH State: 20 bullet examples, the characteristics of the type, and the appropriate tone)**

---

### **STATE 1 — MOST AWARE (Transactional, Features/Specs):**

**Appropriate bullets:** Straight Benefit, Naked Benefit, and Proof/Guarantee.

**The tone:** Direct, factual (not an excessive tease — they already know the product and want a confirmation of its features).

**Curiosity:** Low-moderate (no need for extreme fascinations — State 1 is already motivated).

---

**Examples (20):**

1.  "• Unlimited funnels (build as many as you want, with no caps, on all plans)"

2.  "• 200+ templates (pre-designed, industry-tested, and a 1-click deploy)"

3.  "• 40+ integrations (Stripe, PayPal, Mailchimp, Zapier, and APIs — connect everything)"

4.  "• Built-in A/B testing (for headlines, pages, and emails — for automatic data-driven optimization)"

5.  "• An analytics dashboard (real-time, with conversion tracking and funnel visualization — for informed decisions)"

6.  "• 24/7 support (an email response in <2h on average, with live chat, so you're never alone)"

7.  "• Lifetime updates (the platform evolves, you're grandfathered in, and new features are included for free)"

8.  "• Mobile responsive (100% of the designs, with automatic adaptation and zero coding needed)"

9.  "• SSL security (a certificate is included, with a safe checkout and automatic trust badges)"

10. "• A 30-day guarantee (if you're not satisfied, a 100% refund, and you can keep the funnels you built — zero risk)"

11-20. [Continue — for State 1 features/specs, with concrete deliverables, guarantees, support, and technical capabilities — direct, not a tease]

---

**The characteristics:**

- Specificity ✅✅ (exact, quantified features)
- Clarity ✅✅ (zero ambiguity; you know what you get)
- A tease ❌ (low — State 1 doesn't need mystery; it needs confirmation)
- Technical details are OK ✅ (State 1 understands the product, so specs are appropriate)

---

### **STATE 2 — PRODUCT-AWARE (An Emphasis on Differentiation):**

**Bullets:** Direct + unique, a Comparison, a Reverse Hook (to highlight the differences).

**The tone:** Comparative (an implicit vs. others), with clear differentiation.

---

**Examples (20):**

1.  "• The ONLY course with a guarantee of a first sale in 60 days OR a $500 penalty (verified among the top 20 competitors, and none of them offer this — an extreme differentiation)"

2.  "• Hands-on implementation (not just passive videos — an 87% completion rate vs. an industry average of 12%, a difference in accountability)"

3.  "• Why a price of $1,997 (and not the $497 of cheap competitors) gets you better clients (it attracts committed people, not tire-kickers, for a retention rate of 91% vs. 40% for low-price courses)"

4.  "• Templates + revisions (not templates alone without feedback — for iteration until it's good, and accelerated learning)"

5-20. [Differentiation bullets — each one emphasizes a unique element, with an implicit comparison to competitors, why yours is superior, and validation with social proof]

---

### **STATE 3 — SOLUTION-AWARE (Educational, with a Focus on the HOW):**

**Bullets:** A Tease Mechanism, How-To hints, a Reason-Why.

**The tone:** Educational (a preview of the teaching), with teased mechanisms.

---

**Examples (20):**

1.  "• How the 4 components of the Value Equation maximize the perceived value by 5-10x (without changing the product — an optimization of the presentation alone, with a worksheet on page 45)"

2.  "• The 'SPIN Implication' technique that agitates pain with 3x the effectiveness (vs. listing features — an emotional escalation, with a question framework on page 78)"

3.  "• Why 3 INTEGRATED pillars are > isolated tactics (the math: 1×1×1=1 vs. 10×3×5=150x, an exponential synergy, not a linear one, as proved on page 34)"

4.  "• Outreach templates with a 40-60% response rate (for cold emails, LinkedIn DMs, and pitch calls — the psychology is on page 67, with personalization variables)"

5-20. [Educational bullets — each one is a preview of a teaching concept, with a mechanism teased on a page, and the HOW hinted at but not complete, so the reader wants to learn the whole thing]

---

### **STATE 4 — PROBLEM-AWARE (Empathetic, for Hope Creation):**

**Bullets:** A Story Snippet, a Mistake to be Avoided, a Reverse Warning.

**The tone:** Empathetic (it validates their struggles), with hope (others have succeeded).

---

**Examples (20):**

1.  "• Why you're stuck is NOT a lack of effort (you work TOO MUCH — 60-80h, validated; the problem is the wrong SYSTEM, and page 23 has a self-check to identify which pillar is missing)"

2.  "• Maria, 52: 'I had been in pain for 8 years, I had tried everything for $22k, and the 7-second technique on page 47 made me pain-free for 22 months' (the Johns Hopkins protocol, and you can, too)"

3.  "• The mistake 89% of people make: Extreme restriction (a metabolism crash — page 56 explains why the regain is inevitable, and why keto avoids it)"

4.  "• It wasn't your fault that the previous systems failed (they were incomplete — they were theory, not implementation, and page 89 shows the gap that 90% of courses have)"

5-20. [Empathetic bullets — they validate the pain, justify the failures with Warren, offer hope through the success of others, and are protective by showing mistakes to avoid; each is relatable to the suffering of State 4]

---

### **STATE 5 — UNAWARE (Discovery, with Revelation Angles):**

**Bullets:** Insider/Conspiracy, Blind Curiosity, a Story Transformation.

**The tone:** Discovery (a revelation), with extreme curiosity (forbidden info).

---

**Examples (20):**

1.  "• What Wall Street doesn't want you to know about retirement (a conflict of a $4,200/year loss for them, with page 34 exposing the documents and the hidden list of 67 stocks)"

2.  "• Shocking: 89% of [a group] have a [hidden problem] without knowing it (a self-check is on page 12, with 7 symptoms; do you have 3+? It's likely you do)"

3.  "• A 67-year-old doctor who has never been sick: His secret habit #3 (that 89% of the population ignores — a Harvard study of 12k, with page 78 revealing the exact protocol)"

4.  "• From being broke at $3k/month to $45k/month in 22 months — Laura's story is on page 145 (a single mom, who started with zero investment, and a step-by-step to replicate)"

5-20. [Discovery bullets — with revelations, secrets, shocking statistics, and dramatic transformations — for creating awareness in State 5 via curiosity, not a direct problem]
