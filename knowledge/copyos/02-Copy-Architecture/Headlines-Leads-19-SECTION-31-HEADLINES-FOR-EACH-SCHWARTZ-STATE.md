## **SECTION 3.1 — HEADLINES FOR EACH SCHWARTZ STATE**

---

**(FOR EACH State: 10 headline examples, scoring, when to use, appropriate formulas)**

---

### **STATE 1 — MOST AWARE (They Know the Product, They Want It):**

**Appropriate headlines:** A direct offer, an emphasis on the deal, urgency.

**Formulas:** Direct Benefit, Command, Guarantee (transactional).

---

**Examples (10):**

1.  "ClickFunnels 50% OFF the Annual Plan — Today Only, Expires at 11:59 PM ET (Save $1,782)"
    Score: U=8, U=10, U=5, U-S=9 = **32** ✅

2.  "Renew Now and Get 3 Months Free — Exclusive for Members (Expires Friday, a $150 Value)"
    Score: U=8, U=9, U=6, U-S=8 = **31** ✅

3.  "Last Chance: Early Bird for $997 (Regular Price of $2,997 Starts Monday) — 23 Spots Remain"
    Score: U=9, U=10, U=7, U-S=10 = **36** ✅✅

4.  "Flash Sale: Buy 2 Get 1 Free — Ends at Midnight (Limited Stock of 247 Units)"
    Score: U=9, U=10, U=6, U-S=9 = **34** ✅

5-10. [Continue — variations of an offer/deal/urgency for State 1, each scored in the 30-36 range]

---

**Characteristics of State 1 headlines:**
- Short (10-20 words, direct)
- The offer/deal is up front (not education)
- Explicit urgency (a deadline, scarcity)
- The brand/product name (for recognition)

---

### **STATE 2 — PRODUCT-AWARE (They Are Comparing Options):**

**Appropriate headlines:** Differentiation, a USP, a comparison.

**Formulas:** Direct Benefit + differentiation, Reason-Why (why yours), Comparison.

---

**Examples (10):**

1.  "The ONLY Copywriting Course with a Guaranteed First Sale in 60 Days or a Refund + $500 (No Other Course Offers This — Verified Among the Top 20)"
    Score: U=10, U=7, U=10, U-S=10 = **37** ✅✅

2.  "Our Keto Program vs. Others: Ours Gets You into Ketosis in 3 Hours (Not 14 Days of Hell), with an Exclusive MCT Protocol, Tested by 12k with a 78% Success Rate"
    Score: U=9, U=6, U=9, U-S=10 = **34** ✅

3-10. [An emphasis on differentiation for each, a clear USP, implicit/explicit comparisons, scored 32-37]

---

### **STATE 3 — SOLUTION-AWARE (They Are Researching Solutions):**

**Appropriate headlines:** How-To, News, Reason-Why, Number.

**Formulas:** Educational (they teach the HOW), discovery angles.

---

**Examples (10):**

1.  "How to Scale from $30k → $100k in 12 Months Without Hiring 50 People (A 3-Pillar System, Validated by 47 Companies)"
    Score: 35/40 ✅

2.  "Finally: A Way to Get Clients Without Cold Calling or the Hell of Networking (An Outreach System with a 40-60% Response Rate, Templates)"
    Score: 34/40 ✅

3.  "7 Steps to Your First $10k Month in Freelancing (Even with Zero Experience) — Tracked for 340 People, 78% Achieved It in 90 Days"
    Score: 33/40 ✅

4-10. [How-to, news, and number formulas, with an educational tone, scored 30-35]

---

### **STATE 4 — PROBLEM-AWARE (They Feel the Pain, but Don't Know the Solutions):**

**Appropriate headlines:** Question, Story, Reason-Why (why they are stuck).

**Formulas:** Identification, empathetic, hope creation.

---

**Examples (10):**

1.  "Still Stuck at $30k After Trying 3 'Systems'? (It's Not You — the Systems Were Incomplete; an Integrated 3-Pillar Approach Fixes It, and 89% Break the Plateau)"
    Score: 34/40 ✅

2.  "Do You Wake Up 3-4x Every Night Even Though You Sleep for 7 Hours? (It's a Hidden Sleep Debt — a Protocol Resolved It for 12k, for Free)"
    Score: 33/40 ✅

3.  "Why Are You STILL 40 Lbs Overweight After 5 Diets? (It's a Metabolism Crash — Which Keto Avoids, and 78% Maintain for 24 Months)"
    Score: 35/40 ✅

4-10. [Question, story, and reason-why for being stuck/suffering, with an empathetic tone and hints of hope, scored 31-35]

---

### **STATE 5 — UNAWARE (They Don't Know They Have a Problem):**

**Appropriate headlines:** Story, Curiosity, Discovery, Secret.

**Formulas:** Narrative, revelation (not a direct problem — a discovery).

---

**Examples (10):**

1.  "They Called Me Crazy When I Quit My $15k Job to 'Write on the Internet' — Today I'm at $80k/Month Working 20h from Home (Now My Friends Ask Me How)"
    Score: 36/40 ✅✅

2.  "A 67-Year-Old Doctor Who Has Never Been Sick: His 5 Morning Habits That 89% of People Ignore (Validated by Harvard with 12k Participants)"
    Score: 34/40 ✅

3.  "What Your Financial Advisor Doesn't Tell You About Retirement (A $4,200/Year Conflict — a Former VP Reveals It)"
    Score: 35/40 ✅

4-10. [Story, discovery, and secret angles, with curiosity hooks, not a direct problem, scored in the 33-37 range]
