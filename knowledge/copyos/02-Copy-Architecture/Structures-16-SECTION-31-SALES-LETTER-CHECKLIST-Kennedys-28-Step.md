## **SECTION 3.1 — SALES LETTER CHECKLIST (Kennedy's 28-Step)**

```
☐ STEP 1: The Headline (a 4 U's score of 30+)
☐ STEP 2: The Subhead (expands on the promise OR adds proof)
☐ STEP 3-7: The Lead (a hook, identification, a transition — 300-500w)
☐ STEP 8-10: The Problem (agitation, false solutions, holistic)
☐ STEP 11-14: The Solution (the mechanism, differentiation, why it works)
☐ STEP 15-17: The Benefits (15-25 bullets, organized into categories)
☐ STEP 18-20: The Proof (5 tiers — testimonials, data, credentials, guarantee)
☐ STEP 21-24: The Offer (Brunson's stack, value before price, contrast)
☐ STEP 25-26: The FAQ (6-10 objections overcome)
☐ STEP 27-28: The CTA (a specific command, ease, urgency)
☐ ALWAYS: The P.S. (a reminder of the urgency or the guarantee)

TOTAL: 28 items checked = the letter is complete
<25 items: There are gaps (identify and fix them before publishing)
```
