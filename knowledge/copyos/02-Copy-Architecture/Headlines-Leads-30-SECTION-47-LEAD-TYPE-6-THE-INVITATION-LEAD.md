## **SECTION 4.7 — LEAD TYPE #6: THE INVITATION LEAD**

---

**(A condensed development — 2p vs. 5-6p for the others; less common, and webinar-specific)**

---

### **WHEN TO USE IT:**

✅ **For webinars** (live or automated)

✅ **For events** (workshops, masterclasses, challenges)

✅ **For an exclusivity angle** (limited seats, an application is required, for VIPs)

✅ **For community-building** (join a group, belong to a tribe)

---

### **THE STRUCTURE (3 Paragraphs, 200-400w):**

```
P1: AN INVITATION (you are invited to participate in [an exclusive event/group])
P2: WHY YOU WERE INVITED (you are qualified because of [criteria], and what you will gain by attending)
P3: WHAT WILL HAPPEN (a preview of the agenda, a promise of value, a CTA to register)

EXAMPLES: 3 (For a webinar registration, a Mastermind application, and joining a challenge — each 200-350w, with annotated exclusivity elements)
```

---

**The tone:** Exclusive (not for everyone, only for the qualified), an invitation (it's an honor to receive it), value (what you will gain by attending is clear).

**Conversion:** 15-40% for registrations (an invitation framing leads to higher compliance than a transactional "sign up").

---

[**The Invitation Lead is developed in less depth — it's less commonly used, it's specific to webinars/events, and it's 2p vs. 5-6p for the other leads**]
