## **SECTION 7.1 — THE HEADLINE CHECKLIST (15 Items)**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE CHECKLIST — HEADLINE VALIDATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

PRE-CREATION:
☐ 1. The Big Idea is defined (Doc 3 — score 5-6/6)
☐ 2. The State/Stage is known (Doc 2 — diagnosed)
☐ 3. Avatar research is done (their language, pain, and desires — Doc 2)

CREATION:
☐ 4. 10+ variations have been generated (for quantity exploration)
☐ 5. Multiple formulas were used (not just 1 type)
☐ 6. EACH one was scored with the 4 U's (objectively, not by gut)

QUALITY:
☐ 7. The top headlines have 30+ points (Bly's threshold)
☐ 8. The triple function is present (it selects, promises, and captures)
☐ 9. It's focused on one idea (not a confusion of multiple ideas)
☐ 10. A curiosity gap has been engineered (it's incomplete to drive them to continue)
☐ 11. The Big Idea is expressed (the headlines connect to the concept from Doc 3)

VALIDATION:
☐ 12. It's appropriate for the State (the matrix in Part III has been checked)
☐ 13. It's appropriate for the Stage (the sophistication is matched)
☐ 14. A peer review has been done (3-5 people have voted, and the top ones have been shortlisted)
☐ 15. A test is planned (an A/B test of the top 3-5, or a commitment to the best-scored one)

SCORE: __/15

13-15: ✅ Excellent (the headlines are well-engineered)
10-12: ✅ Good (minor gaps are acceptable)
7-9: ⚠️ Needs work (there are significant gaps)
<7: ❌ A problem (redo the process)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
