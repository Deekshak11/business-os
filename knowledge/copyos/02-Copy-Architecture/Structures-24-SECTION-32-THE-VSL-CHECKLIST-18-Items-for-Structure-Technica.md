## **SECTION 3.2 — THE VSL CHECKLIST (18 Items for Structure + Technical)**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE CHECKLIST — THE 45-MIN VSL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE STRUCTURE (The Timing):
☐ 1. A strong hook (from min 00-02 — a pattern interrupt, a promise, a sense of worth, and a roadmap, with a 70%+ retention after min 2)
☐ 2. A diagnosis (from min 02-08 — the 3 pillars/components, a self-check for the viewer, and the gaps are identified)
☐ 3. A generous education (from min 08-25 — each pillar is taught in an actionable way, the viewer could implement it for free, and reciprocity is established)
☐ 4. Extensive proof (from min 25-35 — 3+ video testimonials, aggregated data, a case study, and the guarantee is explained)
☐ 5. Brunson's Stack (from min 35-38 — a progressive value reveal, with an anchor before the price)
☐ 6. The price reveal (from min 38-40 — with suspense, a contrast, and the ROI shown)
☐ 7. A surprise bonus (at min 40, AFTER the price — the timing is critical, and a 30-min timer starts)
☐ 8. An urgency stack (from min 40-42 — 3-4 types, it's multi-layered, and the loss from waiting is quantified)
☐ 9. The CTA appears (at min 35, not before — the button shows up after value has been delivered, and it's sticky after that)
☐ 10. A respectful wrap-up (from min 43-45 — a recap, final urgency, gratitude, and autonomy)

TECHNICAL (The Production):
☐ 11. 100% captions (for the 85% of muted viewings — accessibility is critical)
☐ 12. Visual slides (bullets appear during the benefits/stack, with text overlays, and it's not just a talking head)
☐ 13. Countdown timers (a 30-min bonus timer at min 38, and a deadline timer that's visible after that, to build anxiety)
☐ 14. The CTA button (hidden from min 00-34, appears at 35, is sticky on scroll after that, and it's a contrasting green)
☐ 15. The audio quality (it's clear, with no noise, and a consistent volume — professionalism)
☐ 16. The video quality (a minimum of 1080p, good lighting, a centered framing, and it's professional BUT not an overproduced corporate video)

A VALIDATION OF THE RATIO:
☐ 17. The education is 78%+ (from min 00-35 out of a 45 total — massive value before the pitch)
☐ 18. The pitch is 18-20% (from min 35-43 — it's after the value, and the timing is appropriate)

THE SCORE: __/18

16-18: ✅ Excellent
14-15: ✅ Good
12-13: ⚠️ Acceptable
<12: ❌ A problem (there are structural or technical gaps)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
