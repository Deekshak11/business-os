## **SECTION 3.2 — VSL CHECKLIST**

```
☐ Min 00-02: The Hook (a pattern interrupt, a promise, a roadmap — 80% retention)
☐ Min 02-08: The Diagnosis (the problem is identified, the viewer does a self-check)
☐ Min 08-25: The Education (the mechanisms are taught, it's actionable and generous)
☐ Min 25-35: The Proof (video testimonials, data, the guarantee is explained)
☐ Min 35-38: The Stack (a progressive value reveal, the anchor is established)
☐ Min 38-40: The Price Reveal (suspense is built, a contrast)
☐ Min 40-42: The Surprise Bonus (the timing is after the price)
☐ Min 42-43: The Urgency (a stack of 3-4 types, timers are visible)
☐ Min 43-45: The CTA (appears at minute 35, is sticky, a specific command)
☐ Min 43-45: The Wrap-up (a recap, final urgency, a respectful close)

Technical:
☐ 100% Captions (for muted viewing)
☐ Visual slides (text bullets appear during the benefits/stack)
☐ Countdown timers (a 30-min bonus timer, the main deadline)
☐ The CTA button (hidden from min 0-34, appears at 35, is sticky after that)

TOTAL: 14 items = a complete, functional VSL
```
