## **SECTION B — MEDIUM-FORM (1,000-4,000 WORDS)**

---

### **2.B.1 — THE PERFECT WEBINAR (BRUNSON — 60-90 MIN STRUCTURE)**

---

**Russell Brunson** (Expert Secrets, The Perfect Webinar — a structure tested in 1,000+ webinars, with $100M+ in attributed revenue):

> *"A webinar is a LIVE VSL (or a simulated live one).*
>
> *60-90 minutes is the sweet spot (long enough for deep education, short enough for attention spans).*
>
> *The structure: The Origin Story (10min) + The 3 Secrets (45min of education) + The Stack/Close (a 20min pitch) + a Q&A (15min, optional)."*

---

**WHEN TO USE A WEBINAR:**

✅ **State 3-4** (they need education, and a webinar is the ideal format to teach)

✅ **Price R$1,000-10,000** (high-ticket — justifies the 90 min + live interaction)

✅ **Launch events** (a new product, cohort-based, creating buzz — live = FOMO)

✅ **Authority building** (you're on-camera live = your personality and expertise are demonstrated in real-time, and the Q&A handles objections live)

✅ **High engagement** (live polls, chat interaction, a Q&A — participation > a passive VSL)

---

**THE STRUCTURE (Brunson's Proven Structure — Minute-by-Minute):**

---

**MIN 00-10 — THE ORIGIN STORY (Backstory, Relate, Credibility):**

**Objective:** Establish who you are, why you care, and relatability (Doc 3 Section 7.2.1 — the Backstory element of the Attractive Character).

---

```
Min 00-03: THE INTRODUCTION (Who, What, and Why You're Here)

"Hello! I'm [Your Name]. Thanks for joining (900 registered, 340 live right now — I appreciate you being here).

Over the next 90 minutes, I'm going to teach you [the topic] (specifically [a narrowed subtopic]).

Why should you listen? [Ultra-brief credentials — 30 sec]:
• [Years of experience]
• [A result you've achieved — $ scaled, clients helped, etc.]
• [Authority — media, clients, proof]

My promise: Even if you decide NOT to buy (at the end of the webinar, when I present the offer), you will leave with [value]: [3 actionable frameworks, templates, and strategies you can apply today — a FREE education worth R$2k+ in consulting].

Is that worth 90 minutes of your time? You decide (stay or leave, you're autonomous — no pressure).

Let's go."

[3 min — the intro, quick credibility, a promise of value, permission, a transition]

Min 03-10: THE ORIGIN STORY (Your Journey — To Relate)

"How did I get here (teaching this)?

Before: [A situation similar to the avatar — a relatable struggle]

Example: 'In 2015, I was working at an agency (a junior partner, 70h/week, R$6k/month — overworked, underpaid, frustrated). I wanted to leave (for entrepreneurship), BUT I was afraid (no guarantees, it was a risk).'

[2 min — the before state, relatable to the avatar]

Attempts: [You tried 2-3 things and failed — this validates the audience's attempts]

'I tried a digital product (it failed after 6 months, and I lost R$3k), e-commerce (an inventory nightmare, I quit), and freelance (inconsistent, feast or famine).'

[2 min — the failures, validates Warren's justification, empathy]

The turning point: [The moment of discovery]

'In 2017, I discovered copywriting (a high-demand, low-supply skill, remote, and scalable). I was skeptical, BUT I invested R$1,200 in a course (a last-chance mentality). 4 months later: my first R$2,500 sale (a logo design project for a client). It was a breakthrough (the skill WORKS, a market exists, I CAN do it).'

[1.5 min — the discovery, the skepticism, the breakthrough result]

After: [The transformation]

'Today: R$60k/month on avg (from copywriting + teaching), I work 30h/week, from home (the beach on Tuesdays), and my family is happy. It was a 7-year journey (2017-2024), and now I teach others (to replicate my success, 340+ students, with 78% of them getting their first sale in 60 days, tracked).'

[1.5 min — the transformation is complete, a transition to teaching, social proof]

You can too: 'The same frustration I felt (overworked, underpaid, stuck) — do YOU feel that? I understand (deeply — I lived it). A transformation is possible (I've proved it — me + 340 others). Here's how...'

[A transition to the content — relatability has been established, credibility via results, and hope via proof from others]"
```

**The Origin Story: 10 minutes** (relatability + credibility + hope = the foundation of trust).

**Don't:** Skip straight to the content (it's cold, there's no connection).

**Do:** The story FIRST (to warm them up, relate, and build trust) → then the content AFTER (they're receptive).

---

**MIN 10-55 — THE 3 SECRETS (The Core Education — 45 Min):**

**Objective:** Teach 3 frameworks/secrets (each one is an Epiphany Bridge that breaks 1 False Belief — Doc 11 Section 1.2).

---

**SECRET #1 (Min 10-25 — 15 Minutes) — Breaks the VEHICLE False Belief:**

**Setup the FB:** "You might believe: 'Copywriting only works for native English speakers, or that big companies in Brazil don't pay well' (a vehicle belief — skepticism about the category)."

**The Epiphany Bridge Story:**

Before: "I used to think that (in 2017 — 'Brazil doesn't value copy, it's impossible to make good money')."

The discovery: "My first client (a small business in Curitiba, with R$8k/month in revenue) paid me R$2,500 for a sales letter (I was shocked — a small business, and they paid well). Why? The ROI (the letter generated R$40k in revenue in 3 months, so R$2,500 was a 6% cost, a 16:1 ROI — it was worth it for them). I learned: Businesses WILL pay well if you deliver an ROI (it's not charity, it's an investment)."

After: "Today: Brazilian clients pay me R$5-15k/project (regularly — 8-12 projects/year). The market exists (it's validated — you just need to deliver an ROI, and the price justifies itself)."

**The Secret/Lesson:** "Copywriting works in Brazil (the vehicle works — it's not a myth), IF you position it based on value (delivering an ROI) and not as an hourly commodity. Businesses pay for RESULTS (not hours)."

[15 min — the vehicle FB is broken via a story, it's an actionable lesson, and hope is restored]

---

**SECRET #2 (Min 25-40 — 15 Minutes) — Breaks the INTERNAL False Belief:**

**Setup the FB:** "You think: 'I don't have a talent for writing, I'm not creative enough, copywriting is a natural gift' (an internal belief — self-doubt)."

**The Epiphany Bridge:**

Before: "I thought that (in 2017 — 'I'm not a writer, my Portuguese is bad, I have no creativity')."

The discovery: "My first copy attempt (a sales letter using a fill-in-the-blank template, not a blank page). The result: It worked (the R$2,500 sale generated R$40k for the client — the template WORKED, no genius was required). I learned: Copywriting is a SYSTEM (formulas, templates, frameworks — you follow a recipe, and the results come), it's NOT a gift/talent (a myth — 90% is systematic, 10% is creativity)."

After: "I've taught 340+ people (the majority claimed to be 'not writers,' with average Portuguese and zero creativity — BUT 78% of them made sales with templates/systems). Talent is not a requirement (a system is)."

**The Secret:** "You can do copywriting (you have the internal capability — you don't need a gift), IF you use systems/templates (not an intimidating blank page). Anyone can follow a recipe."

[15 min — the internal FB is broken, self-doubt is dissolved, empowerment]

---

**SECRET #3 (Min 40-55 — 15 Minutes) — Breaks the EXTERNAL False Belief:**

**Setup the FB:** "You think: 'The market is saturated (1,000 copywriters in Brazil), it's impossible to compete, and I can't get clients without a network' (an external belief — the environment is a block)."

**The Epiphany Bridge:**

Before: "I thought that (in 2018 — 1 year into copywriting, afraid of saturation, with zero network on LinkedIn)."

The discovery: "I did cold outreach (with email templates and Instagram DMs — it was systematic, not about my network). I sent 30, got 4 responses, and closed 1 project for R$3,500. I discovered: The demand >>> the supply (businesses NEED copy, and few deliver quality — you're not competing with 1,000, you're competing with the 10-20 who are actually good). A network is unnecessary (outreach works — with templates + persistence)."

After: "I've taught 340 students how to do outreach (most with zero initial network — 78% got their first clients in 60 days with cold outreach, not a warm network). The external barriers can be overcome (strategy > circumstances)."

**The Secret:** "The market isn't saturated with quality (the demand is high, the supply of good copywriters is low), a network isn't necessary (systematic outreach works), and external barriers are myths (strategy removes them)."

[15 min — the external FB is broken, excuses are dissolved, the path is clear]

---

**The 3 Secrets = 45 minutes** (the core education, 3 False Beliefs are broken — applying Doc 2 Part V + Doc 11 Section 1.2).

**The viewer:** Is deeply educated (they can implement pieces for free), their beliefs have shifted (the vehicle works, I can do it, and external barriers can be overcome), and they are receptive to the offer (it's the natural next step — the complete system vs. DIY pieces).

---

**MIN 55-75 — THE STACK (The Offer Presentation — 20 Min):**

**Brunson's sequence** (Doc 4 Part VII):

Min 55-65: The progressive stack (the core + bonuses, with an itemized value for each, totaling R$7,500).

Min 65-68: The price reveal (build suspense, R$2,997).

Min 68-70: The surprise bonus (a 30-min timer).

Min 70-75: The urgency (a deadline, spots available, a price increase), the guarantee (results-based), and the CTA (register now).

[20 min — a pitch after 55 min of value = optimal timing for reciprocity]

---

**MIN 75-90 — THE Q&A (Optional for Live, Skipped if Automated):**

**A live webinar:** A 15-min Q&A (chat questions, answered in real-time, objections handled live).

**Automated:** Skip it (it's not interactive) OR a fake Q&A (pre-recorded "common questions" — less authentic, BUT it addresses objections).

---

**Total: 90 minutes** (60 min if the automated Q&A is skipped).

### **2.B.2 — THE ADVERTORIAL (LADEIRA, NATIVE ADS — 800-1,200 WORDS)**

---

**Advertorial** (Advertisement + Editorial = a hybrid):

It looks like a journalistic article (an editorial format) BUT it's an ad (it sells a product in the end).

**Used for:** Native ads (Taboola, Outbrain, Mgid — the "Recommended Content" widgets on sites).

---

**WHEN TO USE IT:**

✅ **Cold traffic** (State 4-5 — they weren't looking for you, they discovered you via "related articles")

✅ **A 2-step funnel:** Advertorial (hooks, educates briefly, teases) → VSL (the complete pitch)

✅ **Native platforms** (Taboola, Outbrain — an editorial style = a native blend, not an obvious ad)

✅ **A curiosity angle** (a discovery, a secret, a mistake — intriguing headlines, not a direct pitch)

---

**THE STRUCTURE (800-1,200 Words):**

```
SECTION 1 — THE HEADLINE (The Native Ad Creative — 10-15 Words):

The Formula: Curiosity + Authority + Numbers (not a pitch).

Example: "A 67-Year-Old Doctor Who Never Gets Sick: The 5 Morning Habits 89% of People Ignore (Validated by Harvard with 12,000 Participants)"

[A discovery angle — the doctor's authority, curiosity about the 5 habits, social proof that 89% ignore them, Harvard's credibility]

It looks like: A health article (an editorial — Estadão, UOL style).

It is: An ad (it leads to a VSL selling a health protocol).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 2 — THE DATELINE + BYLINE (Editorial Credibility — 20-40 Words):

"São Paulo, November 19, 2024 — By [Your Name, brief credentials] | Source: [A Publication or 'Investigative Report']"

[A journalistic format — a dateline (city, date), a byline (author), and a source (legitimacy) — it looks like a news article]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 3 — THE LEAD PARAGRAPHS (200-300 Words — Editorial Style):

Paras 1-3: A story opening (NOT a pitch — a narrative, a discovery angle).

Example: "Dr. Andrew Chen, 67, a retired cardiologist from Johns Hopkins (a 40-year practice, 15,000+ patients), has never been sick in the last 20 years (zero sick days, zero antibiotics, zero hospitalizations — a record verified by his staff).

His colleagues would ask: 'What's your secret?' (Genetics? Luck?).

Chen: 'No. It's habits. 5 specific ones. In the morning. They're simple. Anyone can do them (it's not genetics, it's not luck — it's systematic).'

In 2019, Chen published his own study (in the Harvard Public Health Journal — peer-reviewed): 12,000 participants (tracked for 8 years), 5 morning habits, the results: 67% were never sick (zero sick days for years), and 89% of the population does NOT do these habits (out of ignorance, not laziness — they don't know).

The discovery: Habits > genetics (health is a choice, not a lottery — Chen proves it)."

[300w — an editorial-style story, the doctor's authority, the discovery of the study, the hook continues — what are the 5 habits?]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 4 — THE BODY (400-700 Words — Educate + Tease):

Paras 4-8: A partial development (valuable education, BUT not complete — an intentional gap).

"Dr. Chen's 5 habits (validated by the 12,000-person study):

HABIT #1: [Revealed — specific, actionable]
Example: '500ml of water immediately upon waking (before coffee, before anything — it rehydrates you from overnight depletion, kickstarts your metabolism, and prepares your digestion). Simple. It takes 90 seconds.'

HABIT #2: [Partially revealed — mentioned, but not in full detail]
'10-15 min of sunlight exposure (to reset your circadian rhythm, regulate cortisol, and for Vitamin D production). A window or outside (face the sun). Timing matters (before 10 AM is ideal).'

HABITS #3-5: [Mentioned that they exist, NOT fully revealed — a gap]
'Chen's study showed that habits 3-5 are CRITICAL (1-2 alone = a 40% effect, all 5 together = 67% are never sick — a synergy). What are they exactly? [Tease] The complete protocol (the 5 habits in detail, the timing for each, the science behind them, and the common mistakes to avoid) is available at [the link below] (a free 40-page report, authorized by Chen, with references to the Harvard study).'

[A gap is created — habits 1-2 are revealed (value is delivered, it's actionable), and 3-5 are teased (a curiosity gap, they must click to learn)]"

[500w — partial education, real value (2 free actionable habits), an intentional gap (the 3 habits are the motivation for the CTA)]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION 5 — THE ADVERTORIAL CTA (100-200 Words):

"How to access the complete protocol? (The 5 habits in detail + the science + an implementation guide)

Click the button below (it takes 60 seconds):

→ A landing page will open (for a free report download — an email is required, you'll opt-in).

→ An instant email (a 40-page PDF, with habits 3-5 revealed, references to the study, and it's actionable).

→ A BONUS: A 15-min video from Dr. Chen (demonstrating the habits, and a Q&A on common mistakes).

[BUTTON: DOWNLOAD THE COMPLETE PROTOCOL FOR FREE]

It's free (zero cost, it's educational — not a pitch yet, pure value).

12,000 have downloaded it (in the last week — social proof, others have accessed it).

Next: After you download it, you'll decide (to implement it for free OR to watch an exclusive video of Chen teaching advanced applications — the choice is yours).

Click now (your curiosity about habits 3-5 will be resolved, the science will be satisfied, and you'll gain the implementation)."

[150w — a lead magnet CTA (not selling a product yet — step 1 of the funnel), ease (60 sec), value (free), social proof (12k), autonomy (you decide), and action]
```

**Total Advertorial: 800-1,000 words.**

It does **NOT sell a product** (not yet — it's step 1 of the funnel).

It **sells:** The click (to download a lead magnet) → The email is captured → A sequence nurtures them → A VSL pitches the product (funnel steps 2-3).

---

**THE COMPLETE FUNNEL (For Native Ads):**

```
STEP 1: The Native Ad (a Taboola creative)
Headline: "A 67-Year-Old Doctor Who Is Never Sick: The 5 Habits 89% of People Ignore"
Image: Dr. Chen (a visual of authority)
↓ A click (a 1-3% CTR from cold traffic)

STEP 2: THE ADVERTORIAL (800-1,000w)
An editorial format, a story, partial education, a tease, a lead magnet CTA
↓ An opt-in (30-50% of the advertorial viewers)

STEP 3: Lead Magnet Delivery + an Email Sequence
An instant PDF, a 5-7 day email sequence (a Soap Opera — Doc 10), nurturing them
↓ Email 5-7: A link to the VSL (the pitch)

STEP 4: A VSL (45-Min)
The full pitch (complete education, the offer, the urgency)
↓ A purchase (3-8% of the VSL viewers in State 3-4)

The total funnel: 1-3% from cold traffic → to a purchase (it's multi-step, with each step being optimized)
```

**The Advertorial = the bridge** (from cold traffic → to a warm lead → to a hot prospect → to a customer).

**Not:** A direct pitch to cold traffic (it fails — State 5 isn't ready to buy, a 0.1% CVR).

**Yes:** Educate → nurture → pitch (a progression from State 5 → 4 → 3 → to a purchase, a 1-3% CVR).
