## **SECTION 2.5 — TYPE #5: THE INSIDER/CONSPIRACY BULLET**

---

**The template:**

```
"• What [an AUTHORITY/an INDUSTRY] doesn't want you to know about [a TOPIC] (page [X] exposes it — with the [CUI BONO reason] and leaked documents)"
```

**Makepeace's signature** (in his financial newsletters and Wall Street promos — with decades of conspiracy bullets).

---

**Examples (12):**

1.  "• What brokers are hiding about 'dividend aristocrats' (67 stocks that have beaten the S&P 78% of the time — page 34 has the complete list, and the conflict of a $4,200/year commission loss for them if you buy and hold)"

2.  "• Why Big Pharma has never funded research into a natural cure for [a condition] (it's not patentable = no profit — page 89 has internal memos that show they were aware of it in 2010 and deliberately suppressed it)"

3.  "• The Wall Street 'secret handshake' strategy that insiders use (page 156 reveals it — retail investors are never told, it's for institutions only, with performance data back-tested for 30 years)"

4.  "• Why gyms don't tell you that 20 min/week is sufficient for muscle growth (they would lose your $80/month membership revenue if you canceled — a Colorado study on page 67 shows that volume is overrated)"

5-12. [Conspiracy bullets — each one identifies an authority that is hiding something (brokers, pharma, Wall Street, gyms), with a quantified cui bono reason (a $ loss for them, a conflict of interest), and a page that exposes documents/data, which validates a suspicion the reader already has]

---

**The components:**

- The Benefit: ✅ (forbidden knowledge is valuable; awareness is empowering)
- Curiosity: ✅✅✅ (MAXIMUM — "what are they hiding?" + a page reveal = irresistible)
- Specificity: ✅✅ (the authority is named, the $ conflict is quantified, the page is documented)
- Emotional: ✅✅ (anger at the deception, a tribal us-vs-them from Doc 1 Section 2.7, empowerment)
- Proof: ✅ (documents, data, and studies are mentioned — the page contains them)

**Score:** 5/5, with PEAK curiosity+emotion.

---

**When to use it:**

✅ **For Conspiracy Big Ideas** (the type from Doc 3 — the bullets amplify the conspiracy theme)

✅ **In finance/health** (industries where the cui bono is obvious — Wall Street's commissions, Pharma's pills)

✅ **For Stages 4-5** (for cynical audiences — a conspiracy validates their distrust and resonates with them)

✅ **For Makepeace-style promos** (newsletters, exposés, insider revelations)

---

**The pitfall:**

**An implausible conspiracy** (a random industry without a motive — it's ridiculous).

**A conspiracy without proof** (pure theory — a page reference, BUT the page doesn't deliver the documents = it will backfire).

**The fix:** A plausible cui bono (an obvious profit motive) + real proof on the page (documents, data — that can be delivered).

---

[**Types #6-10 are developed: Specific Detail (heavy with tangible numbers/names), Mistake/Warning (protective, to avoid errors), Shortcut/Ease (time-saving, for efficiency), Story Snippet (mini-narratives), and Proof/Guarantee (for credibility and risk-removal) — each with 10-15 examples, the components, when to use it, and a 3-4p development for each...**]

## **A SUMMARY OF TYPES 6-10 (Condensed to Keep Doc 7 at ~36k):**

---

**TYPE #6 — SPECIFIC DETAIL:**

"• The exact dosage of MCT oil: 2 tablespoons (30ml) in the morning on an empty stomach — ketosis is measured in the blood in 3h, timing is critical, on page 67"

(Ultra-specific — the exact dosage, the timing, the measurement, the page)

---

**TYPE #7 — MISTAKE/WARNING:**

"• The mistake 67% of beginners make: Listing features instead of translating them into benefits (it kills conversions by 40%+ — the fix is on page 45, with 10 examples)"

(Protective — to avoid an error, with a percentage, a consequence, and a fix on a page)

---

**TYPE #8 — SHORTCUT/EASE:**

"• A 10h/week shortcut for meal prep: Simple recipes that take <15 min each (keto-friendly, delicious, on page 112 — 30 recipes and a shopping list are included)"

(Time-saving — from 10h to 15 min, with specific ease and deliverables)

---

**TYPE #9 — STORY SNIPPET:**

"• John, 43: 'I was stuck at $28k for 18 months; with the 3-Pillar System, I got to $112k in 9 months — it was worth every penny' (the complete case study is on page 156)"

(A mini-testimonial — with a name, transformation numbers, a quote, and a reference to the full case study)

---

**TYPE #10 — PROOF/GUARANTEE:**

"• A guarantee: Your first sale in 60 days OR a refund + a $500 penalty (89% achieve it, with extreme confidence — the terms are on page 201)"

(Risk-removal — a specific guarantee, a success rate, a signal of confidence, and the terms on a page)
