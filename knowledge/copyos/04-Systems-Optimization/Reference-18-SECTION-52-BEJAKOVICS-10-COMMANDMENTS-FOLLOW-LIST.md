## **SECTION 5.2 — BEJAKOVIC'S 10 COMMANDMENTS (FOLLOW LIST)**

---

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE 10 COMMANDMENTS OF A-LIST COPYWRITERS (BEJAKOVIC)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ COMMANDMENT #1: Know Thy Customer Better Than They Know Themselves

80% Research, 20% Writing (Bencivenga's ratio — most of the time is spent on research, not writing; deep client knowledge means the copy almost writes itself, shallow knowledge means a struggle with every sentence).

Application: Complete Doc 2 (72p of avatar research — interviews, surveys, mining reviews, identifying exact pain points/desires/objections/language).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #2: Write to ONE Person (A Specific Avatar, Not "Everyone")

Select your audience (the #1 function of a headline in Doc 6 Section 1.1 — qualifying "To people who...").

Application: The Campaign Brief in Doc 2 (a singular avatar is defined — Robert, a 43-year-old entrepreneur, NOT a generic "all entrepreneurs").

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #3: Sell the Sizzle, Not the Steak (The Result, Not the Product)

Wheeler's "Sizzle Sells" — the customer buys the RESULT (a transformed identity, a different life), NOT the product (the features and specs are boring).

Application: The Drill from Doc 3 (feature → identity Level 5, the copy focuses on the aspirational/identity levels 4-5).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #4: Make It Believable (Claim Size ∝ Proof Size)

Doc 8 Section 3 (Bejakovic's Believability Formula — a bigger claim requires exponentially more proof).

Application: A bold claim like "cure diabetes in 30 days" requires MASSIVE proof (peer-reviewed clinical trials, FDA approval, 100+ testimonials, an extreme guarantee), OR reduce the claim to "manage blood sugar in 90 days," which requires moderate proof (5 testimonials, 1 study, a 60-day guarantee).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #5: Tell a Compelling Story (Facts Tell, Stories SELL)

Heath from Doc 1 (Stories are 22x more memorable than facts — the Availability Heuristic, a narrative is remembered, stats are forgotten).

Application: Story Leads from Doc 6 (Section 4.5 — 600-1,200-word stories with a protagonist that mirrors the avatar), the Epiphany Bridge from Doc 11 (a compelling transformation arc).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #6: The Power of Specificity (8,347 NOT ~8,000)

Hopkins/Schwartz/Kahneman are unanimous — specifics are credible (a System 1 heuristic, what's measured implies it's real, not guessed).

Application: Every number in the copy (revenues, users, percentages, timeframes) = a specific odd number (8,347, 89%, 9.2 months, $2,997 — NOT round numbers like 8,000, 90%, 9 months, $3,000).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #7: Create Curiosity Gaps (Tease, Don't Tell — in Bullets)

Schwartz's Fascinations (Doc 7 — reveal enough to intrigue, conceal enough to create gaps that drive the purchase to discover).

Application: 15-25 bullets (each with a gap — "the technique on page 47," "the 7-letter word," "the 3 fatal errors" — 15-25 accumulated gaps = unbearable curiosity, buying is the only relief).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #8: Write With Passion (Enthusiasm Is Contagious — Carlton)

An authentic voice from Doc 3 (a visible personality, genuine emotion, NOT bland corporate robotics).

Application: Inject personality (personal stories, strong opinions with polarity, vulnerability with admitted flaws — human, not a machine, for emotional engagement with readers).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #9: Test, Measure, Optimize (The Market Decides, NOT You)

Hopkins' Scientific Advertising (all of Doc 12 — testing hierarchy, one-variable, 5x iteration, data-driven humility).

Application: Every campaign = test a baseline, A/B test variations, implement the winner, and continuously iterate (never assume, always validate market preference).

---

✅ COMMANDMENT #10: Never Stop Learning (Be a Perpetual Student — Read, Handwrite Winners)

Halbert's practice: Handwrite winning sales letters (for deep absorption, internalizing patterns subconsciously, skill development through osmosis).

Application: Monthly, read 2-3 winning promos (Agora, Empiricus newsletters, successful launches — analyze what works, handwrite 1 completely for absorption), annually, re-read Doc 1-13 of the SO (to refresh principles, new depths are noticed on re-reading — perpetual layers of learning).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

AFTER WRITING YOUR COPY: Go through the 10 Commandments checklist (how many did you follow? 8-10/10 = on the path to A-list, <7/10 = gaps to address and improve).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
