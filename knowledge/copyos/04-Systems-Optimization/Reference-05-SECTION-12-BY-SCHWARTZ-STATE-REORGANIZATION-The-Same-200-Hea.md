## **SECTION 1.2 — BY SCHWARTZ STATE (REORGANIZATION — The Same 200 Headlines)**

---

**(The same 200 headlines from Section 1.1, REORGANIZED by State — a different view of the same content, choose the organization that's most useful)**

---

**STATE 1 (Most Aware) — 30 Headlines:**

Offer-focused, urgency, emphasis on the deal (transactional).

Examples:
- "50% OFF Annual Plan — Expires Friday at 11:59 PM"
- "Last Chance for Early Bird Price of $997 (Goes to $2,997 on Monday)"
- "Flash Sale: Buy 2 Get 1 Free — Ends at Midnight"
[27 more State 1 — transactional, urgency, deadline, scarcity, pricing]

---

**STATE 2 (Product-Aware) — 50 Headlines:**

Differentiation, USP, comparison (choosing between multiple products).

Examples:
- "The ONLY Course With a Guaranteed First Sale in 60 Days or Your Money Back + $500"
- "Why This CRM Beats Salesforce for Small Businesses (1/10th the Price, 2x Easier to Use)"
- "Our Keto Program vs. Others: Ours Gets You into Ketosis in 3 Hours (Not 14 Days of Hell)"
[47 more State 2 — differentiation, USP, unique, only, comparison]

---

**STATE 3 (Solution-Aware) — 60 Headlines:**

Educational, how-to, news, mechanism reveal (researching solutions).

Examples:
- "How to Scale from $30k → $100k in 12 Months Without Hiring a Team"
- "Finally: A Way to Get Clients Without Cold Calling or Networking"
- "The Science Behind Why Fat Doesn't Make You Fat (The Insulin Mechanism)"
[57 more State 3 — how-to, news, educational, mechanism, solutions]

---

**STATE 4 (Problem-Aware) — 40 Headlines:**

Empathetic questions, stories of struggle, reasons why they're stuck (they feel the pain but don't know the solutions).

Examples:
- "Still Stuck at $30k After Trying 3 'Systems'? (It's Not You — The Systems Were Incomplete)"
- "Why Are You STILL 40 Lbs Overweight After 5 Diets? (The Metabolism Crash Secret)"
- "Do You Wake Up 3-4x Every Night Even After 7 Hours of Sleep? (The Sleep Debt Protocol)"
[37 more State 4 — empathetic, validation, struggling, question, story]

---

**STATE 5 (Unaware) — 20 Headlines:**

Transformation story, discovery of secrets, extreme curiosity (they don't know they have a problem).

Examples:
- "They Laughed When I Said I'd Make $50k/Month Writing — But Then I Showed Them My Bank Statement..."
- "This 67-Year-Old Doctor Never Gets Sick: His 5 Morning Habits (Validated by Harvard with 12,000 People)"
- "What Your Financial Advisor Isn't Telling You About Fees (It's Costing You $127k Over 30 Years)"
[17 more State 5 — story, discovery, secret, unaware, awareness creation]

---

**Total:** 200 headlines (30+50+60+40+20), dual-organized by type AND State (for navigation flexibility).
