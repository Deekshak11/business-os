**Pattern for Type #1:**

**Pattern for Type #2:**

✅ **NEVER (all caps)** in 20/20 (emphasizes the warning, Hodgson validated that selective capitalization boosts attention by 15-25%)

✅ **Conditional "if you want"** in 20/20 (the goal is stated — provides context, allows for self-selection)

✅ **Proximity mention** in 16/20 (80% — "in 89% of kitchens," "67% of reps make," "appears in 78% of headlines" — you probably have/do this, which aids identification)

✅ **Consequence OR percentage** in 18/20 (90% — severity "irreversible damage," frequency "78% make" — urgency to avoid)

✅ **Page reference** in 20/20 (100% — "page X" proves the content exists and reveals what to do instead)

**Appeal:** Protective (LF3 fear-free — avoid a mistake, prevent a loss), mistake correction (helps to justify past failures — it wasn't your fault, you were doing X wrong, now you can fix it).
