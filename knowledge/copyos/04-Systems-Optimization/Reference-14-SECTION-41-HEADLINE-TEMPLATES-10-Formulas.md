## **SECTION 4.1 — HEADLINE TEMPLATES (10 Formulas)**

---

**TEMPLATE #1 — HOW-TO:**

```
"How to [BENEFIT] in [TIMEFRAME] Without [OBSTACLE]"

FILL IN:
• Benefit: _________________________
• Timeframe: _________________________
• Obstacle: _________________________

RESULT:
"How to _____________ in _____________ Without _____________"

EXAMPLE FILLED:
"How to Get Your First Copywriting Client in 60 Days Without a Portfolio or Network"
```

---

**TEMPLATE #2 — QUESTION:**

```
"Do You [NEGATIVE ACTION/STATE] [CONTEXT]?"

FILL IN:
• Negative action: make mistakes, feel stuck, lose money, waste time
• Context: in [topic], with [situation], about [problem]

RESULT:
"Do You _____________ _____________?"

EXAMPLE:
"Do You Make These 7 Mistakes in Email Marketing?"
```
