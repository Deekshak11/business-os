## **SECTION 7.2 — THE ADAPTATION WORKFLOW (THE CORRECT 4-STEP PROCESS)**

---

**STEP 1 — FIND A SIMILAR SWIPE (Relevance):**

**Similarity criteria:**
- Same niche (a health swipe → a health product, a business swipe → a business product)
- Same State (a Solution-Aware swipe → YOUR Solution-Aware avatar)
- Same format (a headline swipe → a headline need, a bullet → a bullet)

**Process:** Browse Parts I-II (headlines, bullets), use Ctrl+F for keywords (niche, State, type), select 3-5 swipes that resonate.

---

**STEP 2 — EXTRACT THE TEMPLATE (The Structure, NOT the Content):**

**Swipe analysis:**

**Swipe:** "How a Shy Accountant Made $10k in 30 Days Using a 3-Step Method (Even With Zero Experience)"

**Identify the structure:**
- Formula: "How a [Unlikely Person] Made [$ Amount] in [Time] Using a [Method] (Even With [Limitation])"

**Extract the template:**
```
"How a [___________] Made [___________] in [___________] Using a [___________] (Even With [___________])"
```

**Components:**
- Unlikely Person (a relatable underdog for YOUR avatar)
- $ Amount (a realistic outcome for YOUR product)
- Time (an achievable timeline for YOUR results)
- Method (the unique mechanism of YOUR product)
- Limitation (an obstacle YOUR avatar has, removed)

---

**STEP 3 — FILL IN YOUR SPECIFICS (Total Personalization):**

**YOUR Product:** A copywriting course

**YOUR Avatar:** A busy parent (NOT a shy accountant — different)

**Fill in the template:**

- Unlikely Person: "Busy Parent" (2 kids, a full-time job — relatable for YOUR avatar)
- $ Amount: "$15,000/Month" (YOUR realistic outcome, localized with a currency if needed)
- Time: "90 Days" (YOUR timeline — 30 days from the swipe is too fast for YOUR product, 90 is realistic)
- Method: "Template System" (YOUR mechanism — not a generic "3-step," templates are a specific differentiator)
- Limitation: "Even With Only 2 Hours/Day Available" (YOUR avatar's obstacle — a busy parent's time scarcity, NOT the accountant's "zero experience")

**Adapted result:**

"How a Busy Parent Made $15,000/Month in 90 Days Using a Template System (Even With Only 2 Hours/Day Available)"

---

**STEP 4 — VALIDATE THE ADAPTATION (Quality Check):**

**Score with the 4 U's:**
- Useful: 9/10 (a specific outcome of $15k/month is clear)
- Urgent: 6/10 (a 90-day timeframe, not an explicit deadline — could be boosted by adding "before the market gets saturated")
- Unique: 8/10 (a busy parent + 2h/day + a template system = a unique combination, not the overused "shy accountant")
- Ultra-Specific: 10/10 ($15k, 90 days, 2h/day — abundant numbers)
- **TOTAL: 33/40** ✅ (a strong adapted headline, usable)

**Possible improvements:**
- Add urgency ("...Before the Competition Discovers This Method" — a soft deadline)
- Test variations (change "Busy Parent" → "Single Mom," "2h/day" → "Only on Lunch Breaks" — test which different avatars resonate)

---

**Compare the original vs. the adaptation:**

**Swipe:** Shy Accountant, $10k, 30 days, 3-step, zero experience

**Yours:** Busy Parent, $15k/month, 90 days, Template System, 2h/day

**Content overlap:** ZERO (100% different words) — structure is the SAME (the formula), ethical ✅, legal ✅, effective ✅ (customized for YOUR context).
