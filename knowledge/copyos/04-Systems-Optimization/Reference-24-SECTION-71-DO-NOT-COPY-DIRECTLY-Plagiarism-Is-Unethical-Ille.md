## **SECTION 7.1 — DO NOT COPY DIRECTLY (Plagiarism Is Unethical, Illegal, and Ineffective)**

---

**Why NOT to copy word-for-word:**

**LEGAL:** Copyright (headlines/copy are protected in some cases, famous swipes are owned, copying = risk of an infringement lawsuit).

**ETHICAL:** Plagiarism (intellectual property theft, professionally unethical).

**PRACTICAL:** It doesn't work (a swipe is for a different product/avatar/market, an exact copy = a mismatch for YOUR context, lower conversion than an adaptation).

---

**Example of plagiarism:**

**Swipe:** "How a Shy Accountant Made $10k in 30 Days Using a 3-Step Method (Even With Zero Experience)"

**Your copy (WRONG):** "How a Shy Accountant Made $10k in 30 Days Using a 3-Step Method (Even With Zero Experience)" (identical — plagiarism, not YOUR product, ridiculous to use "shy accountant" if YOUR avatar is a busy parent, a disconnect).
