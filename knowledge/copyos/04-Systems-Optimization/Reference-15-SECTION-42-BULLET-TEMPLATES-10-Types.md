## **SECTION 4.2 — BULLET TEMPLATES (10 Types)**

---

**TEMPLATE TYPE #1 — SPECIFIC MECHANISM:**

```
"• The [CATEGORY] with [NUMBER]-[UNIT] that [RESULT] ([SOCIAL PROOF], [QUANTIFIED IMPACT] — on page [X])"

FILL IN:
• Category: word, technique, question, food, habit, system
• Number-unit: 7-letter, 3-second, 5-word, 9-step
• Result: a dramatic benefit (open wallets, eliminate pain, close sales)
• Social proof: A-listers use, 8,000 have tested, Harvard doctors
• Impact: 40%+ conversion, 15 points lower, 3x faster
• Page: 23, 47, 89, 134, 178

EXAMPLE:
"• The 9-second technique that eliminates procrastination (tested by 2,400 people, 76% reported an immediate increase in action — on page 156)"
```

## **SECTIONS 4.3-4.8 — ADDITIONAL TEMPLATES (CTA, Guarantee, Stack, Email, Openings, Closings):**

---

**4.3 — CTA TEMPLATE:**

```
"[COMMAND VERB] [SPECIFIC ACTION] [EASE FACTOR] [BENEFIT REMINDER] [URGENCY]"

EXAMPLE:
"Click the button below (it takes 60 seconds) to guarantee your instant access before the offer expires at midnight"
```

---

**4.4 — GUARANTEE TEMPLATE (5 Levels from Doc 4):**

```
LEVEL 4 — RESULTS-BASED:

"[TIMEFRAME] Guarantee: If you [APPLY THE CONDITION] and DON'T achieve [SPECIFIC MINIMUM RESULT], we will refund [REFUND AMOUNT] + you get to keep [BONUSES] (a [TOTAL VALUE] total value). No questions asked."

FILL IN:
• Timeframe: 60 days, 90 days, 12 months
• Condition: complete 100% of the modules, apply the techniques, attend 4+ calls
• Minimum result: your first sale, lose 15 lbs, 2x your revenue
• Refund: 100%, the total amount invested
• Bonuses to keep: templates, recordings, group access
• Total value: calculate bonuses + refund

EXAMPLE:
"90-Day Guarantee: If you complete 100% of the program and DON'T make your first sale of at least $500, we will refund your $1,997 + you get to keep all the templates and recordings (a $3,200 total value). No questions asked."
```
