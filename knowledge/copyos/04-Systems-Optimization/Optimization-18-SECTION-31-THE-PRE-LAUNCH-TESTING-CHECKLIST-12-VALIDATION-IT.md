## **SECTION 3.1 — THE PRE-LAUNCH TESTING CHECKLIST (12 VALIDATION ITEMS)**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE CHECKLIST — PRE-LAUNCH (Before You Spend $1 on Ads)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

THE OFFER'S VALIDATION:
☐ 1. Has Joyner's 10-Person Test been performed? (8-10/10 said YES = the offer is irresistible, <8 = iterate the offer before you launch)

☐ 2. Has the Value Equation been scored? (the Dream Outcome, the Perceived Likelihood, the Time Delay, and the Effort — each from 1-10, with an overall average of 5+ for the components)

☐ 3. Has the ROI been calculated? (a minimum Perceived Value ÷ Price of ≥ 3:1, with an ideal of 10:1+)

☐ 4. Is the Guarantee a Level 3+? (a minimum of a Better-than-money-back, with a results-based Level 4 being ideal)

☐ 5. Are the Urgencies REAL? (an enforceable deadline, a genuine limit for the scarcity, a price increase that will happen — zero fakes, and it must be Procon-proof for Brazil's compliance)

THE COPY'S VALIDATION:
☐ 6. Have the headlines been tested? (10+ have been generated, they've been scored with the 4 U's, the top 3-5 have been selected with 30+ points, and they've been voted on in a peer review)

☐ 7. Is the lead type appropriate for the State? (the decision tree from Doc 6 — a State 1 Offer, a State 2 Promise, a State 3 Problem-Solution, a State 4 Story, and a State 5 Secret — has it been matched correctly)

☐ 8. Is there a 4-5 tier proof? (Testimonials, Data, a Case Study, Credentials, and a Guarantee — stacked, NOT just a single tier)

☐ 9. Have the top 5 objections been addressed? (in an FAQ section OR pre-empted in the body — at a minimum, price, time, trust, fit, and timing must be covered)

THE FUNNEL'S VALIDATION:
☐ 10. Have the baseline metrics been established? (if it's an existing funnel, you must know the current CVR for each step — for a baseline comparison so that the improvement can be measured)

☐ 11. Has the tracking been set up? (with UTMs, Facebook/Google pixels, email platform analytics, and promo codes — for a clear attribution of the source of each customer)

☐ 12. Has the LTV:CAC been projected? (an estimate of the LTV based on the offer's pricing + the planned upsells, and an estimate of the CAC based on the channel's CPC benchmarks — a projected ratio of ≥3:1 is viable before the launch, <3:1 is a problem that needs the offer to be fixed before the ads)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE SCORE: __/12

12/12: ✅✅ Ready to launch (high confidence, minimized risk, thoroughly validated)
10-11/12: ✅ An acceptable launch (minor gaps, to be closely monitored)
8-9/12: ⚠️ Hesitate (significant gaps, to be addressed before a large budget spend)
<8/12: ❌ NOT ready (critical gaps to be fixed before the launch — to prevent waste)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
