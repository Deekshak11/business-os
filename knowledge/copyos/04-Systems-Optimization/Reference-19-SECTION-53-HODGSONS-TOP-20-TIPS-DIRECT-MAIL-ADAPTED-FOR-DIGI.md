## **SECTION 5.3 — HODGSON'S TOP 20 TIPS (DIRECT MAIL ADAPTED FOR DIGITAL)**

---

```
1. ✅ Johnson Box (at the top of the page — a 50-word offer summary, catches the eye first)
2. ✅ Red/bold headline (color emphasis, a 15-25% attention boost)
3. ✅ ALWAYS use a P.S. (90% read it even if they skip the body — critical real estate)
4. ✅ Subheads every 200-300 words (to guide skimmers, for a scannable structure)
5. ✅ Indented paragraphs (breathing room, NOT an intimidating wall of text)
6. ✅ Blue signature (digitally = an image of a signature in blue ink, simulates a personal, handwritten touch)
7. ✅ Bullets (break up the text, stand out visually, for quick, scannable benefits)
8. ✅ Interspersed testimonials (don't clump them into a single section — spread them throughout)
9. ✅ A visual guarantee box (with a background color, a border, make it stand out — not buried in the text)
10. ✅ Multiple CTAs (after the benefits, after the offer, after the FAQ, before the P.S. — 3-5 opportunities)
11. ✅ Generous white space (wide margins, line-height of 1.5-1.8, NOT cramped — for readability)
12. ✅ Serif font for the body (Times, Georgia — for long-form readability), sans-serif for headlines (Arial, Helvetica — for impact)
13. ✅ High contrast (black text, white OR cream background — zero eye strain, accessibility)
14. ✅ Images to break up the text (every 400-600 words, a visual rest, maintains engagement)
15. ✅ Visual urgency (a countdown timer, "X Remaining" badges, the deadline in bold red — can't be missed)
16. ✅ Social proof logos (at the top of the page — "As Seen In" Forbes/WSJ/etc., for immediate credibility)
17. ✅ Mobile-first (60-70% of traffic is mobile — test the mobile view BEFORE the desktop, 100% responsive)
18. ✅ Load speed <3 sec (critical for fast loading — every second of delay is +7% bounce, optimize images, clean code)
19. ✅ Anchor links (a table of contents at the top of long pages — to jump to sections, for ease of navigation)
20. ✅ Sticky CTA (a button that follows the scroll after a certain point — always visible, zero friction to click)

```
