## **SECTION 6.2 — DR TOUCHSTONES (20 Info Products — Joyner Style)**

---

**TOUCHSTONE #1:**

```
"Your First Sale in 60 Days or Your Money Back + $500" — Copywriting Course

ANALYSIS:

FORMULA (Joyner, Doc 4 Section 5):
[SPECIFIC BENEFIT] + [TIMEFRAME] + [RISK-NEGATIVE GUARANTEE]

BREAKDOWN:
• Benefit: "First sale" (a concrete outcome, a significant milestone for beginners)
• Quantified: Implicitly a minimum of $500 (the refund + penalty suggests the sale is worth it)
• Timeframe: "60 days" (specific, achievable but not overnight, realistic and credible)
• Guarantee: "Money back + $500" (better-than — you gain even if you fail, negative risk, a signal of confidence)

MEMORABILITY TEST:
• Repeatable? YES (a person hears it once and can repeat it — "first sale in 60 days or money back plus $500")
• Specific? YES (60 days, $500 — numbers stick)
• Clear benefit? YES (first sale = income starts, career launches)

EMOTIONAL APPEAL:
• LF5 (money — income from the first sale)
• LF3 (fear-free — the guarantee completely removes the risk)
• LF6 (superiority — be a copywriter, not an employee)

WHY IT WORKS:
• Tangible outcome ("first sale" is measurable, NOT a vague "learn copywriting")
• Realistic timeline (60 days is achievable, not an absurd 7 days)
• Negative risk (you gain $500 even if you fail — the logic to try it is irresistible, the downside = an upside)
• A signal of confidence (you only offer a $500 penalty if you KNOW you can deliver — it builds trust)

USAGE IN COPY:
• Headline (mention the guarantee upfront for differentiation)
• Subhead (expand on the promise)
• Bullets ("Guaranteed first sale in 60d..." — a proof bullet)
• Offer section (with detailed terms)
• CTA ("The guarantee protects you..." — reassurance)
• P.S. (a final reminder)

```
