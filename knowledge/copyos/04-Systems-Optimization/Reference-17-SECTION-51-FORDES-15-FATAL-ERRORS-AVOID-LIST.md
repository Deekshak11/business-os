## **SECTION 5.1 — FORDE'S 15 FATAL ERRORS (AVOID LIST)**

---

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE 15 MOST FATAL ERRORS IN COPYWRITING (FORDE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

❌ ERROR #1: Not Knowing the Customer (Missing Avatar Research)
FIX: 3-5 hours of research in Doc 2 (interviews, review mining, surveys)
CONSEQUENCE: Generic copy (doesn't resonate, conversion of 1-2% vs. 5-15% for researched copy)

❌ ERROR #2: A Generic Headline (Vague, Not Scored)
FIX: Generate 10+ in Doc 6, score with the 4 U's, aim for 30+
CONSEQUENCE: 80% of readers are lost (a failed headline = the body is never read)

❌ ERROR #3: Burying the Lede (No value in the first 100 words)
FIX: An immediate hook with the lead types in Doc 6, value/benefit in the first 2 paragraphs
CONSEQUENCE: Bounce rate of 60-80% (zero patience, they'll exit if not hooked fast)

❌ ERROR #4: Listing Features (Not Translating Them into Benefits)
FIX: The Drill method from Doc 3 (feature → functional → emotional → aspirational → identity)
CONSEQUENCE: A "so what?" reaction from the reader, zero desire for features alone

❌ ERROR #5: No Big Idea (Missing Angle, Generic Positioning)
FIX: Create a Big Idea with Doc 3 (5 types, score 5-6/6 on the checklist)
CONSEQUENCE: Invisibility (blending in with competitors, no differentiation, ignored)

❌ ERROR #6: Insufficient Proof (Claims Without Backing)
FIX: The 5-tier stack from Doc 8 (testimonials + data + case studies + credentials + guarantee)
CONSEQUENCE: Skepticism wins (no trust, no purchase, conversion <2%)

❌ ERROR #7: No Urgency (Enabling Procrastination)
FIX: The stack of 3-4 urgency types from Doc 4 Part VI (deadline, scarcity, price, opportunity cost)
CONSEQUENCE: "I'll buy later" = never (infinite procrastination, conversion is delayed indefinitely)

❌ ERROR #8: A Weak CTA (Vague, Difficult, No Benefit)
FIX: A specific command + ease + benefit + urgency (from the CTA section blueprints in Doc 5)
CONSEQUENCE: A confused reader (what do I do next? High friction, they exit)

❌ ERROR #9: Forgetting the P.S. (Losing an 80% Readership Opportunity)
FIX: Always include a P.S. (urgency recap, guarantee reminder, testimonial quote)
CONSEQUENCE: 80% read the P.S. (Hodgson — they skip the body, read the P.S., a wasted opportunity not to use it)

❌ ERROR #10: Copy Is Too Short (When It Should Be Long)
FIX: Complex products, high-ticket items, cold traffic = long copy of 6-12k words (Doc 5 — education and justification are needed)
CONSEQUENCE: Unaddressed objections (the prospect hesitates, doesn't buy due to insufficient info)

❌ ERROR #11: Copy Is Too Long (When It Should Be Short)
FIX: Hot traffic, simple products, low-ticket items = short copy of 300-800 words (Evaldo's minimalism)
CONSEQUENCE: Impatience (known customers are irritated by lengthiness, TL;DR, they exit)

❌ ERROR #12: Ignoring Objections (The Top 5 Aren't Addressed)
FIX: The FAQ in Doc 11 (the top 5-10 objections are answered), identify them in the avatar research from Doc 2
CONSEQUENCE: Silent objections (the reader mentally hesitates, doesn't vocalize it, and exits; you never know why you lost the sale)

❌ ERROR #13: A Weak Guarantee (A basic, generic 30-day)
FIX: Level 3+ from Doc 4 (better-than, results-based, penalty)
CONSEQUENCE: High perceived risk (a weak guarantee signals low confidence in the product, conversion is -30-50% vs. a strong guarantee)

❌ ERROR #14: Not Testing (Assuming the First Headline/Offer Is the Best)
FIX: The testing in Doc 12 (10+ headlines, 2-3 offers, systematic A/B testing)
CONSEQUENCE: Scaling the wrong version (wasting $10-50k in ads on a weak headline that converts at 1% when an untested alternative converts at 5% — a massive opportunity cost)

❌ ERROR #15: Giving Up Too Soon (Quitting after the 1st iteration fails)
FIX: The commitment to 5 iterations from Doc 12 Section 1.6 (headline, lede, offer, idea, market)
CONSEQUENCE: Premature failure (90% quit at iteration 1-2, success often comes at iteration 3-5, persistence is required, data-driven not ego-driven)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE PUBLISHING YOUR COPY: Audit these 15 errors (if you've made zero, launch with confidence. 3+ errors? Fix them before spending your ad budget).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
```
