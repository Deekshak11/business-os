## **SECTION 3.1 — TOP 150 (10 Categories, 15 Each)**

---

**(Curated from Bly's 2,000+ Big Book of Words + Morgan's 500 Emotional Triggers — selected the TOP 150 highest-impact words for a copywriting context, organized into functional categories for quick reference)**

---

### **CATEGORY #1 — URGENCY (15 Words):**

1.  **NOW** (maximum immediacy, a System 1 trigger for instant action)
2.  **TODAY** (a temporal deadline, a specific day)
3.  **IMMEDIATELY** (emphasizes zero delay)
4.  **LAST** (finality, temporal scarcity)
5.  **EXPIRES** (approaching deadline, imminent loss)
6.  **REMAINING** (quantity scarcity, a countdown)
7.  **URGENT** (explicit priority)
8.  **FAST** (emphasis on speed)
9.  **LIMITED** (general scarcity)
10. **DEADLINE** (a standard term in the context of urgency)
11. **FINAL** (the end, the last opportunity)
12. **ENDS** (termination)
13. **WHILE** (a limited temporal window)
14. **BEFORE** (timing is critical for action pre-event)
15. **NEVER AGAIN** (extreme finality, the last chance ever)

---

### **CATEGORY #2 — CREDIBILITY (15 Words):**

1.  **PROVEN** (testing is implied, validation)
2.  **GUARANTEED** (certainty, risk removed)
3.  **VERIFIED** (checked by a third party)
4.  **TESTED** (validation through experimentation)
5.  **SCIENTIFIC** (research-backed authority)
6.  **VALIDATED** (external confirmation)
7.  **CERTIFIED** (official authority)
8.  **DOCUMENTED** (records exist)
9.  **TRACKED** (data collected systematically)
10. **RESEARCH** (study-based)
11. **STUDY** (a scientific investigation)
12. **DATA** (numbers and facts)
13. **RESULTS** (tangible outcomes)
14. **EVIDENCE** (proof)
15. **AUTHENTIC** (genuine, not fake)

---
